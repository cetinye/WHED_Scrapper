History
Founded 1939 as Sichuan Provincial Experimental School of Drama. Successively became Sichuan Provincial Experimental School of Music, Sichuan Provincial Vocational College of Art Skills, Sichuan Provincial Vocational College of Arts, Chengdu Vocational College of Arts, Southwest Vocational School of Music. Acquired present status and title 1959. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
