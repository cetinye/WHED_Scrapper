History
Founded 1958 as Changchun College of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Merged with Jilin Nursing School for Cadres 1962. Merged with Jilin School of Medicine 1970. Renamed Changchun College of Traditional Chinese Medicine by the famous literatus Guo Moruo 1978. Incorporation of Jilin University of Medical Sciences for Workers and Changchun College of Traditional Chinese Medicine and its affiliated Nursing School 2000. Acquired present status and title 2006. Main administrative province: Jilin Province
