Admission Requirements
Secondary School Certificate or its equivalent with at least seven passes in seven subjects obtained at one and the same examination. In case of O-Level/IGCSE, a pass in each basic sciences subjects (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics and English Language) + any two other subjects which qualify a candidate for admission.
