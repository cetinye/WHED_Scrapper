History
Founded 1919 as l’Ecole Centrale de TSF. Since 2016, ECE is a school of the Management sector of Omnes Education group (previously INSEEC U), a private institution of higher learning, grouping together sixteen grandes écoles and schools specializing in management, engineering, communication and political science
