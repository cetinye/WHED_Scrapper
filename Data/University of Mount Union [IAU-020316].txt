History
Founded 1846 as programmes taught at the “Old Carding Mill”, a “select school” of six students. Became Mount Union Seminary 1849, chartered and incorporated under the laws of the State of Ohio as Mount Union College 1858, united with Scio University 1911. Acquired present status and title 2010
