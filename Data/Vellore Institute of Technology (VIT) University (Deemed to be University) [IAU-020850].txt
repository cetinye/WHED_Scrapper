Admission Requirements
Undergraduate : A pass in 10+2 or its equivalent with a minimum average of 60% of marks. Admission is based on the marks secured in the Entrance Examination conducted by VIT (VITEE). A pass in Higher Secondary Examination conducted by the State/Central Board of Examination with relevant subjects (Science Courses). Postgraduate : BE/Btech Degree in relevant discipline with minimum of 50% aggregate marks is required.
