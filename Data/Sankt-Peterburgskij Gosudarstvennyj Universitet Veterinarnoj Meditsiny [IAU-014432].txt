History
Founded 1808 as a veterinary department within the Imperial Medical-Surgical Academy before becoming the Petrograd Veterinary Institute in 1919. It was then changed to Sankt-Peterburgskaja Gosudarstvennaja Veterinarnaja Akademija (Saint-Petersburg State Academy of Veterinary Medicine) in 1994 and acquired current title and status 2020
