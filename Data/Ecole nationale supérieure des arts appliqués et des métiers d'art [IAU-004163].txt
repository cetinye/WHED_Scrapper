History
Established in 1922, ENSAAMA was later created in 1969 through the merger of the École des Arts Appliqués à l’industrie and the École des Métiers d’Art. Member of HESAM Université (Hautes Ecoles Sorbonne Arts et Métiers Université) – Modified Decree n°2015-1065 of August 26 2015. Also known as Ecole Olivier-de-Serres
