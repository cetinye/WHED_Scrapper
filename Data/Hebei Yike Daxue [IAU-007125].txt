History
Founded 1894. The college was renamed Hebei Medical College 1949. The college moved from Baoding to Shijiazhuang 1958. Hebei Medical College, Hebei College of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Junior Medical College of Shijiazhuang merged into one school, which was named Hebei Medical University 1995. Main administrative body: Hebei Province
