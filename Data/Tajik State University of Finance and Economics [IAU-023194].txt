History
Founded 1942 as Finance and Economics College, became a branch of the All-Union Correspondence Financial College in 1957, became Dushanbe Technical School of Finance and Economics in 1973, became Tajik Financial Institute in 2009, then Finance and Economics Institute of Tajikistan in 2012. Acquired current status and title 2018.
