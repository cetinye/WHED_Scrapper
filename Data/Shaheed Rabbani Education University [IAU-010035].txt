History
Founded 1913 as Teachers Training Centre. Changed name to Darul Malimeen (DM). Became a Teacher Training Academy (TTA) 1946. Transformed into Kabul Institute of Pedagogy (KIP) 1984. Became Kabul Education University 2003. Acquired current title 2012
