History
Founded 1953 as Shijiazhuang School of Supply and Sales under the administration of Hebei Supply and Sales Corporation. HUEB has then integrated three former institutions including Hebei College of Finance and Economics, Hebei College of Economics and Trade, and Hebei Advanced Academy of Business in 1955. Acquired present status and title 1995. Main administrative body: Hebei Province
