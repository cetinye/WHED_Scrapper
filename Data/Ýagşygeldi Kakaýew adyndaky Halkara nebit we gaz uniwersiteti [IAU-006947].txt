History
Created in 1963 as Turkmen Politechnical Institute. Formerly known as Turkmen State Institute of Oil and Gas until 2012. Became International Oil and Gas University in 2013. February 12, 2021, the International Oil and Gas University was given the name of Yagshygeldi Kakayev, in connection with which it was renamed the Yagshygeldi Kakayev International Oil and Gas University
