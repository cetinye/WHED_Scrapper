History
Founded 1896 as Imperial Chinese Railway College. Successively became Jiaotong University Tangshan Technology College, National Jiaotong University Guizhou branch, China Jiaotong University, Northern Jiaotong University, Tangshan Railway College. Acquired present status and title 1972. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
