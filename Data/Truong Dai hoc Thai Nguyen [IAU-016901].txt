History
Founded 1994 following merger of four colleges located in Thai Nguyen City (the College of Engineering, founded 1965; College of Agriculture and Forestry, founded 1970; College of Education, founded 1966; and College of Medicine, founded 1968. Incorporated Vocational Training School (founded 1974); Faculty of Information Technology (2001) and Faculty of Natural Sciences (2002). TNU also includes a Regional Centre for National Defense Education. Acquired present status and title 2004.
