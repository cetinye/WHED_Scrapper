History
Founded 1666. In addition to Swedish-taught education, Lund University provides a large range of English-taught studies, including Bachelor's and Master's degrees, exchange and PhD studies. Campuses are based in Lund, Malmö and Helsingborg
Academic Year
September to June (September-January; January-June)
Admission Requirements
Entry to Bachelor's studies requires completed upper secondary school education; entry to Master's studies requires a Bachelor's degree. English language proficiency of IELTS 6.5 (no section less than 5.5) or TOEFL 90 (writing 20), or an accepted exemption from previous university or secondary school studies taught in English
