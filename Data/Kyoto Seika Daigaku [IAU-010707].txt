History
Founded 1968 as a private two-year College with Departments of Arts and English. In 1979 introduced fully accredited and distinctive 4-yr undergraduate courses in the Faculty of Arts. Faculty of Humanities enrolled its first students 1989. Masters programmes introduced 1991. Doctorate programmes introduced 2003
