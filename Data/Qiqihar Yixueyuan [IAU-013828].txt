History
Founded 1946 as Heilongjiang Military Region Medical College. Became Heilongjiang Province Nursing School 1948, Qiqihar Medical School 1953, Qiqihar Medical Vocational School 1978. Upgraded to a university-level institution and acquired present title 1986. Main administrative body: Heilongjiang Province
