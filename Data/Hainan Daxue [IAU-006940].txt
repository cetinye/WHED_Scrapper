History
Created 1958 as South China University of Tropical Agriculture. Became the original Hainan University following the merger between Hainan Normal Vocational College, Hainan Medical Vocational College, Hainan Agriculture Vocational College 1983. Acquired present status and title following the merger with South China University of Tropical Agriculture. Part of "211 Project" universities. Under the administration of Hainan Province
