History
Founded 1942 as Aerial Science College, recognized 1946 as Tokai University, and officially authorized by the Ministry of Education. From 2008, Tokai Daigaku (Tokai University) Kyushu Tokai Daigaku (Kyushu Tokai University) and Hokkaido Tokai Daigaku (Hokkaido Tokai University) were integrated under one banner as Tokai University
