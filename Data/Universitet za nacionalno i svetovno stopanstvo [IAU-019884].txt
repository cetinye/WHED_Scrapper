History
Founded 1920 as the Free University of Political and Economic Sciences established by Order No.2155 of July, 5th issued by the Minister of National Enlightment. Transformed into State Higher Institute of Finance and Administrative Sciences 1940 and reorganized as a Faculty of Economic and Social Studies at Sofia University 1947. Regained autonomy under the name Higher Institute of Economics 1952. Renamed after Karl Marx 1953. Reorganized as University of National and World Economy 1990. Acquired current status and title 1995. the eldest higher economic school in Bulgaria and Southeastern Europe
