History
Founded 1978 as Taizhou Junior-College-Level Normal School. Upgraded and acquired present title 2002. Between 1995 and 2002 the University incorporated the Taizhou Teachers Training Institute, the Linhai Teachers Training School, the Taizhou Nursing School and Lingwen Teachers Training School. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
