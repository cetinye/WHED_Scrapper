History
Founded 1477. Reorganized 1977 under National Higher Education Reform, incorporating former School of Education founded 1965. Responsible to the Ministry of Education
Academic Year
September to June (September-January; January-June)
Admission Requirements
Bachelor admission: Secondary school certificate. Master's admission: Bachelor degree from an internationally recognized university and poficiency in English by means of an internationally recognised test, e.g. TOEFL, IELTS or the equivalent
Tuition Fees
International:
Students from the UE, EES and Switzerland pay no tuition fees. Students from outside those regions pay c. SEK 100,000-145,000 per year (please visit website for up to date information) (SEK)
