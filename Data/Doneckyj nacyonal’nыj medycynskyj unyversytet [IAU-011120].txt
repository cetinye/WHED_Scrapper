History
Founded 1930 as Stalino State Medical Insitute named after M Gorky. Became Donetsk Medical Institute named after M. Gorky 1961, acquired university status 1994, awarded present title and status 2007. Originally located in Donetsk, it was relocated to Kropyvnytskyi, Kramatorsk and Mariupol in 2014
