History
Founded 1871 out of the Wadsworth Normal and Training School as the Geneseo Normal and Training School, renamed State Normal School of Geneseo 1938, Geneseo State Teachers College 1942, became part of the new State University of New York (SUNY) system 1948, renamed State University College at Geneseo 1962
