History
Founded as Academy of Finance and Banking (Akademi Keuangan dan Perbankan), 1968. Became Stikubank College of Economics (STIE Stikubank), 1992. Stikubank College of Computer Science (STIMIK) was established, 1994. Acquired present title and status in 2001 from the merging of College of Computer Science (STIMIK), College of Foreign Languages (STIBA) and College of Law (STIH).
