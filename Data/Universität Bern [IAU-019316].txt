History
The University of Bern has roots dating back to the 16th century and was officially transformed into a university in 1834. The University of Bern stands for excellence in teaching and research across the entire institution. Its comprehensive study programs range from the sciences to the humanities and beyond. With many internationally acclaimed research strengths, highlights include climate science, space, medicine and sustainable development
