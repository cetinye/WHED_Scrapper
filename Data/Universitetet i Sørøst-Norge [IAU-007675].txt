History
Founded as Høgskolen i Buskerud og Vestfold (Buskerud and Vestfold University College) in 2014 after the merger of Buskerud University College (Høgskolen i Buskerud) and Vestfold University College (Høgskolen i Vestfold). Became Høgskolen i Sørøst-Norge (University College of Southeast Norway) in 2016 after a further merger with Høgskolen i Telemark (Telemark University College). Acquired current title and status in 2018
