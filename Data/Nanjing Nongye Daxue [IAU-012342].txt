History
Its origins trace back to Sanjiang Normal School Agriculture and Natural Sciences Section 1902. Following several mergers became Nanjing Institute of Agriculture 1952. Moved to Yangzhou and merged with Subei Institute of Agriculture, giving rise to Jiangsu Agriculture Institute 1972. Moved to Nanjing and renamed Nanjing Agricultural Institute 1979. Acquired present title 1984. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
