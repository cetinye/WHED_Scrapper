History
Founded 1956 as Nanjing Sports School, builded on the field of the Republican Period National Stadium (1931). Merged with Jiangsu Normal Vocational Sports School, Jiangsu Sport Training Class for Cadres 1958. The School was withdrawed and replaced by the Jiangsu Sports Work Team. The School was reestablished 1978-1980. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
