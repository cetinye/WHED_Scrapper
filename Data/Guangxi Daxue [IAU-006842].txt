History
Founded 1928 in Wuzhou as Guangxi Provincial University. Became National Guangxi University 1939. Incorporated Guangxi Technology College and Science and Technology College 1961. Acquired present status and title 1997 following the merger with Guangxi Agriculture Industry University (founded 1952 as Guangxi College of Agriculture). Part of "211 Project" universities. Under the jurisdiction of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
