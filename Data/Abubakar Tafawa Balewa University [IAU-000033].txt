History
Founded 1980 as the Federal University of Technology, Bauchi. First students admitted 1981, merged with Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria 1984 and renamed Abubakar Tafawa Balewa College, Ahmadu Bello University, Bauchi campus 1984. Acquired autonomy, current status and title 1988. A Federal University
