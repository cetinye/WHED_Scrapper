History
Founded 1944 as Kazakh State Women’s Teacher Training Institute and Pedagogical Women’s College. Became Kazakh State Women's Teacher Training University (Kazakhskij Gosudarstvennyj Ženskij Pedagogičeskij Universitet) in 2008 and acquired current status and title in 2018.
