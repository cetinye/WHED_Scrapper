History
Founded 1949. Graduate Schools established 1953. Known as Tokyo Toritsu Daigaku. Tokyo Toritsu Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan University), Tokyo Toritsu Kagaku Gijutsu Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan Institute of Technology), Tokyo Toritsu Hoken Kagaku Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan University of Health Sciences) and Tokyo Toritsu Tanki Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan College) were merged under the name of Shuto Daigaku Tokyo (Tokyo Metropolitan University) in 2005. Shuto Daigaku Tokyo (Tokyo Metropolitan University) was renamed as Tokyo Toritsu Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan University) in 2020. Tokyoto Koritsu Daigaku Hojin(Tokyo Metropolitan Public University Corporation) is an umbrella organization representing Tokyo Toritsu Daigaku (Tokyo Metropolitan University), Tokyo Toritsu Sangyo Gijutu Daigakuin Daigaku (Advanced Institute of Industrial Technology), and Tokyo Toritsu Sangyo Gijutu Koutou Senmon Gakko (Tokyo Metropolitan College of Industrial Technology)
