Note
Trisakti Institute of Tourism offers a double degree program with Guilin Tourism University, China. Additionally, it collaborates with Burapha University, Thailand, and UCSI University, Malaysia, for the 'Study in Three Countries' program in the Bachelor of Hotel Management. The Institute also has a collaboration with James Cook University.
