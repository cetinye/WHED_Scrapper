Admission Requirements
Applicants for whom English is not the native language should take the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL). A score of 600 (paper-based)/250 (computer test) are the minimum acceptable scores for application review. All application essays and recommendations must be written in English. Transcripts must be translated by a professional translation agency
