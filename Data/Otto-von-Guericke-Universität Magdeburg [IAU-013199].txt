History
Founded 1953 as College of Heavy Mechanical Engineering, became Technical University (Technische Hochschule) 1961. Renamed Technische Universität 1987. Acquired present status and title 1993 after the merging of 3 Universities of Magdeburg (Technische Universität Otto von Guericke, Pädagogische Hochschule, Medizinische Akademie Magdeburg)
