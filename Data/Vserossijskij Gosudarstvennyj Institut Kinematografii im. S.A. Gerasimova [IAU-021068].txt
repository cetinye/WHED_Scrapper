History
Founded 1919 as a state school of cinematography. Became Всесоюзный государственный институт кинематографии the Vsesoyuznyj Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kinematografii (All-Union State Institute of Cinematography) in 1938, Vserossijskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kinematografii im. S.A. Gerasimova (All-Russian State Institute of Cinematography named after S.A. Gerasimov) in 1992, Vserossijskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Kinematografii im. S.A. Gerasimova (All-Russian State University of Cinematography named after S.A. Gerasimov) in 2008, and acquired its current title and status in 2015
