History
Founded 1929 as Mount Mercy College for Women by Sisters of Mercy. Renamed Carlow College 1970 in memory of the Irish heritage of its founders. Males first granted admission after World War II. Acquired present status and title 2004. A Roman Catholic liberal arts College
