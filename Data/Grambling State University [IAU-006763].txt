History
Founded 1901 as the Colored Industrial and Agricultural School, moved to present location and renamed the North Louisiana Agricultural and Industrial School 1905 and later renamed the Louisiana Negro Normal and Industrial Institute. Became Grambling College 1946, acquired current status and title 1974
