History
Established 2006 Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) is an international joint venture university founded by Xi’an Jiaotong University in China and the University of Liverpool in the United Kingdom. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Provincial Educational Department
Academic Year
Sep. to Jan.; Feb. to June
Admission Requirements
Chinese mainland: High School diploma, scores above Tier 1 line in National College Entrance Examination (Gaokao)
Overseas students: High School diploma or leaving certificate or equivalent qualifications with core modules 75% or above, and IELTS 5.5 or TOEFL (iBT) 75
