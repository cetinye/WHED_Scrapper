History
Founded 1950 as Zhejiang Provincial Ningbo Agriculture Technology School. Successively became Ningbo branch of the Zhejiang Agriculture University, Zhejiang Rural Technology Normal Vocational College, Zhejiang Wanli Vocational College of Technology 1999. Acquired present status and title 2001. Main administrative body: Zhejiang Province
