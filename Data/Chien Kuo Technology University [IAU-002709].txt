History
Founded 1965 as Chienkuo Commercial Junior College. Successively renamed Chienkuo Industrial Junior College (1974) and Chienkuo Industrial-and-Commercial Junior College (1992). Upgraded into Chienkuo Institute of Technology 1999. Acquired present status and title 2004.
