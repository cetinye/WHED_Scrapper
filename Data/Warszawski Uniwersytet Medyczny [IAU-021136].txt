History
Founded in 1809. Became Medical Academy in Warsaw in 1950 and acquired current name Medical University of Warsaw in 2008.
Academic Year
October to June (October-January; February-June)
Admission Requirements
Polish candidates: Secondary School certificate (so called 'nowa matura'); physical ability examination (candidates applying for Paramedic Science programme); entrance examinations (candidates applying for second-cycle degree programmes). Foreigners wishing to study at English Divisions: entrance examination.
