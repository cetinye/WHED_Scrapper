History
Founded 1979 in Atlanta, Georgia as the Institute for Physical Therapy, later renamed the Institute of Graduate Health Sciences. Moved to St. Augustine, Florida in 1991 and acquired present status and title 1997. The St. Augustine, Florida site eventually became a campus of the San Marcus operation.
