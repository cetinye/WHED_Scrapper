History
Founded 1958 as Nanchang College of Water Conservancy and Electric Power. Restored (after the Cultural Revolution) 1978, became successively Jiangxi Water Conservancy and Electric Powe Vocational College, Water Conservancy School Branch of the Jiangxi Engineering College, Jiangxi Water Conservancy Vocational School, Nanchang Water Conservancy and Electric Power Vocational School, Nanchang Water Conservancy and Electric Power Junior College. Acquired present title 2004 and status 2005. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Province
