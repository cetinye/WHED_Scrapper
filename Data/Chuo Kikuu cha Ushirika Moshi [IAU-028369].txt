History
Founded 1963 as Co-operative College Moshi as Co-operative College Moshi an autonomous institution. Transformed 2004 into Moshi University College of Cooperative and Business Studies (MUCCoBS) - a the Constituent University College of Sokoine University of Agriculture (SUA). Acquired current status and title 2014. Received the Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU) accreditation 2018
