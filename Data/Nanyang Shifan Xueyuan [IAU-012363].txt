History
Founded 1907 as Nanyang County Normal School. Became Nanyang Normal School 1949. Became Henan Nanyang Normal School 1951. Successively became Nanyang Normal Vocational College (1958), Nanyang Normal School (1962), Nanyang Normal Vocational College (1978), Nanyang Vocational College (1992). Acquired present status and title 2000. Main administrative body: Henan Province
