Note
Under French law, Catholic faculties are not entitled to award official degrees and diplomas without State University agreement. Students prepare for State degrees and diplomas under convention with several French State Universities (Université de Bretagne Sud, Université d'Angers, Université de Paris V....). Professional titles of Ingénieur are officially recognized. The University awards specific professional titles.
