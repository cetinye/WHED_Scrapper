History
Founded 1964 as the Women’s Talent Corps, began admitting men 1969, renamed the College for Human Services 1970 when granted a Charter by the New York State Board of Regents to award associate degrees and soon after it was accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. Renamed Audrey Cohen College in 1992, acquired current title 2002
