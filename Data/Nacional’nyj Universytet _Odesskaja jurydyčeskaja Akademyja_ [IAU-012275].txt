History
Founded 1997 on the basis of the Law Institute of Odessa State University, which originates from the Law faculty of the Richelieu Lyceum - the first higher educational institution in Odessa - founded 1847 that became part of the Imperial Novorossiysk University 1865. Acquired membership at European University Association 1998; Acquired the status of a national higher educational institution 2000. By Presidential Decree, No 893/2010, September 2, 2010, the Academy was reorganized into National University "Odessa Law Academy"
