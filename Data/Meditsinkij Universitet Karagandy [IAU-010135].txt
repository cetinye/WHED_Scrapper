History
Created 1950 as Karaganda Medical Institute. First postgraduate and residency programme opened in 1952. Became Karaganda State Medical Academy in in 1997, became Karaganda State Medical University in 2009 and acquired current title 2018, and receiving ‘Research University’ status in 2019.
