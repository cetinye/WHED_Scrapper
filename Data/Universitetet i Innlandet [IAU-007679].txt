History
Established in 2017 as Inland Norway University of Applied Sciences (Høgskoleni Innlandet) following merger between Lillehammer University College (Høgskolen i Lillehammer) and Hedmark University College (Høgskolen i Hedmark). Acquired current title and status 2024.
