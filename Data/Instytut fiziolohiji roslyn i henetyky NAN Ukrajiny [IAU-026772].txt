History
Founded 1946 as Institute of Plant Physiology and Agrochemistry, USSR Academy of Sciences 1956. Renamed Ukrainian Research Institute of Plant Physiology of the Ukrainian Academy of Agricultural Sciences 1956, Institute of Plant Physiology, Academy of Sciences of the USSR 1962. Acquired current title 1986 after association with the denetic departments of the Institute of Molecular Biology and Genetics
