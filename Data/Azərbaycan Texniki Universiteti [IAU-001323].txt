History
Created 1920 following the closure of Baku Polytechnic technical school and was named Baku Polytechnic Institute, focusing on training engineers in sectors such as agriculture, civil engineering, electromechanics, economics, and oil. In 1923, was renamed Azerbaijan Polytechnic Institute. In 1929 the school was divided into three independent schools covering agriculture, economy, and oil and in 1934 became Azerbaijan Industrial Institute. In 1950 the government reorganized the Institute, establishing Azerbaijan Polytechnic Institute (AzPI) and in 1953 it acquired university status and was renamed Azerbaijan Technical University
