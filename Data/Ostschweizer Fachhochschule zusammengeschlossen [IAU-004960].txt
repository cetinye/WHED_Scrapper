History
The three universities of Applied sciences in the canton of St.Gallen - FHS St.Gallen, HSR Rapperswil and NTB Buchs - merged on September 1, 2020 to form the OST - Eastern Switzerland University of Applied Sciences. OST is the university of applied sciences for the six cantons of Eastern Switzerland and the Principality of Liechtenstein
