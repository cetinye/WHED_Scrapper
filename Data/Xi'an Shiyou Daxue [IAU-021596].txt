History
Founded 1951 as Northwest Oil Industry Training School. Upgraded to a college, renamed Xi'an Petroleum College 1958. Transformed into a factory 1969 during the Great Cultural Revolution. Resumed educational activity as Xi'an Petroleum College 1980. Acquired present title 2003. Under the jurisdiciton of Shaanxi Province
