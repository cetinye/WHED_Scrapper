History
Founded 1949 as People Liberation Army XCPP (Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps) Health School. Acquired present status and title 1996 following the merger between Shihezi Medical College, Shihezi Agricultural College, XCPP Teacher Training School, XCPP Economic Vocational College. Part of "211 Project" universities. Under the administration of the XCPP
