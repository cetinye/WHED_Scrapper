History
Founded 1933 as Southern Colorado Junior College (SCJC), renamed Pueblo Junior College 1937 and Pueblo College 1946. Acquired current status and renamed Southern Colorado State College (SCSC) 1961. Started operating voacational programmes thorugh the College for Community Services and Career Education 1974, which changed status from a component of the University of Southern Colorado to a separate and free-standing educational entity 1978. The Pueblo Vocational Community College became a State System Community College 1979. Acquired current title 1982
