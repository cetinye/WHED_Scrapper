History
Founded 1956 as Chengdu College of Geological Survey. Became Chengdu College of Geology 1958, Chengdu college of Technology 1993. Acquired present title 2001 following the incorporation of Sichuan Commercial Junior College and the Non-Ferrous Metal Geological Staff University 2001. Under the administration of Sichuan Province
