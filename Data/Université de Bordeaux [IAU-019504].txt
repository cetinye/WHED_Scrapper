History
Founded 1970 under the 1968 law reforming higher education as one of the four Universities replacing the former Université de Bordeaux - founded 1441 by Papal Bull with Faculties of Theology; Canon Law; Civil Law; Medicine; and Arts. Member of "COMUE d 'Aquitaine" (Communities of universities and establishments) – Decree n° 2015-281 of March 11, 2015
