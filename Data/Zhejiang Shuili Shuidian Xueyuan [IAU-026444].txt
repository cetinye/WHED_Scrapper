History
Established 1953 as Hangzhou Water Power and Electric Power School and Hangzhou Water Resources School 1956. Successively became Zhejiang Electric Power Postsecondary Training School 1958, Zhejiang Water Resources and Electric Power School 1975, Zhejiang Water Resources and Hydraulic Power School Postsecondary Training School 1984. Acquired present status and title 2013. Main administrative province: Zhejiang Province
