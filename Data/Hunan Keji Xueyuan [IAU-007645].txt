History
Created 1941 as Hunan Provincial Seventh Normal School. Became Lingling Normal School 1953. Renamed as Lingling District Middle School Teachers Training Class 1971. Became Hunan Normal Institute Lingling school branch 1976. Became Lingling Teachers Vocational School 1981. Acquired present status as Lingling Normal College 2002. Acquired current title 2004.
