History
Founded 1951 as Southwest Jiaotong Vocational College. Became Chongqing Jiaotong College 1960 following the merger between Chengdu Technology College Civil Engineering Department, Sichuan Metallurgy College Metallurgy Department, Wuhan Water Transport College Water Technology Department. Metallurgy Department. Acquired present title 2006. Main administrative body: Chongqing Municipality
