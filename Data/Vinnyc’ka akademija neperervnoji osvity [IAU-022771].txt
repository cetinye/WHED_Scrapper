History
Founded 1937 as a regional pedagogical office, transformed into Vinnytsia Institute of teacher improvement 1940. Ceased its activities during the Nazi occupation, reopened 1944. Later renamed Vinnytsia Regional Institute of Postgraduate Education for Teachers, acquired current status 2000 and current title 2008
