History
Founded 1970 as Centre Universitaire de Perpignan under the 1968 law reforming higher education and incorporating institutions previously attached to the former Université de Montpellier. Acquired present status and title 1979. Member of the COMUE Languedoc Roussillon Universities - (Décree n° 2014-1682 - 30 décembre 2014, modified)
