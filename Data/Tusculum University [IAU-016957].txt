History
Founded 1794 as Greenville College, merged 1868 with Tusculum College (founded 1828 as Tusculum Academy), became co-ed 1875, became Washington & Tusculum College (W&T) following merger with Washington College 1908, Washington & Tusculum merger dissolved leaving the current Tusculum College 1912, acquired current status and title 2018. A private institution affiliated with the Presbyterian Church
