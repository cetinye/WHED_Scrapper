History
Founded 1930 as Shandong Second Rural Normal School. Successively became Jiaodong School, Laiyang Normal School, Laiyang Vocational College, Yantai Normal Vocational College, Yantai Normal College. Incorporated Shandong Jiaotong School (1978) 2001. Acquired present title 2006. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
