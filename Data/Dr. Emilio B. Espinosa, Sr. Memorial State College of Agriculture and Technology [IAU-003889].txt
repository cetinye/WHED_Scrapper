History
Founded 1952 as Masbate National Agricultural School (MNAS). Became Masbate Agricultural College (MAC) in 1966, became Dr. Emilio B. Espinosa, Sr. Memorial Agricultural College (DEBESMAC) in 1977, acquired current title and status 2005.
