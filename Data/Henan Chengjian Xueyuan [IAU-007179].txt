History
Founded 2000 as Henan Urban Construction College though merger between Pingdingshan Urban Environmental Protection School (founded 1983) and Henan Urban Construction College (formerly the Henan branch of Wuhan Urban Construction University, founded 1985). Upgraded to a university-level institution 2002. Acquired present title 2008. Main administrative body: Henan Province
