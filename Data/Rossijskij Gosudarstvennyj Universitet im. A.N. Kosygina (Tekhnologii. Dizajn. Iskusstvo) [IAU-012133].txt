History
Founded 1930 as Moscow Technological Institute of Light Industry (MTILI). Became Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Dizajna i Tekhnologii (Moscow State University of Design and Technology) in 1999. Acquired current title 2012 following merger with Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekstil'nyj Universitet im. A.N. Kosygina (Moscow State Textile University named after A.N. Kosygin)
