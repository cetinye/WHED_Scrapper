History
Founded 1962 as private Istituto Superiore di Scienze Sociali by the Istituto Trentino di Cultura. Recognized by the State 1966 and became Libera Università 1972. Acquired status and title 1982. A State Institution enjoying administrative autonomy. Acquired present status 1995
