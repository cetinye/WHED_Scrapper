History
Founded 1948. Associate member of the COMUE Université fédérale de Toulouse Midi-Pyrénées – Decree n°2016-468 of April 2016 modified. ENAC continues its international development in Africa: creation of an aeronautical centre of excellence in the Institut national polytechnique Félix Houphouët-Boigny (INP-HB), 2018. An Engineering School and a Public Scientific, Cultural and Professional Establishment (EPSCP)
