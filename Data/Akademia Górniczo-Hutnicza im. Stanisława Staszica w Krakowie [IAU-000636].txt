History
Founded 1919 as Akademia Górnicza (Academy of Mining). Renamed as Akademia Górniczo-Hutnicza 1949. Acquired present title 1969. Was known in English as AGH University of Science and Technology from 2003 and as AGH University of Krakow from 2023.
