History
Founded 1931 as The School of Fine and Applied Art of the John and Mable Ringling Art Museum, an art school which was a remote branch of Southern College. Separated from Southern College and became an independent nonprofit institution renamed Ringling School of Art 1933, an then Ringling School of Art and Design 1979, acquired current title 2008
