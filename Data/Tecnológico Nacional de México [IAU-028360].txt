History
The Tecnológico Nacional de México (National Technological Institute of Mexico) is composed of 254 institutions, including 126 federal technological institutes, 122 decentralised technological institutes (Tecnológico Nacional de México - TecNM), four Regional Center for Optimization and Development of equipment (CRODE), an interdisciplinary centre for research and teaching of technical education (CIIDET) and a national centre for research and technological development (CENIDET). In these institutions, TecNM serves a student population of over 600,000 undergraduate and postgraduate students throughout the country, including Mexico City
