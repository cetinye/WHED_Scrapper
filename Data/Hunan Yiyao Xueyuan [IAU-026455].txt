History
Established 1912 by Yan Fuqing as Changsha Red Cross Nursing Training Institute. Officially established as Changsha Renshu Care Institute 1924. Moved to Anjiang and became Qianyang Health School 1961. Moved to Huaihua and renamed Huaihua Health School 1984. Became Huaihua Junior Medical College 2000. Acquired present status and title 2014. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
