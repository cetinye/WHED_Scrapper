History
Founded 1970 as Zhaoqing Teachers School. Successively became Zhaoqing 5th, 7th Normal School 1975, Zhaoqing Normal Vocational College 1979, Xijiang University 1985. Acquired present status and title 2000 through the merger with Zhaoqing Education College (founded 1976). Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
