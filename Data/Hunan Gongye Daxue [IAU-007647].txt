History
Founded 1958 as Zhouzhou Normal School. Became Zhuzhou University 1985. Renamed as Zhuzhou Institute of Technology 1989. Incorporated Hunan Construction Planning School 2001, Hunan Finance and Accountancy School 2004. Acquired present status and title 2006 following the merger with Zhuzhou Normal College and Hunan Metallurgical Professional Technology College. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
