History
Founded 1913 as the State School of Agriculture and Domestic Science at Delhi, first students enrolled 1915, renamed New York State Agricultural and Technical Institute at Delhi 1941, became one of the original 14 state colleges and agricultural institutes to form the new State University of New York (SUNY) system, renamed New York Agricultural and Technical College at Delhi 1964
