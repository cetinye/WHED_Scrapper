History
Founded 1937 as National Southwest Union University through the merger of Peking University, Tsinghua University and Nankai University. Became National Kunming Normal College 1946, then Kunming Normal College 1950. Acquired present title 1984. Under the jurisdiction of the Yunnan Provincial Government.
