Founded 1866 as Grand Rapids Business College. Renamed Davenport College of Business 1910, Davenport University-Western Region 2000, acquired current title 2003 as Davenport’s three separate, regional colleges (Davenport College, Detroit College of Business and Great Lakes College) were unified into one Davenport University
