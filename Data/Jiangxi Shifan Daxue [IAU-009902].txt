History
Founded 1940 as National Zhongzheng University. Renamed as Nanchang University 1949 and Jiangxi Normal College 1953. Acquired present status and title 1983. Merged with Jiangxi Vocational University of Finance 2003. Main competent authority: Jiangxi Province
