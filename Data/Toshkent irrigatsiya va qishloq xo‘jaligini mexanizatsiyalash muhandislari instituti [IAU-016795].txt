History
Founded 1920 on the base of Hydrotechnical Faculty of the Turkestan State University. Became Toshkent Irrigatsiya va Melioratsiya Instituti (Tashkent Institute of Irrigation and Land Rehabilitation) in 2004 and acquired present status as a National Research University and current title in 2021.
