History
Founded 1902 as Odessa Miller's School. Became Odessa State Academy of Food Technology (Odes’ka deržavna akademija xarčovyx texnolohijvul) in 1994, Odessa National Academy of Food Technologies (Odes’ka nacional’na akademija xarčovyx texnolohij) in 2002 and acquired current title and status in 2021.
