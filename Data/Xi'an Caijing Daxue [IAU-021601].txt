History
Created 1952 as Northwest Trade School. After several changes of titles became Shaanxi Commerce Training School (Postsecondary Vocational School) 1985. Merged with Shaanxi Finance and Politics Training School (founded 1960) and Shaanxi Industry and Commerce College (founded 1978) to shape Shaanxi Economics and Trade College (or Shaanxi Institute of Economics and Trade) 1996. Became Xi'an College of Economics and Trade 2001 following the merger with Xi' an College of Statistics. Acquired present title 2018. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
