History
Founded 1989 as Open Learning Institute of Hong Kong, became the The Open University of Hong Kong, a self-accrediting institution in1997, and acquired current title in 2021.
Admission Requirements
For undergraduate programmes, students should hold the Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education or equivalent. For postgraduate programmes, students should have an undergraduate degree.
