History
Founded 1991 as Koliat Elhasibat Elalia (Computer Man College for Computer Studies) and acquired current title and status 2010.
Admission Requirements
Candidates must have one of the following certificates: Sudan School Certificate (academic, technical, commercial); Equivalent School certificate from Arab countries; Egyptian Secondary School Certificate; Foreign certificate for abroad; All non- Sudanese certificates are equated and assessed on the basis of Sudanese secondary school certificate in which the candidate must pass seven subjects from which the percentage of admission is calculated to include the four main subjects and three optional ones according to area of specialization.
