History
Founded 1635 as Department of Theology of the University founded by Péter Pázmány, Cardinal, Primate, and Archbishop of Esztergom in Nagyszombat. Acquired present status and title in 1993. An ecclesiastic institution recognized by the State. Under supervision of the Holy See.
