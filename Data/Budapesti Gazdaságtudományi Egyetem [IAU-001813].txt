History
Founded as Pest Academy of Commerce in 1857. Re-established as Budapest Business School (Budapesti Gazdasági Főiskola) in 2000. Acquired University of Applied Sciences status in 2013, became Budapesti Gazdasági Egyetem (Budapest Business University) in 2023 and acquired current title and status 1 February 2025.
