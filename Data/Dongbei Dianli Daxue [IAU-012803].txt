History
Founded 1949 as the Shenyang Electrical Machinery Higher Vocational School. Upgraded to a College and renamed as Jilin College of Electric Power 1958. Renamed as Northeast College of Electric Power 1978 . The college changed its name to Northeast Dianli University 2005. Acquired present english title 2016
