Admission Requirements
Undergraduate: 5 subject passes, including English and Chinese, in the Hong Kong Certificate of Education Examination (HKCEE), and a Diploma awarded by the Academy. Equivalent certificates are also considered. Master's programmes: Bachelor's Degree in relevant discipline with requisite marks.
