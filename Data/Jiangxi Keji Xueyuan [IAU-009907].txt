History
Founded 1994 as Jiangxi Provincial Advanced Vocational School. Became postsecondary vocational institute and renamed Lantian Institute of Technology 1999. Acquired present status and renamed Jiangxi Lantian College 2005. Started conferring Bachelor's degrees 2009. Acquired present title 2012. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Provincial Education Department
