History
Founded 1958 as Lanzhou Railway College, through the merger of departments and sections from Tangshan Railway Institute (the present Southwest Jiaotong University) and Beijing Railway Institute (the present Beijing Jiaotong University). Acquired present title 2003. Under the administration of Gansu Province
