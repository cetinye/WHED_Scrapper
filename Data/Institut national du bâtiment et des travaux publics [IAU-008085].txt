History
Founded 1961 as INBTP. Merged with Institut supérieur des géomètres experts immobiliers (ISGEI), a private institution, 1969. Merged with Institut supérieur d’architecture (ISA) et l’Institut supérieur des arts plastiques (ISAP) to form the Institut national des travaux publics (INTP) 1971. Integrated into Université Nationale du Zaïre as UNAZA/INTP 1972. Became IBTP 1981, following dissolution of UNAZA. Split into two institutions 2010. Institut national du bâtiment et des travaux publics (INBTP) and Institut supérieur d’architecture et d'urbanisme (ISAU). Started implementing the LMD System 2019. Also 8 other public Instituts du bâtiment et des travaux publics (IBTP) spread accross the following provinces and cities: KasaÏ (Luebo), Kasai-Oriental (Mbuji-Mayi), Kongo-Central (Matadi), Maniema (Kindu), Nord-Kivu (Butembo), Sud-Kivu (Kamituga, Nyangezi), Tshopo (Kisangani); 1 private Institut du bâtiment et des travaux publics (IBTP) in Buta
