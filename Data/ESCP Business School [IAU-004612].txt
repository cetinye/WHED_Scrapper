History
Established in Paris in 1819, ESCP is the oldest business school in the world and was destined to be European from the outset. In 2023, the school celebrates 50 years of European identity and configuration. Its first overseas campuses date back to 1973. Today, with campuses in Berlin, London, Madrid, Paris, Turin, and Warsaw, ESCP is the only pan-European business school offering a unique and comprehensive vision of Europe within a mature continental multi-campus framework. Since January 2018, the school has operated as a private non-profit institution, with a public shareholder (CCIR) owning the majority of total shares. The Alumni Association and ESCP Foundation are the other two shareholders.

Note
The Master in Management is the flagship programme of the ESCP BS.
ESCP delivers two MBAs which are both accredited by the French Ministry of Higher Education and Research as Master degrees.
- a full-time MBA (MBA in International Management)
- a part-time MBA (Executive MBA).
In 2021, ESCP decided to transform its Specialised Masters and MSc into a common MSc programme and to seek national French accreditation (Master degree ) for the new Diplôme d’études avancées en management international des entreprises (DEAMIE).
In Paris, ESCP BS prepares two doctorate-level degrees :
- The Doctoral programme is accredited by the French Ministry of Higher Education and Research to deliver the «doctorat en sciences de gestion» within the Ecole doctorale Management Panthéon Sorbonne
- Th Global Executive PhD, delivering a School's degree, offers a unique opportunity to follow a world-class practitioner-oriented doctorate education in management.
