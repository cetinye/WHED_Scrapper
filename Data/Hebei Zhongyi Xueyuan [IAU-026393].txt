History
Founded 1956 as Hebei Traditional Chinese Medicine Postsecondary Training School. Incorporated Hebei TCM Advanced Training School and acquired present title 1958. Moved to Tianjin and merged with Tianjin TCM College 1962. Moved to Shijiazhuang and became Hebei New Medicine University 1969. Hebei TCM College became indipendent 1983, merged with Shijiazhuang Junior Medical College to form Hebei Medicine University 1995. Hebei TCM scorporated again 2013.
