History
Founded 1931 as Stalingradskij Industrial'no-pedagogičeskiy Institut (Stalingrad Industrial Pedagogical Institute), became Volgogradskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Institut (Volgograd State Pedagogical Institute) in 1961, Volgogradskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet (Volgograd State Pedagogical University) in 1992 and acquired current title 2011
