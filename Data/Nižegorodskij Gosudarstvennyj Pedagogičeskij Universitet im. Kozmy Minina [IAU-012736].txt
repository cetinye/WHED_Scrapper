History
Founded 1911. Reorganised as Pedagogical Institute 1918. Became Nižegorodskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet (Nizhny Novgorod State Pedagogical University) 1993 and acquired current title and status 2011 following a merger with Volzhsky State Engineering-Pedagogical University (Volžskij Gosudarstvennyj Inženerno-Pedagogičeskij Universitet)
