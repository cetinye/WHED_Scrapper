History
Created 1947 as the National Southwest Normal School. Became Yunnan Wenshan Normal School 1955, then Wenshan Normal Postsecondary Specialised School 1984 ( Vocational College), Wenshan Junior Normal College 1992. Acquired present status and title 2009. Under the administration of Yunnan Province
