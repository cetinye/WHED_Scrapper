History
Universitas Kristen Duta Wacana (UKDW) was established on October 31, 1985, as a further development of Duta Wacana Seminary of Theology founded on October 31, 1962. UKDW is managed by Duta Wacana Higher Education Foundation as the representation of 12 synods or churches that support the university.
