History
Founded 1964 in Ioannina as a branch Department of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki's School of Philosophy. Acquired present status and title 1970. In 2018, according to law 4559/2018, the Technical Educational Institute of Epirus merged with the University of Ioannina. A state institution of the Epirus Region.
