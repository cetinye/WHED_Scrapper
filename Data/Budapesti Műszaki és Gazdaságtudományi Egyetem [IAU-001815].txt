History
Founded 1782 as Institutum Geometricum Hydrotechnicum, acquired University status 1871. Reorganized 1949 as Technical University of Budapest incorporating former Technical University of Building and Transport Engineering, founded 1952. Acquired present title 2000. Under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Culture and Education.
