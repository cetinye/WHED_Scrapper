History
Created 1958 as Xiangyang Teachers Training School. Following the merger between Xiangfan Vocational University and Xiangfan Education College, Xianyang Junior College became Xiangfan University (Xiangfan Xueyuan) 1998. Absorbed Hubei School of Fine Arts 2007. Acquired present title 2012. Main administrative body: Hubei Province
