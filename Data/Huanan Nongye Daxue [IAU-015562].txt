History
Founded 1909 as Guangdong Agricultural Testing Ground affiliated Agricultural Institute. Acquired present status 1952 following the merger between Zhongshan University Agricultural College, Lingnan University Agricultural College and Guangxi University Agricultural College Animal Husbandry, Veterinarian Department, Plant Diseases and Insect Pests. Acquired present title 1984. Part of "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
