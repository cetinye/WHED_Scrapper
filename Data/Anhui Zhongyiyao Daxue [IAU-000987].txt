History
Founded 1959 when Anhui Traditional Chinese Medicine Training Class (1952) became Anhui College of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Incorporated into Anhui Medical College 1970. Became again a separated college 1975. Incorporated Anhui School of Medicine 2000. Became Anhui College of Traditional Medicine 2011. Acquired present title 2013.
