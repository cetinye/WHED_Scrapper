History
Founded 1st August 2007 from an amalgamation of St Martin’s College (set up 1891 by Charlotte Mason as an Ambleside-based training school) , Cumbria Institute of the Arts (established 1822), and the Cumbrian campuses of the University of Central Lancashire. The Ambleside campus is situated within both a UNESCO World Heritage site and the UK's Lake District National Park
