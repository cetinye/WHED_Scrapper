History
Set up 1948 by the People's Government of the Northeast as Shenyang Commercial College. Became Liaoning University 1958, following the merger of the Northeast Institute of Finance and Economics, Shenyang Normal College and Shenyang Institute of Russian. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
