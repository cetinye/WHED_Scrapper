History
Founded under King William I of the Netherlands in 1817. Designated as a State University following the independence of Belgium in 1835. Provision was made in 1959 for the transfer of the University to a new campus. The University enjoys limited autonomy and receives the major share of its income from the Communauté française de Belgique
