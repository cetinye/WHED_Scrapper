History
Founded 1856 as Union Seminary and rechartered as Central Pennsylvania College in 1887. Merged with Albright College (founded as Albright Collegiate Institute 1895 and became Albright College 1898) 1902, merged with Albright College 1902, which then merged wih the Schuylkill College, founded 1881 as the chuylkill Seminary) 1929 and moved from its campus in Myerstown, Pa., to Schuylkill College’s location on the present Albright campus
