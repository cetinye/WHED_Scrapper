History
Founded 1897 as Qiushi Academy. Established as Third National Sun Yat-sen University 1927. Became National Zhejiang University 1928, Moved to Guizhou during the Sino-Japanese War. Moved back to 1946. Separated into several colleges 1952. Merged with Hangzhou University, Zhejiang Agricultural University and Zhejang Medical University to form the new Zhejiang University 1998. Part of "211 Project " and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
