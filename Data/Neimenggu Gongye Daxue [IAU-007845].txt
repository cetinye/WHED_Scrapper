History
Founded 1951 as Suiyuan Higher Technical College. Suiyuan Higher Technical School was upgraded and renamed as Inner Mongolia Engineering College 1960. Initially under direct administration of the Ministry of Mechanical Industry and the Ministry of Agricultural Machinery, the college has been subordinated to Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region 1983. The College was upgraded once again and renamed 1993.
