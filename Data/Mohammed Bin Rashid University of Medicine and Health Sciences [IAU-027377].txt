History
Established in 2014. Earned accreditation status in 2014. Offering undergraduate and postgraduate degrees through two colleges, the Hamdan Bin Mohammed College of Dental Medicine, and the College of Medicine for undergraduate medical students offering a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery program.
