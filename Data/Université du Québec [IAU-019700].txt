History
Founded 1974. An Engineering School, part of the University of Quebec network specialized in applied engineering and technology. Cooperative education system with work-study programme.
Academic Year
September to August (September-December; January-April; May-August)
Admission Requirements
Diploma of Collegial Studies (DEC) following successful completion of 3-year pre-university course in physical technology or technology of computer-controlled systems, at a CEGEP (Collège d'Enseignement général et professionnel) in Quebec, or recognized equivalent
