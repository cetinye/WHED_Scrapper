Admission Requirements
Bachelor’s degree: Swiss Maturity Certificate (Maturität, Matura) or a Swiss Professional Maturity Certificate (Maturité professionnelle) with “Passerelle” or a Swiss Specialised Maturity Certificate (Maturité spécialisée) recognised throughout Switzerland with “Passerelle” or, in the case of foreign candidates, an upper secondary school-leaving certificate considered equivalent; evidence of sufficient English language proficiency.
Candidates that have completed a Swiss Professional or Specialised Maturity need to undergo a complementary examination in order to be eligible for admission into the Bachelor program.
Master’s degrees: A Bachelor's degree from a university or other higher education institution or an equivalent foreign higher education institution recognised or accredited in the country of origin; evidence of sufficient English language proficiency.
Students who wish to pursue a dual degree with our educational partner Tiffin University (TU) are subjected to the specific TU admission requirements.
