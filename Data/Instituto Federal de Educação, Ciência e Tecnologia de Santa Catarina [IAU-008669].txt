History
Founded 1909 as Escola de Aprendizes Artífices de Santa Catarin. Became Liceu Industrial de Florianópolis 1937. Transformed into Escola Industrial de Florianópolis 1942. Name changed to Escola Industrial Federal de Santa Catarina 1965. Became Escola Técnica Federal de Santa Catarina (ETF-SC) 1968. Transformed into Centro Federais de Educação Tecnológica (CEFET-SC) 1994. Acquired present title and status 2008
