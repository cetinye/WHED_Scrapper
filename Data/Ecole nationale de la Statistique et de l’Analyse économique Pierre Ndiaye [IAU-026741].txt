History
Founded 1966 and acquired current status 2011. A national professional higher education institution with a regional focus, attached to the Agence nationale de la Statistique et de la
Démographie (ANSD). Recrutes students from fourteen African countries and also has agreements for double diplomas ISE in partnership with ENSAE Paris and ENSAI Rennes
