History
Founded 1931 as an agricultural academy, became Čuvašskij Sel'skohozjajstvennyj Institut (Chuvash Agricultural Institute) in 1941, was renamed Čuvašskaja Gosudarstvennaja Sel'skohozjajstvennaja Akademija (Chuvash State Agricultural Academy) in 1995 and acquired current title and status 2020
