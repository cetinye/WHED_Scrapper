History
Founded 1898 as Hubei Farming School. Following several changes, became Huazhong Agricultural College (1950-1952) and Huazhong Agricultural College (1952-1985). Acquired present title 1985. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
