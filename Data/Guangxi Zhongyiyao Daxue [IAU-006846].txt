History
Founded 1956 as Guangxi Traditional Chinese Medical School. Became Guangxi Chinese Medicine Vocational School 1958, Guangxi Chinese Medicine College 1964. Incorporated Nanning Medical College 1970. Acquired present title 2012. Formerly english name was Guangxi University of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Under the jurisdiction of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
