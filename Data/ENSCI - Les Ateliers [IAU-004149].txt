History
Founded in 1982, ENSCI is the first French public institute specialising in advanced studies in design. In 1985, ENSCI extended its activities to include the ANAT, National Textile Art Studio. Founding member of the COMUE HESAM Université (Hautes Ecoles Sorbonne Arts et Métier– Decree n°2015-1065 of August 26 2015 modified)
