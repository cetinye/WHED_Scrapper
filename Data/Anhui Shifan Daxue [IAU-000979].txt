History
Founded 1928 as Anhui Provincial University in Anqing. Became National Anhui University 1946. Moved to Wuhu 1949. Renamed respectively as Anhui Normal College, Hefei Normal College, Wan'an University, Anhui Workers and Peasants University. Acquired present title 1972. Incorporated Wuhu Teachers School 2005. Main administrative authority: Anhui Province
