History
Founded 1970, incorporating the Federal Faculty of Law, founded 1934, the Faculty of Philosophy, Science and Letters of Mato Grosso and the Institute of Science and Letters of Cuiabá. A State institution financed by the Federal Government and responsible to the Ministry of Education and Sports
