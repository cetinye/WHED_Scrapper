History
Dalarna University (DU), located in the Dalarna region of Central Sweden, is one of Sweden's mid-sized universities. It offers undergraduate, graduate and doctoral programmes in a wide range of disciplines, the most important of which are Education, Health Sciences, Engineering, Business Studies and Media. Since its establishment in 1977, Dalarna University has grown rapidly and today consists of five schools on two campuses, located in the twin cities of Falun and Borlänge
