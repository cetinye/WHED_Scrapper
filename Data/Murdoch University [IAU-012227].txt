History
First admitted students to the Murdoch campus in 1975 and in 1996 opened a regional campus at Rockingham. Peel campus at Mandurah opened 2005.

Admission Requirements
Demonstrated merit and evidence of suitability for a university education. Western Australian school leavers must demonstrate competence in English or English literature and obtain a sufficiently high Tertiary Entrance Rank (TER) score.
