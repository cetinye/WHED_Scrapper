History
Founded 1981 as Advanced Teacher's College, Waje. Renamed College of Education, Kumbotso 1981 and placed under the then Kano State Institute for Higher Education. Gained autonomy 1987 and renamed Sa'adatu Rimi College of Education (SRCOE). Acquired current status and title 2023
