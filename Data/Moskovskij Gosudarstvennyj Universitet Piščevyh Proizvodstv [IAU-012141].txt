History
Founded 1931 as Institute of Bakery Engineering, became independent 1941 and acquired present status and title 1996. Merged with Moscow State University of Applied Biotechnology (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Prikladnoj Biotehnologii) in 2011, the latter becoming an institute of the university
