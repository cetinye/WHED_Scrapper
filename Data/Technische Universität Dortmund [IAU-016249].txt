History
Founded 1968. An autonomous institution financially supported by the State of North Rhine-Westphalia. On November 1, 2007, The University Dortmund (Universität Dortmund) has been renamed as TU Dortmund University (Technische Universität Dortmund)

Centre : Center for Teacher Training (DoKoLL)
Fields of study:
Education, Teacher Training
More details:
DoKoLL is the central point of contact for all matters pertaining to teacher training. At TU Dort­mund University, prospective teachers receive sound training that is research based and practice related.
Centre : Centre for Synchrotron Radiation (DELTA)
Fields of study:
Physics
More details:
DELTA is the world’s only electron storage ring located on a uni­ver­si­ty campus. Here, researchers shed light on the best-kept secrets of nature – the tiniest constituents of matter, atoms, protein chains.
