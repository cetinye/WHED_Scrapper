History
Founded 2002 as a Centre of St. Augustine University of Tanzania (SAUT) in Mbeya, known as SAUT-Mbeya Centre as of 2013. Became Catholic University College of Mbeya (CUoM) 2020, a constituent college of St. Augustine University of Tanzania (SAUT). Acquired current status and title 2024. Owned by the Tanzania Episcopal Conference (TEC)
