History
Founded 1955 as Institute of Public Administration attached to Thammasat University. Became separate and acquired present title and status 1966 with support from the Ford Foundation and Midwest Consortium for International Activities (Indiana, Illinois, Wisconsin, and Michigan State Universities). A State institution for postgraduate studies.
