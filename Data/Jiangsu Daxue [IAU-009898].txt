History
Founded 1902 as Sanjiang Normal School. Became Nanjin University 1949. Became Zhenjiang Agricultural Industry and Machinery Institute 1961. Became Jiangsu Institute of Technology 1982. Acquired present status 2001 following merger of Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, Zhenjiang Teachers College, and Zhenjiang Medical College. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
