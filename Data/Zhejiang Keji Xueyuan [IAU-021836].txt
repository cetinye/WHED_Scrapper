History
Founded 1980 as the Zhejiang University affiliated Hangzhou Industry Vocational College. Successively became Zhejiang University affiliated Hangzhou Junior College, Hangzhou Applied Engineering and Technology College (Hanghzou Institute of Applied Engineering). Acquired present status and title 2001. Incorporated Zhejiang Light Industry School 2003. Main administrative body: Zhejiang Province
