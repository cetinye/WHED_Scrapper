History
Founded 1923 as National Cultural Development Unit. Then became Jinan Building College 1948, Hebei Ji County Normal School 1949. Became Hengshui Normal Teacher Training School 1978. Hengshui Normal School and Hengshui Education College merged into it 1998. Acquired present status and title 2004. Main administrative body: Hebei Province
