History
Founded as college by the Society of Jesus in 1831. Reconstituted under present title in 1833. First authorized to award degrees in 1929. Granted full University status in 1971. Previously known as Facultés universitaires Notre-Dame de la Paix. Acquired current title 2012. Independent, but recognized and financially supported by the State
