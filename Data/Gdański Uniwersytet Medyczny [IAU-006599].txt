History
Founded 1945 as Akademię Lekarską. Changed name to Akademia Medyczna w Gdańsku (Academy of Medicine, Gdańsk) 1950. Acquired present status 1956. Acquired present title 2009. One of the largest medical universities on the southern coast of the Baltic Sea.
