History
Founded 1996 as Aktobe branch of the Kazakh State Academy of Management, in 1997 became West-Kazakhstan Institute of Economics and Finance, in 2001 became S. Baishev Aktobe University (Aktiubinskij Universitet imeni S. Baisheva) and in 2019, following a rebranding, acquired current title.
