History
Founded 1953 as a division of the Yeshiva University. Montefiore and Yeshiva University finalized an agreement that established Einstein as its own legal entity and transferred operational and financial responsibility for the College of Medicine to Montefiore 2015. Was awarded an absolute charter by the Board of Regents of New York State’s Department of Education 2019, establishing it as an autonomous educational institution with the authority to confer its own medical and graduate degrees. Middle States Commission on Higher Education (MSCHE) granted full independent accreditation for all its programmes 2021, completing the transformation to a fully independent academic entity. The institution is affiliated with both Montefiore Health System and Yeshiva University
