History
Founded 1967 as Institut de gestion du personnel et d'organisation du travail (IGEPOT). Transfered to the newly created Université nationale du Zaïre (UNAZA) 1971, in which it started operating as a department of the Faculty of Educational Sciences. Renamed Institut de gestion des entreprises (IGE) 1981 that was progressively transformed into Institut supérieur de commerce (ISCOM). Acquired autonomy 1981. Acquired current title 1982. Belogns to the UNAZA university network, which also includes Université de Kisangani (UNIKIS), Institut supérieur de commerce de Kinshasa (ISC Kinshasa), Université de Lubumbashi (UNILU)
