History
Founded 1999 through the upgrade of the Ebonyi State University College, that itself resulted from the upgrade of the Abakaliki campus of the Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT) 1998. Acquired autonomous status 2000
Admission Requirements
University Matriculation Examination UME/DE. Credit passes in English Language, Mathematics and other three (3) courses, relevant in the area of study in not more than two (2) sittings
