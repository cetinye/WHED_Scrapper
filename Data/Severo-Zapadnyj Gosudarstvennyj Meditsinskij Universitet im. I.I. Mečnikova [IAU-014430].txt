History
Founded 1907 as Psychoneurological Institute, renamed 1946 as Leningrad Medical Institute of Sanitary and Hygiene. Became Sankt-Peterburgskaja Gosudarstvennaja Medicinskaja Akademija im. I.I. Mečnihova (Saint-Petersburg State Medical Academy named after I.I. Mechnikov) in 1994 and acquired current title and status 2011 following merger with a postgraduate medical training institute
