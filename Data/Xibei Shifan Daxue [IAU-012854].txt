History
Founded 1902 as the Imperial Higher Learning Institution Normal School. Became National Beijing Normal Junior College 1912, then National Beiping Normal University 1923. Became Northwest Union University Education College 1937, then separated from it and became National Northwest Normal College 1949. Moved to Lanzhou. Became Gansu Normal University 1958, then Northwest Normal College 1981. Acquired present title 1988. Under the administration of Gansu Province
