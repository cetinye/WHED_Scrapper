History
Founded 1982 as Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU), renamed Ondo State University (OSUA) 1985, University of Ado-Ekiti 1999, acquired current title 2011 following merger with the University of Education, Ikere-Ekiti and University of Science and Technology, Ifaki-Ekiti
Academic Year
October to July (October-March; April-July)
Admission Requirements
Senior School Certificate (SSC) or General Certificate of Education (GCE) 'O' levels, with credits in 5 relevant subjects, obtained at no more than 2 sittings; and University Matriculation Examination (UME). Some courses have additional requirements. International applicants: equivalent qualifications and UME
