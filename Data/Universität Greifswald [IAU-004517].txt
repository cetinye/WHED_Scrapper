History
Founded 1456 and confirmed by Papal Bull of Calixtus III. The University came under the control of Sweden in 1648, becoming a Prussian University in 1815. Reorganized 1946 and 1990. In 2017, the University of Greifswald's Senate decides to change the official German name of the University Ernst-Moritz-Arndt-Universität Greifswald (Ernst-Moritz-Arndt University Greifswald) for Universität Greifswald (University of Greifswald)
