History
Created in 1980 as an affiliated Engineering Institution with Andhra University. Upgraded to a Deemed to be University in 2007 by the UGC. Off-site campuses were later established (Hyderabad, Telangana State in 2009; Bengaluru, Karnataka State in 2012; and GIMSR, Visakhapatnam in 2015.)
