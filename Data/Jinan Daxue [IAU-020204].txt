History
Founded 1948 as Shandong Insitute of Building Materials. Combined with Jinan Normal College and Jinan Vocational College 1998. Acquired present title following incorporation of the Jinan School of Civil Affairs and the Shandong School of Materials and Supplies 2001. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
