History
Founded as a private institution 1961 with the support of various local and municipal authorities. Received State recognition 1965. Under the supervision of the Ministry of Education. Financed by an Inter-provincial Consortium consisting of the local and municipal authorities of Pescara, Chieti, and Teramo
