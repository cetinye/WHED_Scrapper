History
Founded 1980, Universidad de Occidente (UDO) was an autonomous institution under the jurisdiction of the State of Sinaloa and financially supported by the Centro de Estudios Superiores de Occidente. It acquired its autonomy on February 22, 2018, through Decree Number 376 of the H. Congress of the State of Sinaloa and change its name for the Universidad Autónoma de Occidente (UAdeO)
