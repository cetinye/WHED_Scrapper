History
Founded 1946, the first State theatre academy created after World War II by the Ministry of Culture and Art, incorporating three Drama Workshops. Became Państwowa Wyższa Szkoła Teatralna im. Ludwika Solskiego (PWST), 1991. Acquired present status and title 2017.
