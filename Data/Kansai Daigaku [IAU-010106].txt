History
Founded 1886 as Kansai Law School, became Kansai University 1905. In 1922 the main campus was removed to its current site in Suita, a suburb of Osaka. The Takatsuki Campus was set up in 1994, followed by the Takatsuki Muse Campus and Sakai Campus in 2010. Minami-Senri International Plaza was established in 2012, and in 2016 the Umeda Campus was established
