History
Founded 1958. Focusing on research and lifelong learning with an endeavor to include the entire population in the process of learning, Anadolu University embraces the world. Located in Eskişehir, which is well known as city of science, culture and youth, Anadolu University Yunus Emre Campus, houses 12 faculties (3 of them offer distance education), 6 graduate schools, 2 vocational schools, 3 schools, 30 research centers and 55 student clubs
