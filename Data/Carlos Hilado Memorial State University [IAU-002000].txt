History
Founded 1954 as the Negros Occidental School of Arts and Trade, became Bacolod City National Trade School in 1968, Negros Occidental Provincial Community College in 1970, Paglaum State College in 1983, Carlos Hilado Memorial State College in 1994. Acquired current title and stats 2019.
