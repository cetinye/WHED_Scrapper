History
Founded 1920 as a private institution on the initiative of the Finnish University Society. Inaugurated 1922. Became State institution 1974 under the supervision of the Ministry of Education. Merged with Turku School of Economics in 2010. A Faculty of Engineering is established on January 1, 2021. Turku School of Economics is AACSB accredited. The University of Turku is part of the Seinäjoki University Consortium (UCS) which brings together University of Tampere, Helsinki University of the Arts - Sibelius Academy, University of Vaasa, the University Association of South Ostrobothnia, the Natural Resources Institute Finland and the Migration Institute of Finland. The Seinäjoki University Consortium is one of six university consortia in Finland and is coordinate by Tampere University
