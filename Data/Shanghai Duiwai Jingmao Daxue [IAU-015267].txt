History
Founded 1960 as Shanghai Institute of Foreign Trade (SIFT) following the merger of the International Business and Foreign Languages Department of Shanghai Foreign Languages Institute, Shanghai School of Customs and Shanghai School for Cadres. Incorporated Twice into Shanghai Foreign Languages Institute. Restored 1978. Acquired present title and status 2013. Main administrative body: Shanghai Municipality
