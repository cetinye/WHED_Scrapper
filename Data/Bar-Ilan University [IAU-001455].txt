History
Founded 1955. The University combines secular and religious studies and is recognized by the Ministry of Education. The State provides 60% of the budget. Regional Colleges: Ashkelon; Safed; Western Galilee (Yad Natan); Jordan Valley (Tzemach); Yehuda and Shomron (Ariel)
