History
Founded 1978. Ceased offering university-level degrees 1983. Upraded again to university-level institution and renamed Chengdu College 2003. Incorporated Chengdu Education College, Chengdu Health School, Chengdu Pre-School Education Teachers School 2006. Acquired present title 2018. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
