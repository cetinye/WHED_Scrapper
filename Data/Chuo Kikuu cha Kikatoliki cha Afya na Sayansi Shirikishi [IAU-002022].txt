History
Founded 2003 as Bugando University College of Health Sciences (BUCHS), a constituent college of St. Augustine University of Tanzania (Mwanza). Acquired present status and title 2011. The second university of the Tanzania Episcopal Conference (TEC)
Academic Year

Admission Requirements
Entrance Requirements for Doctor of Medicine (MD) course: Principal Pass at C grade or higher in Chemistry, Biology or Physics, provided that the other two subjects are not below D at Advanced (“A") level. Preference will be given to applicants with “C” Passes or above in Chemistry or Biology, in that order; Or holders of B.Sc. Degrees, from recognized institutions, majoring in Biology or Chemistry or holders of Diploma in Clinical Medicine with principal passes at any level in Chemistry, Biology or Physics, in the advanced certificate examinations
