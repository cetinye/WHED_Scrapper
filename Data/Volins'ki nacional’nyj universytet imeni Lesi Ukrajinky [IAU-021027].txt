History
Founded 1940 as Lutsk Teacher's Institute, transformed into Volins'kij Deržavnij Universitet im. Leci Ukrainki (Lesya Ukrainka Volyn State University) 1993. Became Lesya Ukrainka Eastern European National University (Sxidnojevropejs’kyj nacional’nyj universytet imeni Lesi Ukrajinky) in 2012 and acquired current title 2020
