History
Founded as Riga Medical Institute in 1950. Became Latvian Academy of Medicine in 1990 and acquired present status and title in 2002. In 2024, the Latvian Academy of Sport Education (Latvijas Sporta pedagogijas akadēmija - IAU-010865) was integrated into the university.
