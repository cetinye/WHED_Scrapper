History
Founded 1980 as Anambra State University of Technology (ASUTECH). Merged with Institute of Management and Technology (IMT) 1985. Acquired current title 1991, while Awka and Nnewi campuses became the nucleus of the new Anambra State University, which was later to become a Federal University, the Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka. Mmoved to its permanent site at Agbani 2006
