History
Founded 1909 as Engineering Department of Tomsk Technological Institute, reorganized 1930 as branch of Siberian Civil Engineering Institute and 1989 as branch of Novosibirsk Architectural Institute (NAHRI). Became Novosibirskaja Gosudarstvennaja Arkhitekturno-Khudožestvennaja Akademija (Novosibirsk State Academy of Architecture and Fine Arts) in 1996, became Novosibirskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Arkhitektury, Dizajna i Iskusstv (Novosibirsk State University of Architecture, Design and Fine Arts) in 2015 and acquired current title 2019
