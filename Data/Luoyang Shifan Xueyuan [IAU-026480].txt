History
Founded 1916 as Heluo Normal School. Successively became Henan Provincial Heluo Normal School, Henan Provincial 4th Normal School, Henan Provincial Luoyang Normal School, Luoyang Normal Postsecondary Training School, Luoyang Normal Junior College. Acquired present status and title 2000. Main administrative body: Henan Province
