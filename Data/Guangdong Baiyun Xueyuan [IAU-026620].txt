History
Founded 1989 as Guangzhou Baiyun Applied Technology School. Became Baiyun Vocational Technical College 1999. Renamed as Guangdong Baiyun Vocational Technical College 2001. Upgraded, acquired present title 2005. Started granting Bachelor's degrees 2009. Main administrative body: Guangdong Provincial Education Department
