History
Founded 1945. From Huainan Coal Mine Industry Training School was upgraded to College and renamed as Hefei Mining Industry College 1955. Merged with Huainan Coal Mine School and renamed as Huainan College of Coal 1971. Incorporated East China Coal Medical Training School, Huainan School of Chemical Industry. Acquired present name 2002. Main administrative authority: Anhui Province
