History
Founded 1966 as a branch of the Leningrad North-West Correspondence Institute. Became independent Vologda Polytechnic Institute 1975. Became Vologda State Technical University (Vologodskij Gosudarstvennyj Tehničeskij Universitet) in 1999. Acquired current title in 2013, and merged with Vologodskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet (Vologda State Pedagogical University) in 2017
