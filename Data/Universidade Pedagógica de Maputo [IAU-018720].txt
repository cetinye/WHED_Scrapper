History
Founded 1985 as Instituto Superior Pedagógico (ISP). Became Universidade Pedagógica (UP) 1995. Acquired current title 2019 following restructuration that lead to the separation with all its regional branches, transformed in four new universities: Universidade Save (Uni-Save), Universidade Púnguè (Uni-Púnguè), Universidade Licungo (UniLicungo) and Universidade Rovuma (Uni-Rovuma)
