History
Founded 1916 as a medical department of Perm State University, became an independent institution in 1931 when Permskij Meditsinskij Institut (Perm Medical Institute) was created. It became Permskaja Gosudarstvennaja Meditsinskaja Akademija (Perm State Academy of Medicine) in 1994 and acquired current title and status 2014
