History
Founded 1866 as New Hampshire College of Agriculture and Mechanic Arts. Acquired present title charter 1923. The largest institution of the University System of New Hampshire. Integrated the Granite State College (IAU-006767) 2023, which became its College of Professional Studies as well as the so far affiliated-only University of New Hampshire School of Law (IAU-020332) - now the Franklin Pierce School of Law - the same year
Academic Year
September to May (September-December; January-May). Also Summer Session (June-August)
Admission Requirements
Graduation from high school or equivalent, and College Board SAT or ACT examination. TOEFL test for foreign students (550 minimum)
