History
Founded 1945 as Women's Teacher Training College. Became Kyrgyz Women's Teacher Training Institute 1950, Kirghiz Women's Pedagogical Institute 1952, Kyrgyz State Pedagogical Institute 1992 and Kyrgyz State Pedagogical University 1994. Acquired present status and title 2005, named after Išenaly Arabaev.
