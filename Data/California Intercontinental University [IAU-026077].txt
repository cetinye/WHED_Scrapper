Academic Year
Each academic year lasts two semesters with the start of the academic year for each student is determined by the student’s original start date. Any break in enrollment will result in the student starting a new academic year.
Admission Requirements
Applicants for the Associate or Bachelor’s degree program must submit and meet the following admission prerequisites:
high school diploma or GED, or its recognized equivalent;
DD-214 document showing high school completion may be accepted as unofficial proof of high school graduation for military and veteran students;
Official transcript from a college/university documenting proof of high school graduation, including high school name and date of graduation;
For bachelor’s degree applicants only: official transcript from a college/university documenting proof of completed associate degree;
If the applicant has completed some approved postsecondary college work, a sealed copy of his or her official transcripts may be required.
