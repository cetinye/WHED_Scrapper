History
Founded 1861 as the Oswego Primary Teachers’ Training School, renamed Oswego Normal School 1913 and Oswego State Teachers College, became a charter member of the State University of New York (SUNY) 1948, renamed State University of New York College of Education at Oswego 1960, acquired current title 1962

September to May (September-December; January-May). Also Summer Session (June-August)
