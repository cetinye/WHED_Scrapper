History
Founded 1958 as Baoji University. Baoji Teachers College 1978. The school closed down 1963. Reopened as Shaanxi Normal University Baoji branch 1975. Became Baoji Normal University 1978. Merged with the new Baoji University (1984), acquired present title 1992. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
