History
Founded 1937 in Yan'an by the Communist Party as Northern Shaanxi Public School. Successively became Yan'an University, Northwest People Revolution University, Northwest School of Politics and Law for Cadres, Central School of Politics and Law for Cadres Northwest branch, Yan'an College of Politics and Law 1958. Acquired present title 2006. Under the jurisdiction of Shaanxi Province
