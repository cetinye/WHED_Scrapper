History
Founded in 1959 as Kabianga Farmers Training Centre. Became Kabianga Campus of Moi University in 2007, was upgraded into Kabianga University College (a constituent college of Moi University) in 2009 and received current title and status in 2013 when it was chartered as the University of Kabianga.
