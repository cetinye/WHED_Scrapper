History
Founded 1852 as Agricultural Institute in Pantelimon. Reorganized 1867 as Central School of Agriculture and Silviculture in 1867. Became the Agricultural School of Herăstrău 1893. Reorganized several times and became Agricultural Institute of Bucharest 1948. Named after Nicolae Bălcescu in 1952, acquiring present title in 1995. A state institution
