History
Founded in 2016. The group of Engineering Schools of Lille Catholic University - HEI (Hautes études d'ingénieur), ISA and ISEN (Institut supérieur de l'électronique et du numérique), merged under the name Yncréa Hauts-de-France. In 2020, Yncréa Hauts-de-France is renamed Junia. Partnership with Catholic University of Lille
