History
Founded as Diplomatic Academy of Ukraine at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine (DAU) 1995, reformed and reorganized into a professional development and research institution of postgraduate training and acquired current title 2016. A school of professional diplomacy, a training center for professional development of diplomats and civil servants in charge of international relations and foreign affairs in central executive bodies
