History
Founded as Cork Regional Technical College in 1974. Became Cork Institute of Technology (Institiúid Teicneolaíochta Chorcaí) (CIT) in 1997 and acquired current title and status in January 2021 following a merger with Institute of Technology Tralee (Institúid Teicneolaíochta Trá Lí) (IT Tralee) located in Kerry
