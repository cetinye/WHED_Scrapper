History
Created 1930 as Frunze Cooperative Technical School, which later became Frunze Cooperative College. After the establishment of Kyrgyz Republic in 1991, Frunze Cooperative College became Bishkek Commercial and Economic College which was upgraded Kyrgyz Academy of Cooperation in 1997. Acquired current title and status in the year 2000.
