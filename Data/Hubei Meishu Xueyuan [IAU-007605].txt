History
Founded 1920 as Wuchang School of Fine Arts. Successively became Wuchang Technical School of Fine Arts (1923), Wuchang Private Vocational College of Arts (1930), Zhongyuan University College of Arts and Literature (1949), the Fine Arts Department of Hubei College of Arts (1958), the Fine Arts Department of Hubei Vocational College of Arts (1972), the Fine Arts Subsection of the Hubei College of Arts (1978). Acquired present status and title 1985. Main administrative body: Hubei Province
