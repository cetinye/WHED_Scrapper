History
WIUU was founded in 1996 as 1997 as Ukrainian-American Liberal Arts Institute Wisconsin International University Ukraine, a branch of the Wisconsin International University (International Division of the Lutheran Concordia University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee), and had the name of the Wisconsin International University (WIU) in Ukraine. In 1997, on the recommendation of the Ministry of Education Ukraine WIU was transformed into a joint Ukrainian-American Humanitarian (Liberal Arts) Institute with the Ukrainian co-founder – the National Pedagogical University named by Mykhaylo Dragomanov (Ukraine, Kyiv) Acquired current title 2017
