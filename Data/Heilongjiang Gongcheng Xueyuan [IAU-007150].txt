History
Founded 1952 as the Northeast River Transportation Institute. Became Heilongjiang Transportation Institute 1958. It was renamed as Heilongjiang Higher Vocational Institute of Transportation 1994. Merged with Harbin Higher Vocational Institute of Technology and acquired present title 2000. Main administrative body: Heilongjiang Province
