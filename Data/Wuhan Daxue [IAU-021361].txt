History
Founded 1893 as Ziqiang Institute. Became National Wuhan University 1928. Acquired present status and title 2000, following merger with Wuhan University of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, Wuhan Technical University of Surveying and Mapping, and Hubei Medical University. Part of "211 Project " and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
