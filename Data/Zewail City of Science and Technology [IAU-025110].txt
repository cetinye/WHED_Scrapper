Admission Requirements
Applicants must be holders of the Egyptian secondary school general certificate (Thanaweya Amma) or approved equivalent certificate. The minimum requirement for the application is an overall high school percentage of 90% in the year 2018. The final acceptance to join the university is based on achieving the highest high school scores, in addition to achieving the highest scores in the admission's exam, the English exam, and the interview
