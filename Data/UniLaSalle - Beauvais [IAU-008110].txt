History
Founded 1854 as Institut Normal Agricole. Became Institut polytechnique LaSalle Beauvais in 2006 following the merger of Institut Géologique Albert-de-Lapparent (IGAL) and Institut Supérieur d’Agriculture de Beauvais (ISAB). Associated with the Institut catholique de Paris. Merged with ESTIPA in January 2016 and became UniLaSalle. Merged with Ecole des Métiers de l’Environnement (EME) - Rennes in 2018. Became in 2016 "Etablissement d'enseignement supérieur prive d'intérêt général (EESPIG)"
