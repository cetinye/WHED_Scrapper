History
The Institut polytechnique des sciences avancées replaced the Polytechnic Institute of Applied Sciences founded in 1961 and is located in Ivry-sur-Seine as well as in Lyon and Toulouse.The school is a member of the Conference of Directors of French Engineering Schools (CDEFI), the Union of Independent Grandes Ecoles (UGEI) and belongs to the IONIS Education group
