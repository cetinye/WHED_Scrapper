Admission Requirements
Undergraduate Studies: for enrolment in the 1st year, Matura, vocational matura or final exam (4-year secondary school programme); For enrolment in the 2nd year, degree from a vocational higher education programme or graduation from other vocational higher education programmes.
Postgraduate Studies: for enrolment in 1st year, 1st cycle degrees - vocational higher education programmes (180 ECTS credits) related to economics, business, organisation and administrative sciences, or social and legal studies; if other fields an additional exam is required; for enrolment in the 2nd year, 1st cycle university programmes (4 years and 240 ECTS) related to economics, business, organisation and administrative sciences, or social and legal studies; if other field the redaction of a seminar paper for Research Methodology or Company Strategy and Development is required.
Doctoral study programme: Master's degree (Bologna model), or a scientific Master's degree, or a specialisation degree, or a university study programme worth a minimum of 240 ECTS credits plus an interview, or a specialisation degree (Bologna model) plus an interview if not Business/economics-related field, or 4-year university study programme worth a minimum of 240 credits and successful completetion of an MBA at a certified institution plus at least 5 years of work, or a study programme under paragraph 4 Article 36 of the Higher Education Act, worth at least 300 ECTS credits
