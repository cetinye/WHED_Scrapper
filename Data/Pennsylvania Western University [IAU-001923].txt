History
Founded 1852 as Academy, acquired present status 1865 and renamed California University of Pennsylvania 1983. Merged with Clarion University of Pennsylvania and Edinboro University of Pennsylvania to form Pennsylvania Western University 2022. A member of Pennsylvania's State System of Higher Education (PASSHE)
Academic Year
September to May. Also Summer Session (June-August)
Admission Requirements
Graduation from high school or equivalent, and College Board SAT or ACT examination. TOEFL test for foreign students
