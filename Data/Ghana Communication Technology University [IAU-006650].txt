History
Founded as training centre by the Post and Telecommunications Department, 1948. Formerly known as Ghana Telecom Training Centre (GTTC) then as Ghana Telecom University College. Became Ghana Technology University College, 2014 . Acquired present title and became fully-fledged public university since 2020.
