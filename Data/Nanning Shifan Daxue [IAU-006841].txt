History
Founded 1953 as Guangxi Secondary Teacher Training College and renamed Guangxi Teachers Training College the same year. Successively became Nanning Normal College 1960, incorporated Guangxi University for Nationalities, Guangxi Teachers Training College 1961, Guangxi Education College 1966. Upgraded to university-level college 1978. Successively changed to Nanning Normal College 1979, Guangxi Teachers Education University 1985. Incorporated Nanning Normal School for Nationalities (founded 1905) 2002. Acquired present title 2018. Under the jurisdiction of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region
