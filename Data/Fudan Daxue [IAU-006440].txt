History
Founded 1905 as Fudan Public School. Became Private School 1913-1936. Moved to Chongqing 1937. Changed to Fudan State-run University 1942. Merged with Shanghai Medical University 2000. Part of "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
