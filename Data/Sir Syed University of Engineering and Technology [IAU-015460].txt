History
Created in 1993, Sir Syed University of Engineering and Technology is spiritually associated to Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, India. The role of the Muslim University Aligarh, in the process of uplifting and the wellbeing of the Muslims of the subcontinent, stands in bold relief in the annals of history. The domain of working of this university was not confined to teaching of some subjects or dissemination of knowledge; it rather transcended these spheres and encompassed the building of character and development of personality of the students along with academics. This university works in accordance with a philosophy that was the driving force for Aligarh Movement. This philosophy was Initiated by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan and developed through relentless work by his associates and followers.
