History
Founded 1923 as Chishui Vocational School. Successively became Weinan Normal College, Shaanxi Normal University Weinan Training Section, Weinan Normal Vocational College. Merged with Weinan Education College (founded 1983), acquired present status and title 2000. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
