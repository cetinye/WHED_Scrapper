History
Founded 1993 as Centre universitaire du Nord-Kivu à Goma (CUNK/GOMA), became Centre universitaire extension de Goma (CUEG) in 1996 and operated under the supervision of the University of Kisangani. Acquired current title and status in 2005 via ministerial order N° 055/MINESU/RS/2005 of 15 Octobre 2005, creating an autonomous public institution
