History
Created 1981 as Ivanovo Institute of Civil Engineering (Ivanovskij Inženerno-Stroitel'nyj Institut). In 2005 became Ivanovo State Universoty of Architecture and Civil Engineering (Ivanovskij Gosudarstevennyj Arkhitekturno-Stroitel'nyj Universitet) and acquired current title and status in 2012 following merger with Ivanovo State Textile Academy (Ivanovskaja Gosudarstvennaja Tekstil'naja Akademija, created 1918)
