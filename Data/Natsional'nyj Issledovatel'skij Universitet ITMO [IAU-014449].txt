History
Founded 1900 as St. Petersburg School of Craft of the Crown Prince Nicholas, and a mechanical-optical and watch department was opened, the only educational institution in the country that trained experts in the field of precision mechanics and optics. Became Sankt-Peterburgskij Institut Točnoj Mekhaniki i Optiki (Saint Petersburg Institute of Precision Mechanics and Optics) in 1992, and became a university in 1994.
In 2003, it became Sankt-Peterburgskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Informatsionnykh Tekhnologij, Mekhaniki i Optiki (Saint-Petersburg State University of Information Technology, Mechanics and Optics), and acquired current title and status in 2019
