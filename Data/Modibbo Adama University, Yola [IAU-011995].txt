History
Founded 1981 as Federal University of Technology, Yola (FUT Yola). First set of 214 students admitted 1982. Merged with the University of Maiduguri and became Modibbo Adama College Campus of University of Maiduguri 1984. Separated and granted full autonomy with the name reverted to Federal University of Technology, Yola (FUT Yola) 1988. Renamed Modibbo Adama University of Technology, Yola (MAUTECH, Yola) 2011. Changed status from technology-based University to conventional one and acquired current title 2021
