History
Founded as University 1946 incorporating 3 existing Institutions (Teachers, 1923; Medicine, 1933; Agriculture, 1944) and 2 newly established Colleges (Liberal Arts and Sciences). Main Campus at Sankyuk-dong and Medical Campus at Tongin-dong. Under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Education. Merged with Sangju National University 2008.
