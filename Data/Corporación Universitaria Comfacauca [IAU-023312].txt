History
Founded on April 2, 2001, date on which, the Ministry of National Education (MEN) gives life to the Institution, through Resolution 597 in which it grants its legal status, as the Instituto Tecnológico de Educación Superior de Comfacauca - ITC. In 2004 and 2005, taking into account its valuable results, the Superior Council of Comfacauca, summoned the directive, administrative and academic personnel in order to carry out the management that will allow to continue advancing as a training bet of high level. After the entire process has been completed, the MEN granted by Resolution 8210 of 14 November 2008, the change of character to the University Institution, recognizing it from the moment as the Institución Universitaria Tecnológica de Comfacauca.
With this name, began in 2009, the training process at the level university professional with the academic programs of Systems Engineering and Public accounting. Between 2011 and 2012, the new university programs were created in Social Communication and Journalism, Mechatronics Engineering and Industrial Engineering, decentralizing some programs from external call projects academics to the municipalities of Patía and Miranda in the department of Cauca in the technical and technological levels.
In the framework of this growth, it was necessary to make an adjustment to the organizational structure that would allow the University Institution
project itself at the regional, national and international levels, for which the Council Superior authorized a reform to the statutes, which the MEN ratified through the Resolution 1809 of February 14, 2014 where the name is adjusted to CORPORACIÓN UNIVERSITARIA COMFACAUCA, in its acronym
UNICOMFACAUCA. During the following year, 7 new years were approved Undergraduate programs: Law and Business Administration, as well as its
first own program at postgraduate level: a Specialization called Information and Communication Technologies in Education. Thanks to the results derived from continuous improvement and practice permanent quality, the MEN awarded Unicomfacauca in 2018 the High Quality Accreditation to three of its academic programs: Technology in Gastronomic Management, Agro-Environmental Technology and Systems Engineering.
In 2019, the qualified registration of the Specialization program in Supply Chain and Logistics Management. And consolidating this level of training during 2020, two new records of training programs were obtained Specialization: Intelligent Systems Applied to the Internet of Things and Systems of Information in Management and Control of Organizational Costs.
During the second semester of 2020, he received an appraisal visit from initial conditions by the National Accreditation Council -CNA-, for the High Quality Accreditation at the institutional level.
