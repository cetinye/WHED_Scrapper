History
Founded 1952 as Hankou Aeronautics Industry School. Relocated to Nanchang 1954, became successively Nanchang Industrial School (1955), Nanchang Aeronautics Industry Vocational College (1956), Nanchang College of Aeronautics Industry (1978). Acquired present status and title 2007. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Province
