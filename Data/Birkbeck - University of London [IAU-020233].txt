History
Founded 1823 as London Mechanics Institution. Acquired present status 1920. Most courses are devised to be followed throughout by part-time study after work; most postgraduate taught courses can be taken full-time as well as part-time. Birkbeck is a member institution of the federal University of London
Academic Year
September to July (September-December; January-March; April-July)
Admission Requirements
Overseas students admitted to full-time postgraduate courses must hold good first degree or equivalent, and the following minimum scores in English Language: IELTS: 7.0; TOEFL: 550; AEB Test in English for Educational Purposes: 4
