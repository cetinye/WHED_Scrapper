History
Founded 1967, previously established 1900 as 'Studium Generale' of the Order of Friars Preachers in Canada. A private university institution incorporated in the Province of Ontario since 1909. Previously known as the Collège Dominicain de Philosophie et de Théologie (Dominican College of Philosophy and Theology).
