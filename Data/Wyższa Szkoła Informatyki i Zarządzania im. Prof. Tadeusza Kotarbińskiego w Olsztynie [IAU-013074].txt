History
Founded 1990 as Olsztyn School of Management and Administration. Acquired university status and became known as Kotarbinski University of Management in Olsztyn, 1996. Formerly known as Olsztyńska Wyższa Szkoła Informatyki i Zarządzania im. Prof. Tadeusza Kotarbińskiego. Acquired present title 2001.
