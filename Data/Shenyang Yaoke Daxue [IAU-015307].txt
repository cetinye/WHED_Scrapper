History
Founded 1931 as the pharmaceutical section of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army Nursing School based in the Jiangxi Communist Base (soviet area). Renamed Shenyang Pharmaceutical College following the merger of the Zhejiang Medical and Pharmaceutical Departement, the Shenyang School of Pharmaceutical Industry and the Pharmaceutical Departement of Shandong Medical College.Acquired present status and title1994. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
