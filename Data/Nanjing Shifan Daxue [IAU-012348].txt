History
Founded 1902 with the establishment of Sanjiang Normal College. Evolved into Nanjing University. The other origin of Nanjing Normal University is Jinling University which was established in 1888 and it developed into Private Ginling University in 1910. Integrated to National Ginling University. Some schools and departments of Nanjing University and Ginling University were separated and merged into Nanjing Normal College 1952. Acquired present title University 1984. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
