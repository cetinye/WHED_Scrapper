Hold a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in one of the following subjects: mechanics, physics, civil engineering or mathematics. Competitive entrance examination. A Master’s degree or equivalent in: mathematics, applied mathematics, physics, mechanics, civil engineering or life and earth sciences is required for admission to the 2nd year
