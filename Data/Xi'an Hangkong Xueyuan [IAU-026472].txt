History
Founded 1955 as Xi'an Aeronautical Industry School. Incorporated Lanzhou Aeronautical Industry School 1957. Successively became Xi'an Aeronautical Industry Training School 1960, Xi'an Aeronautical Industry and Technology Training School 1985, Xi'an Junior College of Aeronautical Technology 1993. Acquired present status and title 2012. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
