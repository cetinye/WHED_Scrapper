History
Founded 1866 as St. Teresa’s Academy by Sisters of St. Joseph of Carondelet, became St. Teresa’s College, a two-year college for women 1916, became affiliated with St. Joseph Hospital’s School of Nursing 1930, renamed College of St. Teresa 1940 and then Avila College 1963, became fully coeducational 1969, acquired current status and title 2002
