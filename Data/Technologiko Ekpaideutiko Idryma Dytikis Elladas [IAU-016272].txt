Established 2013 following the merger between the former T.E.I. of Patras (founded 1983) and former T.E.I. of Mesolonghi (1983). An autonomous institution under the supervision of the Institute of Technological Education. Formerly Technological Educational Institute of Western Greece/T.E.I. of Patras-Technologiko Ekpaideutiko Idrima Patras.
