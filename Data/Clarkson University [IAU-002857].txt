History
Founded 1896 as Thomas S. Clarkson Memorial School of Technology, acquired present status and title 1984. Absorbed the Union Graduate College 2016, which became the Clarkson University Capital Region Campus which serves as an integrated hub for graduate student recruitment and admissions for graduate programmes
