History
Created 1907 as normal school. Successively became Yunnan Provincial Third Normal School 1912, Qujing Normal School 1956, Kunming Normal College Qujing Class 1977, Qujing Normal Training School 1978, Qujing Normal Junior College 1993. Merged with Qujing Education College (founded 1984) and Qujing Normal School (founded 1980), acquired present title 2000. Under the administration of Yunnan Province
