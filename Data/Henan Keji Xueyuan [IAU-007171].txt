History
Founded 1939 as the Biology Department of the Yan'an College of Natural Sciences. Successively known as Beifang University Agricultural College, Huabei University Agricultural College, Pingyuan Agricultural College, Baiquan Agricultural Junior College and Henan Normal Institute of Vocational Technology. Acquired present title 2004. Main administrative body: Henan Province
