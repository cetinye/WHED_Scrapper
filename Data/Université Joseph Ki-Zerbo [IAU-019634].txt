History
Founded 1965 as Institut supérieur de formation pédagogique (ISFP), became Centre d’Enseignement Supérieur de Ouagadougou (CESUP) 1969. Became Université de Ouagadougou 1974, and underwent several reorganizations in 1985, 1991 and 1997. Acquired current title 2015
