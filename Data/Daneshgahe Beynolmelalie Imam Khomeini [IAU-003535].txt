History
Imam Khomeini International University (IKIU), was founded in 1991 as a state university with the purpose of promoting science and culture at international level, so far the university has been hosting lots of guests from over 100 countries worldwide. Based on its International missions the University along promoting the higher education programs, provide an appropriate scientific dynamic learning environment for students and researchers and try to train experts and specialists, parallel with emission of sciences, techniques and Islamic educations at international level. IKIU is located in Qazvin where is at 135 km northwest of Tehran, one of the most important crossing point in the country.
