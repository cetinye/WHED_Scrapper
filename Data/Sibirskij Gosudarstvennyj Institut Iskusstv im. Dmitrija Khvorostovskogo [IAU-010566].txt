History
Created 1977 as Krasnoyarsk Institute of Arts (Krasnojarskiy Institut Iskusstv), became Krasnojarsk State Academy of Music and Arts (Krasnojarskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Muzyki i Teatra) in 2000, Krasnojarskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv (Krasnojarsk State Institute of Arts) in 2015 and acquired current title in 2018
