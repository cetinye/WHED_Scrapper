History
Founded 1985 as Kangding Teachers Vocational College for Nationalities. Became Kangding Junior College for Nationalities 1992. Incorporated Ganzizhou Agriculture School, Ganzizhou School of Finance and Economics 2004. Acquired present title and status 2009. Formerly known as Sichuan University for Nationalities in english. Under the administration of the Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture
