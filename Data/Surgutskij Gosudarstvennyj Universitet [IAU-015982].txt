History
Founded in 1993. Became University of Khanty-Maniisky Autonomous Okrug in March 1995. Acquired current title and status 1995. Was also known as Surgutskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Khanty-Mansijskogo avtonomnogo okruga - Yugry (Surgut State University of the Khanty-Mansijsk Autonomous Okrug)
