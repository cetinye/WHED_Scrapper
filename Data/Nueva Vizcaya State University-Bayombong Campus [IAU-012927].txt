History
Nueva Vizcaya State University was created in 2004 out of a merger between Nueva Vizcaya State Institute of Technology (created 1916) and Nueva Vizcaya State Polytechnic College (created 1946).

(Undergraduate) : High School Diploma and entrance exam; (Postgraduate) : Bachelor's degree or equivalent with a GPA of at least 2.00 for Master's programme; Master's degree or equivalent with a GPA of at least 1.75 for Doctorate programmes
