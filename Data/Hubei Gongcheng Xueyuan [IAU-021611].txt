History
Created 1943 as Hubei Provincial 3rd Teachers School. Successively renamed as Xiaogan Teachers School, Xiaogan University, Xiaogan branch of Wuhan Teachers College, Xiaogan Teachers Training School, Xiaogan Teachers Junior College. Merged with Hubei Normal Vocational School of Technology 1986. Incorporated Xiaogan Rural and Town Architectural Engineering 1999. Became Xiaogan College ( Xiaogan University) 2000. Acquired present title 2011. Main administrative body: Hubei Province
