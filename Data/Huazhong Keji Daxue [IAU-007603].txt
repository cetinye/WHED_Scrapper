History
Founded 1952 as Huazhong Institute of Technology. Became Huazhong University of Science and Technology 1988. Merged with Tongji Medical University (founded 1907), Wuhan Urban Construction Institute (founded 1952) to form the new HUST 2000. Part of "211 Project" universities. Directly supervised by the Ministry of Education
