History
Founded 1938 as branch of Hubei United Rural Teaching School. Successively became Hubei Provincial 7th Teaching School, Enshi Teaching School (1950), Enshi Normal Vocational College (1978). Became Enshi University 1984. Renamed as Hubei Minzu College 1989. Merged with Enshi Medical Junior College (founded 1958), acquired present status 1998. Acquired present title 2018. Main administrative bdy: Enshi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture
