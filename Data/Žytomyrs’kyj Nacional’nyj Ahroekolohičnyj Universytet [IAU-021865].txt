History
Founded 1922 as Volyn Agronomic Technical School, became State Academy of Agriculture and Ecology of Ukraine in 1994. Acquired current title and status 2001. A multi-campus state University that educates students from all Ukraine, and supports individuals and communities through integrated programs of teaching, research, and service
