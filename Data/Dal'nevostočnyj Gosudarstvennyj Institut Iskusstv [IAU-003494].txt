History
Founded 1962 as Dal'nevostočnyj Pedagogičeskij Institut Iskusstv (Far Eastern Pedagogical Institute for Art), became Dal'nevostočnyj Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv (Far Eastern State Institute of Art) in 1992, then Dal'nevostočnaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Iskusstv (Far Eastern State Academy of Art) in 2000 before being once again named Dal'nevostočnyj Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv (Far Eastern State Institute of Art)
