History
Founded 1939 as Kolomenskij Pedagogičeskij Tekhnikum (Kolomna Pedagogical College), became Kolomenskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Institut (Kolomna State Pedagogical Institute) in 1953, Moscow State Regional Socio-Humanitarian Institute (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Oblastnoj Sotsial'no-Gumanitarnyj Institut) in 2010 and acquired current title in 2015 after merging with Zaraisk Pedagogical College and Moscow Regional College of Arts and Technology.
