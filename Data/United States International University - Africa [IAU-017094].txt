History
Founded 1969. Acquired status 1999. Merged with the California School of Professional Psychology (CSPP) forming the Alliant International University. USIU-Africa separated itself from the new entity, becoming an independent institution 2005, and receiving its own accreditation as United States International University, from the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) 2008. Acquired current title 2014.
