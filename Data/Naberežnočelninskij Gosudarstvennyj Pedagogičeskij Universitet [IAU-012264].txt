History
Created 1990 as Naberežn-o Čelninskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Institut (Naberezhnje Chelny State Pedagogical Institute), became Naberežnočelninskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Sotsial'no-Pedagogičeskikh Tekhnologij i Resursov (Naberezhnje Chelny Institute of Social and Educational Technology and Resources) in 2011 and acquired current title and status in 2015
