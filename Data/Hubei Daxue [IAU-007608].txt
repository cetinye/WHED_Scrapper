History
Founded 1931 as Hubei Provincial Institute of Education. Successively became National Hubei Teachers College, Hubei College of Education, Hubei Teachers Training College, Hubei Teachers Vocational College, Wuhan Teachers Vocational College, Wuhan Teachers College. Acquired present status and title 1984. Main administrative body: Hubei Province
