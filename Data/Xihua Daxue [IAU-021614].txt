History
Founded 1960 as Sichuan Institute of Agricutural Machinery. Renamed as Sichuan College of Technology 1983. Merged with Chengdu Teachers Junior College (founded 1971) and acquired present title 2003. Incorporated Sichuan Economics and Management College 2008. Main administration: Sichuan Province
