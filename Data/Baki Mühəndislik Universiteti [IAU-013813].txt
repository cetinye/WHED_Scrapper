History
Created 1993 as Qafqaz University. Acquired current title in 2016 and operates in a big campus located in Khirdalan City. BEU prepares engineers at all levels of higher education, executes programmes of higher and additional education in this sphere, and conducts fundamental and applied science researchers, as well as aims to improve the teaching of engineering technologies and to prepare highly-qualified personnel for the industry
