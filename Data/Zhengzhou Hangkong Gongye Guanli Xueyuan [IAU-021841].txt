History
Founded 1949 as Zhengzhou School of Aeronautical Industry; changed name to Zhengzhou Aviation Technical School 1964. Upgraded to College of Zhengzhou Institute of Aeronautical Industry Management 1978. Acquired present status and title 1984. One of the six colleges affiliated to the Ministry of Aviation Industry of the People's Republic of China. Main administrative body: Henan Province
