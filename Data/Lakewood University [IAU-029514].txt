History
Founded 1998 as the American Center for Conflict Resolution Institute (ACCRI), a for-profit organization offering mediation, arbitration, and continuing education services. Started offering mediation courses through distance education 2004. Converted into a non-profit organization that only provided distance education programmes 2005. Renamed Lakewood College 2009. Acquired current status and title 2020
