History
Founded 1906 as Changchun Teacher School. Became Changchun County Teacher College 1914. Became Changchun City Teacher College 1945. Upgraded to Changchun Vocational Teacher College 1958. Became Changchun Teacher College 1981. Acquired present title and status 2013. Main administrative body: Jilin Province
