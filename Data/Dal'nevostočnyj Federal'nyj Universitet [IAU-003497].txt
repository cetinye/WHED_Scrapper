History
Founded 1899 as Institute of Oriental Studies, became Dal'nevostočnyj Gosudarstvennyj Universitet (Far Eastern National University) in 1920. Merged with Tihookeanskij Gosudarstvennyj Ekonomičeskij Universitet (Pacific State University of Economics). Acquired current title and status 2010. In 2011, Dal'nevostočnyj Gosudarstvennyj Tekhničeskij Universitet (Far Eastern State Technical University) was merged with the university to make up the Schools of Engineering and Natural Sciences
