History
Founded 1856 as Colegio Civil, became Instituto Juárez 1872. Acquired status of university and title in 1957. In 1970 the Scientific Research Institute of the UJED was created, with academic autonomy in its organic structure that allowed it to develop without any fundamental relationship with schools, faculties and other research institutes of the University itself
