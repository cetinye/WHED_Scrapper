History
Established in 1929 as Veterinary Institute in accordance with the resolution of Council of Peoples Commissars of RSFSR. This institute was the ﬁrst agricultural and the second higher educational institution in Kazakhstan. In 1933 the Institute was renamed into Almaty Zoo- Veterinary Institute. In 1996 a new institution - Kazakh State University of Agriculture was founded on the basis of two oldest higher education institutions of Kazakhstan – Almaty Zoo-Veterinary Institute and Kazakh Institute of Agriculture. In 2001 the status of National University was awarded by the decree of the President of Kazakhstan.
