History
Founded 1870 as Agricultural School, became Faculty of Agriculture and Forestry within Poznań University 1919, detached as Higher School of Agriculture 1951. Became Agricultural University 1972 and August Cieszkowski Agricultural University as of 1996. Acquired present title 2008.
