History
Founded 1991 out of the Awka and Nnewi campuses of the then defunct Anambra State University of Technology (ASUTECH) - established 1980. Became a federal university 1992
Academic Year
November to August (November-March; April-August)
Admission Requirements
Five credits in West African School Certificate (WASC), Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) or General Certificate of Education (GCE) 'O' level, with minimum grade C and including English language. For direct entry, candidates must possess a higher qualification from an accredited university or polytechnic
