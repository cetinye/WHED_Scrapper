History
Founded 1930 as Nižegorodskij Institut Iniženerov Rečnogo Transporta (Nizhny Novgorod Institute of River Transport Engineers), became Volžskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Vodnogo Transporta (Volga State Academy of Water Transport) in 1993, and acquired current title and status 2015
