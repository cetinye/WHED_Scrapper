History
Founded 1940 with four Faculties, some of which were later reorganized or replaced by others in order to adapt the structure of the University to the particular needs of the country. Merged with Karel'skaja Gosudarstvennaja Pedagogičeskaja Akademija (Karelian State Pedagogical Academy) in 2013
