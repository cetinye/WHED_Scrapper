History
Created 1938 as Guizhou Provincial Bijie Normal School. Successively became Bijie Work and Study Normal School, Bijie Postsecondary Normal Training School, Bijie Normal Junior College 1993. Merged with Bijie Education College to form Bijie College 2005. Acquired present status and title 2014. Main administrative body: Guizhou Province
