History
Founded 1958 as Ningxia Medical College. Became Ningxia University Medicine Department 1962. Merged with Shanghai Railway and Medicine College and renamed Ningxia Medical College 1972. Incorporated Ningxia Health School, Ningxia Nursing School 2002. Acquired present title 2008. Incorporated Ningxia Normal College's Medical College 2014. Under the administration of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
