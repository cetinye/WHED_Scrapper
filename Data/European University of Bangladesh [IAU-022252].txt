Admission Requirements
• Applicants must have passed Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate HSC (or equivalent) examination in Science, Business Studies & Arts group with minimum 2nd division in both the examinations or five subjects in O-level and three major subjects (Math, Physics and Chemistry) in A-level education are required
•The students who have completed SSC and HSC under GPA system will have to have a minimum CGPA of 2.5 out of 5
•The O- and A-level students must have an average grade of B
