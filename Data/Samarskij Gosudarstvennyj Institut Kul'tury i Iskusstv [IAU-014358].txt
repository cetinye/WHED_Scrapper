History
Created 1971 as Kujbyshevskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kul'tury (Kuybyshev State Institute of Culture), became Samarskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv i Kul'tury (Samara State Institute of Art and Culture) in 1991, Samarskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury i Iskusstv (Samara State Academy of Culture and Arts) in 1996 and acquired current title and status 2014
