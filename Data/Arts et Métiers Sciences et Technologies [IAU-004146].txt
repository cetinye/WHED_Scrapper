History
Founded 1780, became Ecole nationale supérieure 1963, Grande Ecole 1966, reorganized 1990. Became "Ecole nationale supérieure d'Arts et Métiers - Paris Tech" (ENSAM). Acquired present title 2007. Member of the HESAM Université by decree n° 2019-638 of June 24, 2019. A Public Scientific, Cultural and Professional Establishment (EPSCP)
