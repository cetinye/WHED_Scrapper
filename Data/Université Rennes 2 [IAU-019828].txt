History
Founded 1969 under the 1968 law reforming higher education as one of the Universities replacing the former Université de Rennes (established in 1461 as University of Nantes with Colleges and Faculties in Nantes and Rennes. Member of the ComUE inter-régionale Université Bretagne Loire (Community of Universities and Higher Education institutions)
