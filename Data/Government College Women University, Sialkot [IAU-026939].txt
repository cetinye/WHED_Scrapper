History
Government College Women University Sialkot history dates back to 1951 when it was established as Government College for Women to educate female students at Higher Secondary level. The BSc classes were started in 1960 while the College was upgraded to Post Graduate College in 1989. The University acquired its current status on 5th January, 2013
