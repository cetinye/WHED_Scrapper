Admission Requirements
Admissions through the Joint Admissions and Matriculations Board (JAMB); For admission to 100 level, candidates must possess five (5) credit passes at Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (SSSCE) or equivalent in relevant subjects in only one sittings; and obtain a minimum of 200 points in University Matriculation Examination. All candidates who score above 200 UME and choose FUPRE as either first or second choice are further invited to a Post University Matriculation Examination (PUME) of the Federal University of Petroleum Resources Effurun. FUPRE Post University Matriculation Examination (PUME) is a two stage (written and oral) assessment process
