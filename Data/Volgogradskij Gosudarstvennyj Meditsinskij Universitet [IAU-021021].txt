History
Founded 1935 as Stalingradskij Meditsinskij Institut (Stalingrad Institute of Medicine), became an Academy in 1993 and acquired current status and title in 2003. Pyatigorsk State Pharmaceutical Academy (Pjatigorskaja Gosudarstvennaja Farmacevtičeskaja Akademija) was integrated as the Institute of Pharmacy in 2013
