History
Founded 1951 Huazhong Public University on the basis of Huazhong University (1902) and Zhongyuan University Educational College (1949) . Successively became Huazhong Normal Junior College 1952, Huazhong Normal College 1953. Acquired present status and title 1985. Part of "985 Project" and "211 Project". Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
