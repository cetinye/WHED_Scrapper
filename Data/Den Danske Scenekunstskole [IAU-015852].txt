History
Founded as Statens Scenekunstskole -The State Performing Arts School. (Also Known as Statens Teaterskole/National Theatre School of Denmark). Became part of The Danish National School of Performing Arts (DDSKS) after merger of Det Danske Musicalakademi i Fredericia (The Danish Musical Academy in Fredericia), Skuespilleruddannelsen ved Aarhus Teater (The Acting School at Aarhus Theatre); Skuespillerskolen i Odense (The Acting School in Odense); Dramatikeruddannelsen ved Aarhus Teater (School of Playwriting at Aarhus Theatre)
