History
Founded 1928 as the "Institute of Communications". Faculty of Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering (established 1954), Faculty of Mechanical Engineering (founded 1973) and Faculty of Aerospace Engineering (initiated 2006) were formally integrated in 1980 and named “Technical and Engineering University Complex”. Acquired current title 1984
