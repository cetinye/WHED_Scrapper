History
Founded 1928 as National Academy of Art. Became Hangzhou National College of Art 1929. Merged with Beijing Art Academy and renamed as National College of Art 1938. Became East-China Campus of Central Academy of Fine Arts 1950. Became Zhejiang Academy of Fine Arts 1958. Acquired present title 1993. Main administrative body: Zhejiang Province
