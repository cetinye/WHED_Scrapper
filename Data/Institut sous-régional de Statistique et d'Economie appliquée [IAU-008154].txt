History
Founded 1961 as Centre de Formation Statistique (CFS), renamed Institut de Formation Statistique (IFS) 1969. Became a national institution and renamed Institut de Statistique, de Planification et d'Economie Appliquée (ISPEA) 1975. Transformed into a sub-regional school of the Central African Customs and Economic Union (UDEAC) and acquired current title 1984.An interstate professional training institution
