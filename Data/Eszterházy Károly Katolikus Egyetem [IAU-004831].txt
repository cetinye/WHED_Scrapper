History
Founded in 1774. Incorporated Teachers' Training College in 1828, became Eszterházy Károly Teacher Training College (Eszterházy Károly Tanárképző Főiskola) in 1990, then Eszterházy Károly College (Eszterházy Károly Főiskola) in 2000 and acquired current title and status 2021.
