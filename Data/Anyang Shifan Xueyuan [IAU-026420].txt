History
First established 1908 as Zhangdefu Anyang Normal School. Successively became Pingyuan Provincial Anyang Normal School 1949, Henan Province Anyang Normal Training School 1958, Anyang First Normal School 1960, Anyang Normal Training School 1978 (restored), Anyang Junior College 1992. Acquired present status and title 2000 through the merger with Anyang Educational College and Anyang Second Normal School. Main administrative body: Henan Province
