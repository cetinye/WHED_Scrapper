History
Founded 1952 as Qingdao Institute of Architecture and Engineering. Upgraded and renamed as Shandong College of Metallurgy 1960. Became Shandong College of Metal Industry 1978. Became Qingdao College of Architecture and Engineering 1985. Acquired present title 2004. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
