History
Founded 1951 as Yunan Finance and Economics Cadres School. Upgraded to university-level and renamed Yunnan Finance and Economics College 1981. Incorporated Yunnan Economics and Administration Cadres School 1998 as well as Yunnan Finance and Economics School 2003. Acquired present title 2006. Under the administration of Yunnan Province
