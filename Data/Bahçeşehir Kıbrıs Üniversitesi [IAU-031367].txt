History
Founded 2016. Welcomed its first students in the 2017-2018 academic year. BAU Cyprus is part of the BAU Global Education Network, which includes Bahçeşehir University in Istanbul (headquarters), Bay Atlantic University in Washington, Berlin International University of Applied Sciences in Berlin, BAU International University in Batumi, and BAU Cyprus itself.
