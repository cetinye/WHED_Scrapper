History
Founded 1933 as Guangdong Provincial Xiangqin University Teachers College. Successively became Guangdong Science and Arts College 1950, South China Normal College following the incorporation of Zhongshan University Normal College and South China Union University Education Department 1951, Guangdong Normal College following several mergers 1970, South China Normal College 1977. Acquired present title 1982. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
