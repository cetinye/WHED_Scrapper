History
Founded 1927. Formerly known as Mindanao Polytechnic State College. Became Mindanao University of Science and Technology, 2009. Acquired present status and title from the merger of Mindanao University of Science and Technology and the Misamis Oriental State College of Agriculture and Technology (MOSCAT), 2016.
