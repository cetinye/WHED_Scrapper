History
Founded 1900 as l'Ecole supérieure de commerce de Nantes (ESC Nantes). Acquired present title 2010 following merger of Audencia Nantes, the Ecole atlantique de Commerce and Sciences Com. In 2013, Audencia Business School formed a strategic alliance with Centrale-Audencia-Ensa to enrich the teaching, research corporate relations and international scope of all three schools. Consular Higher Education Institution (EESC) status with CCI Nantes Saint-Nazaire
