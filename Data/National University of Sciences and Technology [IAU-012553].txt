Admission Requirements
Undergraduate: Minimum 60% aggregate marks each in Matric and in FSC (excluding NCC marks) equivalent exams. No deviation in this regard is allowed. Postgraduate: sixteen years of schooling or 4 years (130 credit hours) calculated after
FA/FSC/A Level mandatory for admission in MS/M Phil programme leading to PhD and MBA. The previous (terminal) degree or academic transcript on the basis of which applicant is eligible to apply for MS Engineering/IT/ M Phil leading to PhD must be PEC/HEC/PM&DC (as applicable) recognized with a minimum CGPA of 2.50 of 4.00 (3.50 of 5.00) or 1st Division (60% marks).
