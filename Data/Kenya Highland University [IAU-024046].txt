History
Founded as Sotik Bible School 1944. Relocated from Sotik to Cheptenye in Belgut area 1950. Transformed into Kenya Highlands Bible School 1955, renamed Kenya Highlands Bible College 1962 and the Bible College Council 1970. Registered by the Commission for Higher Education 1989. Acquired current status and title 2011. Former name: Kenya Highland Evangelical University.
