History
ESITH Casablanca was created in 1996 through a pioneering model of public-private partnership. ESITH belongs to the category of HEI known as the great schools of engineering in Morocco. It started with the main mission of meeting the needs of the Moroccan textile industry for highly qualified human capital. However, as it developed, it opened up to other programmes while remaining focused on industrial vocations such as industrial management, logistics and supply chain management. ESITH supports Research by fostering synergy between academia and industry in order to facilitate the transfer of know-how and innovation. The school's culture is based on quality teaching and proximity to industry, which explains its annual performance in terms of employability of graduates (92% six months after graduation). ESITH Casablanca supports private and public institutions in Morocco and abroad in their efforts to improve productivity and competitiveness thanks to a large team of professors-consultants who combine a strong experience of the industrial field with a solid scientific culture
