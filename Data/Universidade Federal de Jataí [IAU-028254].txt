History
Founded 1980 as Advanced Campus of Jataí (CAJ) a regional campus of the Federal University of Goiás (UFG). 2005, the Advanced Campus of Jataí was renamed Campus Jataí. 2014, the Jataí Campus was renamed Regional Jataí (REJ). After 39 years linked to the UFG, UFJ - Federal University of Jataí acquired present title and status in 2018
