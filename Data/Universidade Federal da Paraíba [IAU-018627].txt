History
Founded 1955, incorporating Faculties established 1947-52, acquired present status 1960. Reorganized 1974 with a structure comprising 7 campuses, located in the cities of João Pessoa, Campina Grande, Areia, Bananeiras, Patos, Sousa and Cajazeiras
