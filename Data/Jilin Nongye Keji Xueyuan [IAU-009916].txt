History
Founded 1907 as School of Agronomy. Renamed as Agricultural School of Jilin Province 1953. Merged into North China University and became the Institute of Agricultural Technology of the North China University 2001. Merged with Jilin Local Specialty College, upgraded to Higher Education Institution and acquired present title 2004.
