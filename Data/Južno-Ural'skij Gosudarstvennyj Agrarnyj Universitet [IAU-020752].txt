History
Founded 1930 as Ural'skij Veterinarnyj Institut (Ural Veterinary Institute). Became Troitskij Veterinarnyj Institut (Trinity Veterinary Institute) in 1940, Ural'skij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Veterinarnoj Meditsiny (Ural State Institute of Veterinary Medicine) in 1995, Ural'skaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Veterinarnoj Meditsiny (Ural State Academy of Veterinary Medicine) in 2000, and acquired current title in 2015 following a merger with Institut Agroinženerij (Institute of Agricultural Engineers)
