History
In 1896, under the Liard law 4 faculties were combined to form the University of Lyon: Science Faculty (1835), Arts Faculty, (1838), Faculty of Law (1875), Faculty of Medicine and Pharmacy (1877). December 1969: University Lyon 2 was created. Became Université Lumière Lyon 2 (1987)
