History
Founded 1941 as Chinese Army Unit of Russian Language (third branch of the Anti-Japanese Military and Political College). Became Yan'an College of Foreign Languages 1944. Renamed Harbin College of Foreign Languages 1956.Acquired present status and title 1958. Incorporated the Heilongjiang University of Science and Technology 1961, Harbin Institute of Foreign Languages 1972, Chinese Institute of Agricultural Sciences 2003, Heilongjiang Hydrology Vocational Institute 2004. Main administrative body: Heilongjiang Province
