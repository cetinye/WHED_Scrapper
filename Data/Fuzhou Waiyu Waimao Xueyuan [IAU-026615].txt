History
Founded 2004 as Fuzhou Vocational and Technical College of International Studies and Trade. Acquired present status (upgraded) and title 2011. Main administrative body: Fujian Provincial Education Department
Admission Requirements
Foreign students: High school education level or above (equivalent to sixth grade of high school), not over 30 years old, the HSK score of Elementary Level 4 or above. Applicant without HSK score can attend Language program first before degree program.
