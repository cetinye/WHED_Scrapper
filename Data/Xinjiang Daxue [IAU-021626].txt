History
Founded 1924 as Xinjiang Russian Political and Law Specialised School. Converted into Xinjiang College 1935. Acquired present title 1960. Merged with Xinjiang Technology College 2000. Part of "211 Project" universities. Under the administration of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region
