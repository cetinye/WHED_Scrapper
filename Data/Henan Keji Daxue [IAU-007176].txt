History
Founded 1952 in Beijing. Moved to Luoyang and became Luoyang Institute of Technology 1956. Acquired present status and title 2002 following the merger with Luoyang Medical Vocational College and Luoyang Agricultural Vocational College. Main administrative body: Henan Province
