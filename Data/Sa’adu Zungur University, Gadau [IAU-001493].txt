History

Admission Requirements
Candidates must posses a Minimum of 5 Credits in WASCE/NECO, or its equivalent at 'O' Level in not more than two sittings including English & Mathematics. Interested applicants are advised to check the University website for further information (www.basug.edu.ng). UTME Candidates without the 180 Minimum Jamb cut off points needs NOT to apply. NCE applicants who wish to apply for B.A ed or B.Sc ed programmes should have a minimum of merit grade in the relevant field of study in addition to the relevant 'O' level credits which must include English and Mathematics
