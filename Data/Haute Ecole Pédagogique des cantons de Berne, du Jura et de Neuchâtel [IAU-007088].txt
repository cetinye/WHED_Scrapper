From August to July (Automn Semester: August to January, Spring Semester: February to July)
Admission Requirements
For pre-primary and primary education : Secondary school certificate (maturité, baccalauraét) or equivalent
For secondary education : Bachelor of Arts or of Science
For higher secondary education : Master of Arts or of Science
For special needs education : Bachelor of Arts in Pre-Primary and Primary Education or Master of Arts or of Science in Secondary Education and/or Higher Education
