History
The school was formed by the merger of two Arts Schools: The National School of Decorative Art established in 1881 and the Municipal Art School founded in 1868. Acquired present status in 2003. Formerly known as Ecole nationale supérieure d'art de Limoges-Aubusson.
Admission Requirements
Baccalauréat or equivalent secondary school exam. Entrance exam with a selection jury.
