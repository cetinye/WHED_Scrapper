History
Founded 1495 as Colegio de Estudiantes by Lope Gómez de Marzoa. Became Estudio Gramática 1501. Approved by Papal Bull of Julian II 1504, and Papal Bull of Clement VII 1526, became university by Papal Bull of Pius V 1566. A State institution under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Education and Science
