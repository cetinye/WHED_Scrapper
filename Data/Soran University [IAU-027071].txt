History
Soran University was founded at the end of 2009, by decree of the Kurdistan Regional Government. Since its foundation, by concentrating on the quality of science, strengthening international relationships and profiting from the academic ability of both Kurdish and international specialists, Soran University has endeavored to present a new model of academic institutional experience
