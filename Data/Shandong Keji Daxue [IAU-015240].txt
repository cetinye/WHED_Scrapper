History
Founded 1951 as Hongshan School of Coal Industry. Became Jinan Coal School 1953. Became Shandong Mining Industry College 1963. Merged with Shandong Coal Education College and acquired present title 1999. Incorporated Shandong School of Finance 2001. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
