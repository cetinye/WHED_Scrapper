History
Founded 1839 as an academy, transformed into Bloomsburg Literary Institute 1866, authorized as a State Normal School 1869, succesively renamed Bloomsburg State Normal School 1916, Bloomsburg State Teachers College 1927, Bloomsburg State Collegere 1960, Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania 1983. Merged with Bloomsburg and Mansfield universities July 1, 2022 to form "Commonwealth University" (IAU-001707), a single institution with multiple locations and campuses. A member of Pennsylvania's State System of Higher Education (PASSHE)
