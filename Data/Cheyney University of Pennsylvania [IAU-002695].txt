History
Founded 1837 by the Quakers as the African Institute, a name changed several weeks later into the Institute for Colored Youth (ICY). Succesively renamed Cheyney Training School for Teachers 1914, Cheyney State Teacher’s College 1951, Cheyney State College 1959, acquired present title 1983. A member of Pennsylvania's State System of Higher Education (PASSHE)
