History
Founded 1955 as Molloy Catholic College for Women. Absolute Charter received from NY State with approval to confer B.A. and B.S. degrees 1960. Accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools. Renamed Molloy College 1971. Became coeducational 1982. Acquired current status and title 2022. Affiliated with the Roman Catholic Church
