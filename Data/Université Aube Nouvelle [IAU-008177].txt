History
Founded 1992 as Institut supérieur d'Informatique et de Gestion (ISIG). Acquired current status and title 2012. Organized in two campuses: Ouagadougou (U-AUBEN Ouaga) and Bobo-Dioulasso (U-AUBEN Bobo), the latter was formerly known as ISIG International
