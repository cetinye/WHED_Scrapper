History
Founded 1934 as National Northwest Agriculture and Forestry Vocational College. Acquired present status and title 1999 following the merger between Northwest Agriculture University, Northwest Forestry University and 7 research institutes. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
