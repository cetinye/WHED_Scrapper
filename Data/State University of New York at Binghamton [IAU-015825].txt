History
Founded as Triple Cities College - a branch of Syracuse University - 1946, incorporated into the State University of New York (SUNY) and renamed Harpur College 1960, transformed into State University of New York at Binghamton 1965. Informal name "Binghamton University" adopted 1992. Is part of the State University of New York (SUNY) system
