History
Created in 1906 and underwent a reorganisation in 1917 and then merged with the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences in 1957. Was closed briefly then reopened in 1979 as Capital University of Medical Sciences before returning to its original name of Peking Union Medical College in 1985.
