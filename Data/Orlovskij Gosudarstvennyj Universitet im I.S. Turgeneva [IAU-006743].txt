History
Founded 1954 as branch of Moscow Instrumental Institute, became independent 1993, and became Orlovskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekhničeskij Universitet (Orel State Technical University) in1995, became Gosudarstvennyj Universitet – Učebno-Naučno-Proizvodstvennyj Kompleks (State University - Teaching, Research and Production Complex) in 2010 and acquired current title and status 2016
