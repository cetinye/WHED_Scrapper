History
New Vision University is a civic, non-profit private institution founded in 2013. NVU mission is to expand human vision through promotion of education, research and innovation. The university inspires individuals to advance and share knowledge, skills and values to contribute to the well-being of the society. NVU is committed to social responsibility and promotes equal access to education to students from around the world. NVU is aspired to create open, innovative and collaborative environment to ensure student engagement and serve the society on a national and international scale

Admission Requirements
- Bachelor programmes: School Leaving Certificate or equivalent
- Master programmes: Bachelor degree or equivalent (Bachelor degree in Law for Master’s program in Law)
- One-cycle programs (Medicine, Dentistry): School Leaving Certificate or equivalent
- PhD programmes: Master's degree (Master’s Degree in Law for PhD program in Law; MD for PhD in Medicine)
- Proof of proficiency in English at B2 Level for Bachelor’s and Master’s degree programs, including one-cycle programs of Medicine and Dentistry. In case of PhD programmes - C1 level, it can be any other European (German, French) language
