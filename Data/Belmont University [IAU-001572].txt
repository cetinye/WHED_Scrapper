History
Founded 1890 as Belmont College for Young Women, merged with Ward Seminary for Young Ladies to form Ward-Belmont College 1913, became co-ed and renamed Belmont College 1951, acquired present status and title 1991. Absorbed O'More College of Design 2018 and Watkins College of Art 2020. A private institution affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention
