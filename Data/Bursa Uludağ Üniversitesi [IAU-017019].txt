History
Founded 1975 as Bursa Üniversitesi, incorporating the Bursa Faculty of Medicine of the University of Istanbul and the Bursa Academy of Economic and Commercial Sciences. Reorganized and acquired present title 1982 following the Higher Education Reform. An autonomous State institution
