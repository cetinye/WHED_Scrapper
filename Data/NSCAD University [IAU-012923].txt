History
Founded 1887 as Victoria School of Art and Design, became Nova Scotia College of Art 1925 and Nova Scotia College of Art and Design 1969. An independent institution of higher education. Students may also arrange to take courses at Dalhousie University and at other universities in Halifax. Acquired current title and status 2003.
