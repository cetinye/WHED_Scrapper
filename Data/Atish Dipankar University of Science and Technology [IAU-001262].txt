Admission Requirements
Candidates seeking admission should have to pass S.S.C, H.S.C or any equivalent public examination securing at least 2nd division or to have GPA 2.5 or equivalent Grade. But in any of the said examinations, candidates should have at least GPA 2.00 and minimum total Grade of 6.00 in both the examinations. A candidate completing O-Level Examination must have at least 5 (five) subjects in O-Level and at least 2 (two) subjects in A-Level examination. In both the examinations he/she should secure‘B’ Grade or GPA 4.00 in 4 (four) subjects and ‘C’ Grade or GPA 3.50 in the rest 3 (three) subjects out of a total 7 (seven) subjects
