History
Founded 1964. The University has 8 campuses, two at Bangalore and one at Mangalore, Mudigere, Ponnampet, Shimadya, Shimoga and Chintamani. It is modelled on the pattern of the Land Grant Institutions of the USA. It aims to integrate teaching, research and extension education in Agricultural Sciences.
