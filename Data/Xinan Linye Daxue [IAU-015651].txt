History
Founded 1938 as the Department of Forestry of the Yunnan University. Became Kunming Forestry College 1958, then Yunnan Forestry College, affiliated to the China’s Ministry of Forestry 1978. Acquired present status, renamed Yulin Forestry College 1983. Acquired present title 2010. Under the jurisdiction of Yunnan Province
