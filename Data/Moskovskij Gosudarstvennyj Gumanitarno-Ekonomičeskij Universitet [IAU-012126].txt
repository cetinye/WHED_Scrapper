History
Founded 1990. Previously known as Moskovskij Institut-Internat dlja Invalidov s Narušeniem Oporno-Dvigatelnoj Sistemy (Moscow Institute-Internate for the Motor Disabled), and Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Sotsialno-Gumanitarnyj Institut dlia Invalidov s Narušeniem Oporn-Dvigatelnoj Sistemy (Moscow State Social-Humanities Institute for the Motor Disabled). Became Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Sotsialno-Gumanitarnyj Institut (Moscow State Socio-Humanities Institute) in 2005 and acquired current title and status 2014
