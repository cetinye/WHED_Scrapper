Admission Requirements
For Bachelor’s Degree Programmes: completion of the secondary education’s 12th grade, or equivalent and passed Institutional Admission Competition to Higher Education (Concurso Institucional de Acesso ao Ensino Superior). Also special entry routes for: students coming from the Portuguese education system through re-admission, degree change and transfer schemes; adults aged more than 23 who have passed institutional tailor-made examinations intended to assess their ability to pursue higher education studies; holders of Intermediary or Graduate degree diplomas, students coming from foreign higher education through degree change and transfer schemes
For Master’s Degree Programme ("Mestre"): licenciado degree or equivalent corresponding to the first cycle of higher education; a foreign higher education degree awarded on completion of a first-cycle programme organized in the framework of the Bologna Process; an academic, scientific and/ or professional curriculum which is deemed by the IPAM Technical-Scientific Committee as appropriate to access the programme
