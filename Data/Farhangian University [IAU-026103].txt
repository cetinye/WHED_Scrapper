History
Founded 2011 after numerous teacher training centers in Iran were merged into a single entity. Teaching began in January 2012 by enrolling nearly 25000 undergraduate students. The university is an umbrella institution with around 64 main campuses and 34 centers evenly distributed in 32 provinces of the country.
