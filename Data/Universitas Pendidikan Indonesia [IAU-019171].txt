History
Founded 1954 as Higher Education Institute for Teacher Education (PIPG), became Institute of Teacher Training and Educational Sciences (IKIP Bandung) 1963, then was transformed into Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia 1999. Acquired present status 2012.
