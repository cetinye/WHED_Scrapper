History
Founded 1977 as Nanchang Branch of Jiangxi Normal College. Renamed Nanchang Teachers Vocational College 1984. Upgraded to Nanchang Vocational Normal College of Technology 1987. Renamed Jiangxi Normal Vocational College of Science and Technology 2002. Incorporated Nanchang Junior College 2004. Acquired present title 2012. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Province
