History
Founded 1941 as Gansu Provincial Zhangye Teachers School. Successively became Zhangye Normal College 1958, Zhangye Normal Training School 1959, Zhangye Normal School 1962. Closed down between 1962 and 1978. Reopened as Zhangye Normal Training School 1978, Zhangye Normal Junior College 1992. Incorporated Zhangye Agriculture School and Zhangye Vocational School 2000. Acquired present title 2001. Acquired present status following the merger with Zhangye Medical Junior College, Zhangye People Medical College 2014. Under the jurisdiction of Gansu Province
