History
Established in 1945 as Dow Medical College (DMC), it is one of the oldest medical colleges in Pakistan and offers MBBS and BDS undergraduate education, with a strong emphasis in the medical, biomedical sciences, public health, allied health sciences and business management programs. The University has one main campus, and another sub-campus located in the urban metropolitan city of Karachi, Sindh.
