History
Pelita Harapan University is a private Christian higher education institution and was founded in 1994. The university is located in Lippo Village, Tangerang, Banten, Indonesia. In 2018, Universitas Pelita Harapan Surabaya (Pelita Harapan University of Surabaya - IAU-019162) and Universitas Pelita Harapan Medan (Pelita Harapan University of Medan - IAU-019161) came together to form one university, also known as ONE UPH.
