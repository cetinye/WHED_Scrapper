History
Founded 1970 under the 1968 law reforming higher education as one of three Universities replacing the former Université de Toulouse, created in 1229. Acquired present title 2010. Member of the COMUE Université fédérale de Toulouse Midi-Pyrénées – Décree n° 2015- 663 of 10th June 2015
