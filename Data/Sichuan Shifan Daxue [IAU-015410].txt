History
Founded 1946 as Northeast Agriculture Technology College. Successively became Private Northern Sichuan University 1949, Public Northern Sichuan University 1950, Northern Sichuan University 1950, Sichuan Normal College 1952 following the merger with East Sichuan Education College and part of the Sichuan University and West China University. Incorporated part of the Chengdu University (present Southwest University of Finance and Economics)1964. Incorporated the Chengdu Coal Administration College for Cadres 1999. Acquired present status and title 1985. Under the jurisdiction of Sichuan Province
