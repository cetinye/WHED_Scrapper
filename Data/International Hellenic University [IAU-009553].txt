History
The International Hellenic University (IHU) was established by Law (No 3391/2005) and was Greece’s first public university where programmes were taught exclusively in English. According to Law No 4610/2019, IHU has merged with the Alexander Technological Educational Institute of Thessaloniki, the Technological Educational Institute of Central Macedonia and the Technological Educational Institute of Eastern Macedonia and Thrace
