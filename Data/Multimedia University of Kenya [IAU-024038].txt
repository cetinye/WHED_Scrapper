History
Founded as Central Training School (CTS) 1948. Upgraded into Kenya College of Communications Technology (KCCT) 1992 and then into Multimedia University College of Kenya, constituent college of Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology 2008. Acquired current status and title 2013.
