History
Founded as Donogh O’Malley Regional Technical College in 1971, then became Letterkenny Institute of Technology (Institiúid Teicneolaíochta Leitir Ceanainn) in 1998. Merged with Institute of Technology Sligo (Institiúid Teicneolaíochta Sligeach) and Galway-Mayo Institute of Technology (Institiúid Teicneolaíochta na Gaillimhe-Maigh Eo) in 2022 to become Atlantic Technological University (Ollscoil Teicneolaíochta an Atlantaigh).
