History
Established 1952 resulting from the merger between Shandong Accountancy Vocational College and Qilu University Economics Department. Became Shandong College of Economics 1978. Acquired present title 1986. Merged with Shandong College of Economics and Shandong College of Finance 2011. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
