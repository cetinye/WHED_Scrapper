History
Founded 1928 by the local school board as an extension of the high school, Fort Smith Junior College (FSJC). Separated 1950 the college separated from the high school, renamed Westark Junior College (WJC) 1966 and then Westark Community College (WCC) 1972, became Westark College 1998, merged into the University of Arkansas system and acquired current status and title 2002
