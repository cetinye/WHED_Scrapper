History
Founded 1998 as M. H. Dulaty Taraz State University (Tarazskij Gosudartsvennyj Universitet imeni M. Kh. Dulati) following merger of Zhambyl University, Zhambyl Institute of Irrigation, Land Reclamation and Construction ((created 1962) and Zhambyl Technological Institute of Light and Food Industry (created 1958). Acquired current title and status 2020 following merger with Taraz State Pedagogical University - IAU-016155 (Tarazskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet).
