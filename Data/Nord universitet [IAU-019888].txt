History
Founded as Høgskolen i Bodø in 1994. Became Universitetet i Nordland (University of Nordland) in 2010. Acquired current accreditation status and title in 2016, following the merger of (Høgskolen i Nord-Trøndelag) Nord-Trøndelag University College and Høgskolen i Nesna (Nesna University College)
