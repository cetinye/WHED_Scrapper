History
Created 1923 as Suide Normal School. Successively became Xi'an Normal College Suide branch 1958, Shaanxi Normal University Yulin Trainin Section 1978, Yulin Normal Training School 1984. Merged Yulin Training School (vocational college) of Agriculture and Forestry and renamed Yulin Junior College 1991. Acquired present status and title 2003. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
