Became Jakutskij Sel'skokhozjajstvennyj institut (Jakutsk Agricultural Institute) in 1985, Jakutskaja Gosudarstvennaja Sel'skokhozjajstvennaja Akademija (Jakutsk State Academy of Agriculture) in 1995 and acquired current title and status in 2020
