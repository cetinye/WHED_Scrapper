History
Founded 1917 as Falls City Center. Became Indiana University-Purdue University Fort Wayne 1968. The University officially splits into two campuses in 2018. Purdue University Fort Wayne and Indiana University Fort Wayne; IU Fort Wayne takes charge of the health sciences, while other areas become a part of Purdue University Fort Wayne
