History
Founded 1896 in Shanghai as Nanyang Public School. Became Jiaotong University 1921, moved to Xi'an and renamed Jiaotong University Xi'an Division 1956. Converted to Xi'an Jiaotong University 1959. Acquired present status 2000 following the merger with the Xi'an Medical University and the Shaanxi Institute of Finance and Economics 2000. One of the only two universities in China with a “class for gifted young people". Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
