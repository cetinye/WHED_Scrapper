History
Founded 1834 as St Volodymyr University; became Kyiv Institute of Public Education (KIPE) in 1920 , Kyiv Pedagogical Gorky Institute in 1933, Ukrainian State Pedagogical Dragomanov University in 1993, Nacional’nyj pedahohičnyj universytet imeni M.P. Drahomanova (National Pedagogical Dragomanov University) in 1997 and acquired current title and status 2022.
