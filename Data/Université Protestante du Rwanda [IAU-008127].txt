History
Founded 1990 as Faculty of Protestant Theology of Butare (Faculté de Théologie Protestante de Butare). Became Protestant Institute of Arts and Social Sciences in 2010.
Admission Requirements
Candidates must have sat Rwanda ‘A2’ level examination and obtained at least pass mark. Candidates with certificate from neighbouring countries such as Burundi, Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, and Republic Democratic of Congo must have their grade first equated by the National Examination Board before seeking admission.
