History
Founded 1902 as Royal Pages' School, became Civil Service College 1911 and University 1917.
Academic Year
June to March (June-October; November-March); International Programme: August to May (August-December; January-May)
Admission Requirements
Undergraduate: Secondary school certificate (Mathayom 6/Grade 12) or recognized equivalent, and entrance examination. Graduate: Completion of a Bachelor/Master degree or the equivalent from an accredited university, English proficiency test, and one year of work experience for those who apply for curriculum B
