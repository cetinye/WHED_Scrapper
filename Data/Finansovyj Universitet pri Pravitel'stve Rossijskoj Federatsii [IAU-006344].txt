History
Founded 1918, incorporating two existing institutes. Formerly known as Finansovaja Akademija pri Pravitel'stve Rossijskoj Federacii. Acquired current title 2010. Merged with Vserossijskaja Gosudarstvennaja Nalogavaja Akademija Ministerstva Finansov Rossijskoj Federatsii (All-Russian State Tax Academy of the Russian Federation Finance Ministry) in 2015
