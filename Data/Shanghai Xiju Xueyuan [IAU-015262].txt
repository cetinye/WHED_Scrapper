History
Cofounded 1945 as Shanghai Municipal Experimental Theatre School by the famous dramatists Li Jianwu, Gu Zhongyi and Huang Zuolin. Acquired present title 1956. Merger with Shanghai Normal University School of Performing and Fine Arts, Shanghai City School of Chinese Opera and Shanghai City School of Dance 2004. Main administrative body: Shanghai Municipality.
Admission Requirements
Foreign students: undergraduate program: between 18 and 26 years old. Senior high school （equivalent or above）graduation certificate. Valid foreign passport and provide the record or certificate indicating the passport holder actually lives overseas for more than 2 years in the past 4 years （or above). Be in good health, with good character and no criminal record, and abide by the laws, decrees of the People's Republic of China, and regulations of STA.Valid Chinese Proficiency Certificate HSK -4+（above 220 scores）.
