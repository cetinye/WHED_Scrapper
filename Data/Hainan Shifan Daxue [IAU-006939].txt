History
Founded 1949 as Hainan Teachers College (the formerly Qing Qiongtai Academy 1705). Successivel became Hainan Normal Vocational College 1952, Hainan Normal College 1986. Merged with Hainan Education College to shape the new Hainan Normal College 1999. Acquired present title 2007.
