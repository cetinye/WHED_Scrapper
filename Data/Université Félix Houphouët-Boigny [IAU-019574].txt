History
Founded 1958 as Centre d’Études spécialisées, attached to Université de Dakar 1959. Transformed into Université d’Abidjan and acquired autonomy 1964. Renamed Université nationale de Côte d’Ivoire (UNCI) 1977. The Centre universitaire de Cocody established 1992 at UNCI, transformed into Université de Cocody -an autonomous univesity - 1995. Closed March 2011 and reopened 2012 under current name
