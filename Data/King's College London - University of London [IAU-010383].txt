History
Founded 1829. The College has grown through mergers with several institutions which include the United Medical and Dental Schools of Guy's and St. Thomas Hospitals; Chelsea College, Queen Elizabeth College, and the Institute of Psychiatry. Granted degree-awarding power in its own right 2006. King's College London is a member institution of the federal University of London
