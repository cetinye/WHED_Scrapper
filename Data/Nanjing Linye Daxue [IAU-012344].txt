History
Emerged 1952 as Nanjing Institute of Forestry from the merger of the Central University (1902) Forestry Departement and the Jinling University (1910) Forestry Department. Incorporated the Central Agriculture Institute's Forestry Department 1955. Became Nanjing Forest Products Industry Institute 1972. Acquired present title 1985. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
