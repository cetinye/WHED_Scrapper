History
Founded 1974 as Narendra Deva University of Agriculture and Technology. Based on the model of the Land Grant Colleges of the USA and undertaking teaching research and extension education in an integrated manner. Responsible for all round development of rural communities in 15 districts of the Faizabad, Gorakhpur and Varanasi regions.
