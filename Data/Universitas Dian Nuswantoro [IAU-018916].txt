History
Founded as Sekolah Tinggi Manajemen Informatika dan Komputer Dian Nuswantoro. Acquired present status and title 2001 following the merger of the four colleges managed by Dian Nuswantoro Foundation: College of Informatics Management and Computer, College of Economics, College of Foreign Languages, and College of Health.
