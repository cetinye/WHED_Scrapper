History
Sri P.V. Narasimha Rao Telangana State University for Veterinary, Animal and Fisheries Sciences at Rajendranagar (Hyderabad) was established in 2014 by the Telangana Government in response to the division of the state of Andhra Pradesh into two states, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. This relocation to the state of Telangana duly distinguished it from Sri Venkateswara Veterinary University located in Tirupati (Andhra Pradesh - IAU-015716). The university acquired its present name in 2016
