History
Founded 1870 as Female Normal and High School, acquired postsecondary status 1888. Became Hunter College 1914, and co-educational 1951. Acquired present title 1961. Is part of the City of New York (CUNY) system
Academic Year
August to May. Also Summer Session (June-August)
