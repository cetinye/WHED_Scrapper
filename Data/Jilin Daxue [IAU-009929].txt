History
Founded 1946 as Northeast Administration Institute in Harbin. Became Northeast People's University and moved to Changchun 1950. Acquired present title 1958. Incorporated the former Jilin University of Technology (founded 1955), the former Norman Bethune University of Medical Sciences (founded 1939), the former Changchun University of Science and Technology (founded 1951) and the former Changchun Institute of Posts and Telecommunications (founded 1947) 2000. Incorporated the People's Liberation Army Military Logistics University 2004. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities.
