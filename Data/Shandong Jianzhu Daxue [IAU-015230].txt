History
Founded 1956 as Jinan Urban Construction School. Upgraded to a university for undergraduate education 1958. Successively renamed Shandong Institute of Architecture and Shandong School of Architecture. Reborn as the Shandong Institute of Architecture and Civil Engineering 1978. Acquired present title 2006. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
