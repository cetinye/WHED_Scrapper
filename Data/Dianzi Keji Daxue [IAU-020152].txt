History
Founded 1956 as Chengdu College of Telecommunications Engineering through the merger of the former Jiaotong University, Nanjing Technology College, South China Technology College's telecommunications departments. Acquired present title 1988. Incorporated Metallurgy Administration College for Cadres. Part of "985 Project" and "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
