History
Founded as Northeastern Pennsylvania Medical Education Development Consortium (MEDC) 2005, incorporated as Commonwealth Medical College (TCMC) 2008. Full MSCHE accreditation granted 2014. Integrated with Geisinger 2017 and became Geisinger Commonwealth School of Medicine. United with Geisinger-Lewistown Hospital School of Nursing under the name Geisinger College of Health Sciences 2022
