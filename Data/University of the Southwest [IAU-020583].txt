History
Founded 1956 as Hobbs Baptist College, a two-year junior college 1956. Changed to New Mexico Baptist College when the school became a four-year college 1958. Became College of the Southwest to University 1962. Acquired present status and title 2008

Admission Requirements
Undergraduates: GPA of 2.0 on a 4.0 scale, Class rank: top 50% of class, ACT of 19 or SAT of 910. Taking the even if a student is admitted on 2/3 criteria, the ACT or SAT is required for admission; Transfer students : GPA of 2.0 on a 4.0 scale; Graduates : Bachelor's degree, GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale for all college/university work, copy of respective licensure or certification, graduate record exam (GRE) from General Test. Further requirements : English Proficiency (ACT, SAT, TOEFL)
