History
Created 1811 in Ithaca as Ionian Academy by the British philhellene Frederic North. Moved to Corfu following the Greek War of Independence 1821. Officially started operating 1824. In 2018, according to law 4559/2018, the Technical Educational Institute of Ionian Islands merged with the Ionian University.
