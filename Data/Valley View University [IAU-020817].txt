History
Created 1979 by West African Union Mission of Seventh-day Adventists as Adventist Missionary College. In 1995 was affiliated to Griggs University, USA in order to offer Bachelor degrees. Accredited by NAB, Ghana in 1997 to award own degrees. Obtained current status 2006.
