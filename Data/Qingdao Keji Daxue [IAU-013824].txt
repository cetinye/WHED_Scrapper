History
Founded 1950 as Shenyang Senior Vocational School of Light Industry. Became Shandong College of Chemical Technology 1958. Renamed Qingdao Institute of Chemical Technology 1984. Incorporated Qingdao School of Arts and Craft 2001, acquired present title 2002. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
