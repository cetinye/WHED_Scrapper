History
Founded 1970 as Institut national d'Etudes judiciaires. Acquired present status and title 2002. Reform of training and diplomas of the Institute in progress.
Admission Requirements
A Master’s degree, a specialized Master’s degree, or a postgraduate or its equivalent, accompanied by a degree in Sharia or private law. The judicial attaché competition may be opened to holders of a BA in private law, a BA in Sharia, or their equivalents.
