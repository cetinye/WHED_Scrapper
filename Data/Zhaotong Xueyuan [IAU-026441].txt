History
Established 1906 as Normal Institute by the Qing dynasty. Successively became Zhaotong Normal Training School 1978, Zhaotong Normal Junior College 1992. Merged with Zhaotong Education College and Yunnan Radio and Television University's Zhaotong branch and Zhaotong Regional Normal School to form Zhaotong Normal Junior College 2000. Acquired present status and title 2012. Under the jurisdiction of Yunnan Province
