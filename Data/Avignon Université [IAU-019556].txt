History
Founded in 1303 under the Pope. Closed during the French Revolution. Founded in 1972 as Centre universitaire. Formerly the Unit of Arts and Humanities of the Université de Provence. Became Université d'Avignon et des Pays de Vaucluse, 1984. Acquired present title 2018
