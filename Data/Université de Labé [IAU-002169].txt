History
Founded in 2001 as Centre Universitaire de Labé (University Centre of Labé) under a decentralization policy of the government in order to supplement the two largest universities in the country and help students have access to universities close to their hometowns. The University Centre of Labé was part of Gamal Abdel Nasser University of Conakry until 2007 when it became an independent institution. Acquired university title in 2016
