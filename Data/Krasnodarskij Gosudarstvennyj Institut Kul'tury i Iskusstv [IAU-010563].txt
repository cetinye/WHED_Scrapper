History
Founded 1967 as Institute of Culture (Institut Kul'tury), became Krasnodarskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv i Kul'tury (Krasnodar State Institute of Arts and Culture) in 1991, Krasnodarskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury (Krasnodar State Academy of Culture) in 1993, Krasnodarskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury i Iskusstv (Krasnodar State Academy of Culture and Arts) in 1997, Krasnodarskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Kul'tury i Iskusstv (Krasnodar State University of Culture and Arts) in 1998, and acquired current title and status in 2015
