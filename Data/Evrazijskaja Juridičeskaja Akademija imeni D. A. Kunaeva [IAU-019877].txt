History
Created 1993 as Kunaev College, became Kazakh Humanitarian Institute named after. D.A. Kunaev in 1995, D.A. Kunaeva Humanities University in 1999, D. A. Kunaeva University in 2000; merged with Kazakh University of Railways in 2013 and became D.A. Kunaev University of Humanities, Law, and Transport (Gumanitarnyj Universitet Transporta i Prava imeni D.A. Kunaeva). Acquired current title and status 2015.
