History
Founded 1902 as Guizhou Institute of Higher Learning. Successively became Guizhou Provincial University, National Guizhou Agriculture Technology College. Acquired present title 1950. Incorporated Guizhou Agricultural College 1997. Merged with Guizhou University of Technology, 2004. Part of "211 Project" universities. Under the administration of Guizhou Province
