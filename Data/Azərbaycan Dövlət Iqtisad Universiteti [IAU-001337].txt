History
Founded in 1930. Azerbaijan State University of Economics is one of the largest higher education institutions in the Southern Caucasus. There are 11 faculties operating at UNEC with more than 18000 students studying 21 specialities. More than one thousand teachers, as well as 77 professors and 326 assistant professors among whom figure members of the National Academy of Sciences
