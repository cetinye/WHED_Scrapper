History
Founded 1848 as Masonic University of Tennessee, became Stewart College 1850, and Southwestern Presbyterian University 1875, shortened its name to Southwestern 1925, renamed Southwestern at Memphis 1945, acquired current title 1984. A private institution affiliated with the Presbyterian Church
