History
Founded 1959 as Zhangzhou Normal Vocational School. Became Fujian 2nd Normal College following the merger between Zhangzhou Normal Vocational School, Xiamen Normal College, Quanzhou Normal College, and the Nanping Normal College 1963. The College closed down during the Cultural Revolution. The College was reopened and renamed as Zhangzhou Normal College 1986. Acquired present title 2013. Main administrative body: Fujian Province
