History
Founded 1921 as Tianjin Technology and Business University. This university was upgraded to a comprehensive one and received the name of Hebei University 1960, which was relocated in 1970 from Tianjin to Baoding in Hebei Province. The Technology Supervision School of Hebei Province and Hebei Medical Staff College, as well as its affiliated hospital, were incorporated respectively into Hebei University in 2000 and 2005. Main administrative bdy: Hebei Province
