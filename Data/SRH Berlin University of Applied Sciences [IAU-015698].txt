History
Founded 2002 as OTA Hochschule (OTA Private University of Applied Sciences). Transformed into SRH Hochschule Berlin 2007. Took over the courses of the BEST-Sabel-Hochschule Berlin 2015. Acquired current title 2019 following merger with SRH Hochschule der populären Künste and Design Akademie Berlin, SRH Hochschule für Kommunikation und Design into the new SRH Berlin University of Applied Sciences
