History
Created 1950 as the People Liberation Army's Russian Training Corps of Southwest University of Military and Political Sciences. Renamed as the Russian Training Brigade of No.2 Senior Infantry School of the PLA 1951. Renamed as Russian Department of Southwest People’s Revolutionary University 1952. Became Southwest Russian College 1953, Sichuan Institute of Foreign Languages 1959. Acquired present title 1990. Main administrative body: Chongqing Municipality
