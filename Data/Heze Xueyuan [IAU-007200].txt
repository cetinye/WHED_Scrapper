History
Founded 1949 as Shandong and Henan area second Normal School. Renamed as Pingyuan Provincial Heze Normal School 1949. Renamed as Shandong First Normal School 1952. Turned into Heze Normal Vocational College 1958. Acquired present title 2002 following the merger between Heze Teachers Junior College, Heze Education College, Heze Radio and Television University, Heze Normal College and Heze Agricultural School. Acquired present status 2004. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
