History
Founded 1936 as Private Agricultural College of Fukien Christian University and Fukien Provincial Agricultural College. Renamed Fujian Agricultural College 1952. Became Fujian Agricultural University 1972. Acquired present status and title out of the merger between Fujian College of Forestry and Fujian Agricultural University 2000. Main administrative body: Fujian Province
