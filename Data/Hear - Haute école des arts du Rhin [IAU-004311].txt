History
Founded in 2011 following the merger of the Ecole supérieure des arts décoratifs de Strasbourg (ESADS), the Ecole supérieure d'art de Mulhouse (Le Quai) and the Académie supérieure de musique de Strasbourg
Admission Requirements
Fine Arts: Baccalauréat or equivalent secondary school exam, Entrance exam with a selection jury; Music: For admissions to the Bachelor DNSPM (performance or composition), the candidate must possess a DEM (a musical education diploma) or foreign equivalent. Admissions to the Masters programme are open to candidates with a Bachelor
