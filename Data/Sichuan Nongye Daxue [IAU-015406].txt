History
Founded 1906 as Sichuan Tongsheng Agricultural School. Successively became Sichuan Advanced Agriculture School 1912, Sichuan Public Agriculture Specialised School 1914, Public Sichuan University Agricultural Sciences College 1927, Sichuan Provincial Agriculture College 1932, National Sichuan University Agriculture College 1935, Sichuan University Agriculture College 1950. Became an independent college 1956. Acquired present title 1985. Incorporated Sichuan Forestry School 2001. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
