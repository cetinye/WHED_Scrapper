History
Established 1913 as Sichuan Vocational School. CDTU has been renamed 12 times in different periods of history, known as from “Sichuan Class A Industrial School”, “Chengdu Advanced Industrial Vocational School”, “Chengdu Radio Machinery College”, “Chengdu Electromechanical College”. Acquired present status and title 2012. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
