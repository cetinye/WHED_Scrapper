History
Founded as Forestry Institute in 1948. Incorporated the Institute of Mechanics in 1949. Reorganized in 1956 as Polytechnic Institute. Developed 1959 with Faculty of Wood Industry and Faculty of Mechanical Manufacturing Technology 1964. Acquired present status and title 1971. A state institution
