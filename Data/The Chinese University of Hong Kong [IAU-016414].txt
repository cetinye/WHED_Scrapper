History
CUHK is a highly esteemed, forward-looking comprehensive research university, recognized for excellence both regionally and globally. Founded in 1963, it is the second oldest university in Hong Kong. What sets CUHK apart are its deep roots in Chinese culture, its emphasis on bilingualism and multiculturalism, and a unique college system. The University’s founding philosophy is to combine tradition with modernity and to bring together China and the West

Admission Requirements
For Undergraduate Programmes
(1)
Local students: Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education (HKDSE) examination results
(2) )
International students: GCE AL/ International AL, International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma, and other high school qualifications (e.g. high school diploma plus SAT / ACT and AP Tests / SAT Subject Tests in the USA, UEC/STPM in Malaysia, ATAR in Australia, OSSD/BC diploma in Canada, AISSCE (CBSE)/ ISC in India, SMA in Indonesia, etc) would be considered
(3)
Students from mainland China: apply through the National Colleges and Universities Enrolment System - Details of admission requirements:
https://admission.cuhk.edu.hk
For Postgraduate Programmes
(1)
For Postgraduate Diploma Programmes: a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university
(2)
For MPhil-PhD and Master's Programmes: a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university, with Second Class honours or above; or a Bachelor's degree from an honours programme of a recognized university, with an average grade of "B" or above
(3)
For PhD, Doctor of Music and Taught Doctoral Programmes: a Master's degree from a recognized university - Details of admission requirements:
