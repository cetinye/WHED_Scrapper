History
Founded 1975 as Tianshui (Gansu Province) School of Seismology of the China Seismic Bureau. Upgraded to Higher Vocational College of Seismology Techniques 1992. Upgraded to a University and Renamed Institute of Disaster-Prevention Science and Technology 2006. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: China Earthquake Administration
