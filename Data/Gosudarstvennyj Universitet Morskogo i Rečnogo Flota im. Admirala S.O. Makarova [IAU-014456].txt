History
Founded 1809 as Institute of Engineers. Became Sankt-Peterburgskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Vodnykh Kommunikacij (Saint-Petersburg State University of Waterways Communications) in 1993 and acquired current title in 2012 following merger with Gosudarstvennaja Morskaja Akademija im. Admirala S.O. Makarova (State Marine Academy named after Admiral Makarov). Was merged with Moskovskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Vodnogo Transporta (Moscow State Academy of Water Transport) in 2019
