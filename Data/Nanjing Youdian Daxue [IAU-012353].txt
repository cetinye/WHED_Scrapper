History
Founded 1942 as Shandong Anti-Japanese Base Area's CCP-led Eighth Route Army Class Training for Cadres. Upgraded to College and renamed Nanjing College of Posts and Telecommunications 1958. Acquired present title 2005. Incorporated Nanjing Population Administration Institute for Cadres 2013. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province.
