History
Westcliff University was established in 1993 to provide quality education for students wishing to enter the expanding fields of Business, Education, Law, Technology, and Engineering. We offer full-time working students a chance to take courses onsite at our beautiful California and Florida campuses and live online. All programs at Westcliff are convenient, flexible, and affordable.
Our graduate and undergraduate certificates and degree programs prepare students for immediate entry and advancement in their respective industries. While some schools only teach theoretical concepts, the programs at Westcliff University prepare students for the practical and theoretical elements required in the job market today. We prepare students for personal, professional, and academic success with innovative, up-to-date, and high-quality programs. The University has guidance and expertise from its Faculty, Program Advisory Council, and Board of Trustees that includes key leaders from local, national, international businesses and Fortune 500 organizations.
Westcliff is approved by the Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education (www.bppe.ca.gov) and is accredited by Senior College and University Commission (WSCUC) (www.wscuc.org), as well as programmatic accreditations for our College of Business by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) (
https://acbsp.org/
) and Western State College of Law is accredited by the American Bar Association (ABA) (www.americanbar.org/).
