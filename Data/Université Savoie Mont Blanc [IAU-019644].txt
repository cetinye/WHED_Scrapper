History
Founded 1970 as Centre Universitaire de Savoie under the 1968 law reforming higher education and replacing institutions attached to former Université de Grenoble. Acquired present status 1979. Previously known as University of the Savoie, Chambéry (Université de Savoie). Acquired current title 2014
