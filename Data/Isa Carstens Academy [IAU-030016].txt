History
Established in 1978 in Stellenbosch as the Isabelle Carstens Training Centre. Later renamed as the Stellenbosch Academy of Beauty Therapy. In 1998, it became the Isa Carstens Health and Skin Care Therapy.
Recently renamed the Isa Carstens Academy and began offering other qualifications. The Pretoria Campus opened in 2011.
