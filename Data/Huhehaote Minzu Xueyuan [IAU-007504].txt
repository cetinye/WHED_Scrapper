History
Founded 1953. Inner Mongolia Humanities Training Section merged with Inner Mongolia Normal School for Nationalities and became Inner Mongolia Normal Junior College for Nationalities 1955. Acquired present status 2013. Formerly known as Hohhot Mongolia University for Nationalities. Main administrative body: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
