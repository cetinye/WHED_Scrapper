History
MCI | The Entrepreneurial School® brings together the best of science, economy and consulting in the unique concept of an International Entrepreneurial University of Applied Sciences providing the platform for numerous academic and business activities, including start-ups, executive education, summer and winter schools, study abroad programmes, international conferences and more. It stands for internationality, academic quality, practical orientation, innovation, close cooperation with industry, solution-oriented research and development, first-class infrastructure, high level of customer and service orientation, and international reputation
