History
Founded 1942 as the Medical Faculty of the Mongolian State University. Became independent of the Mongolian State University and re-named as the Mongolian State Medical Institute 1961. Reorganized as the National Medical University of Mongolia (NMUM) 1989. Restructured and organized as a multidisciplinary training and research center called The Health Sciences University of Mongolia (HSUM) 2003. Acquired current title 2014
