History
Founded 1951 as East China Textile Engineering Institute as a result of several mergers. Became China Textile University 1985 and incorporated Cadre Management School, China Textile Union 1994. Acquired present title, absorbed Shanghai Textile Higher Vocational Institute and State Bureau of Light Industry, Shanghai Glass and Enamel Research Institute 1999. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
