History
Created 1984 as Zhengzhou Self-Study Examination Preparatory Class. Became Huanghe Postsecondary School of Technology 1985, then Zhengzhou Huanghe University of Technology 1989. Acquired present title, privatized 1994. Upgraded to a university-level institution 2000. Main administrative body: Henan Provincial Education Department.
