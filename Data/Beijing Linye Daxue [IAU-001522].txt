History
Founded 1952 as Beijing Forestry College following the merger between Beijing Agricultural University Forestry Department and the Hebei Agricultural College Forestry Department. Incorporated Beijing Agriculture University Landscape Department and Qinghua University Architecture Department 1956. Acquired present title 1985. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
