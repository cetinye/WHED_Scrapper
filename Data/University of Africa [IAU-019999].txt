History
Founded 2008, was merged with Central Africa Correspondence College [CACC] in 2010. In 2020 the institution was bought by Keystone University of Africa (which also bought the African Research University) and Keystone coordinates the different functions of the two universities, which both continue to operate as separate institutions.
