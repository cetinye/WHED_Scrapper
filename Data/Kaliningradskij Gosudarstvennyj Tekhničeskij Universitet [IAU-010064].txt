History
Founded 1930 as State Technical Institute for Fisheries and Economy in Moscow. Became Kaliningrad State Technical Institute for Fishery Industry and Economy in Kaliningrad. Acquired present status and title 1994. Merged with Baltijskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Rybopromyslovogo Flota (Baltic State Fishing Fleet Academy) in 2013
