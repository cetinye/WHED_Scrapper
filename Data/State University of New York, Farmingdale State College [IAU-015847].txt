History
Founded 1912 as New York State School of Agriculture on Long Island (NYSSA), successively renamed: State Institute of Applied Agriculture on Long Island 1920, State Institute of Applied Agriculture 1924, State Institute of Agriculture 1939, Long Island Agricultural and Technical Institute (LIATI) 1946, SUNY Long Island Agricultural and Technical Institute at Farmingdale, New York 1953, Agricultural and Technical College at Farmingdale 1966, SUNY College of Technology at Farmingdale 1987, Farmingdale State College 1993. Acquired present title 2002, is part of the State University of New York (SUNY) system
