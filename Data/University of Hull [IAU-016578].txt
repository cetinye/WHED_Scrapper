Admission Requirements
General Certificate of Education (GCE) or equivalent qualifications with passes in 2 approved subjects at Ordinary ('O')/General Certificate of Secondary Education (GCSE) level and 2 at Advanced ('A') level, or 1 at 'O' and 3 at 'A'. 2 Advanced Supplementary ('AS') level will be accepted in place of 1 'A' level in all relevant options of the general requirement. 'AS' levels will be accepted in place of 'O' level/GCSE subjects where they are specified in the general requirement
