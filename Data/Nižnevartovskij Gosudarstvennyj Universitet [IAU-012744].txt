History
Founded 1988 as a branch of the Tobolsk Pedagogical Institute. Became Nižnevartovsk Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Institut (Nizhnevartovsk State Pedagogical Institute) in 1992, became Nižnevartovskij Gosudarstvennyj Gumanitarnyj Universitet (Nizhnevartovsk State University for the Humanities) in 2005 and acquired current title in 2013
