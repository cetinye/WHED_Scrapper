History
Founded as Wrexham Technical Institute 1927. Became North East Wales Institute 1975 and a full member of the University of Wales 2004. Became Glyndwr University - Prifysgol Glyndwr in 2008 when it received degree-awarding powers, and acquired current title 2014. Name change, acquired present title 2023.
