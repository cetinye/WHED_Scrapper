History
Founded 1853 as Louisiana State Seminary of Learning and Military Academy which opened 1860, renamed Louisiana State University (LSU) 1870. Merged 1877 with Louisiana State Agricultural and Mechanical College (founded 1874). The oldest and largest institution in the LSU System. (The Paul M. Hebert Law Centre is a separate campus of the System)
Academic Year
August to May (August-December; January-May). Also Summer Terms (June-August; June-July)
Admission Requirements
