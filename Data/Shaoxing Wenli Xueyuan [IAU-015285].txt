History
Founded 1909 as Shanhui Elementary Level Teachers School supervised by Lu Xun. Shaoxing Junior College Level Teachers School and Shaoxing Higher Vocational School merged and became present University 1995. Incorporated Shaoxing Nursing School, Shangyu Teachers School, Shaoxing Staff Secondary Professional School 2000. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
