History
Founded 1802 as a university department Voronezh University. Became independent Medical Institute 1930 and became Voronežskaja Gosudarstvennaja Medicinskaja Akademija im. N.N. Burdenko (Voronezh State Medical Academy named after N.N. Burdenko) in 1994 and acquired present status and title 2015
Academic Year
September-December; February-June
Admission Requirements
Secondary school certificate (Attestat o sredem obrazovanii), with over 50% in biology, chemistry and physics. Overseas students must take entrance examination in natural sciences and Russian language.
