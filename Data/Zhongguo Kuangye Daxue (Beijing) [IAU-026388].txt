History
Founded 1909 as Jiaozuo School of Railroad and Mines. Became Private Jiaozuo Technology College 1931, then National Northwest Technology University 1938.The school moved from Jiaozuo to Tianjin and was renamed China Institute of Mining & Technology (CIMT) 1950. In the nationwide restructuring of universities and colleges, the mining engineering departments of Tsinghua University, the former Beiyang University, and Tangshan Institute of Railroad were merged into CIMT 1952. Moved to Beijing and renamed Beijing Institute of Mining & Technology 1953. Moved to Sichuan Province 1970s. The Institute resumed its former name CIMT 1978. Acquired present title 1988. Acquired present status 1998. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
