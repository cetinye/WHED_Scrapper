History
Founded 1742 as a Girls’ School and a Boys' school. A second boys' school opened 1743 and merged with the pre-existing one 1759 to form Nazareth Hall. Moravian College. Renamed Moravian College and Theological Seminary 1804. Women’s and men’s institutions merged 1954 to form first coeducational institution of higher education in the Lehigh Valley. Acquired current status and title 2021. A private institution affiliated with the Moravian Church
