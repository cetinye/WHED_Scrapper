History
Founded 1958 as Ulan Chab League Normal Training School. The school reopened as Ulan Chaab League Normal School 1976. Became Ulan Chab Normal Training School 1985, then Jining Normal Junior College 1995. Incorporated Ulan Chab League Normal School, Ulan Chaab League Mongolian Minorities Normal School 2000. Acquired current title 2009. Acquired present status 2013. Main administrative body: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
