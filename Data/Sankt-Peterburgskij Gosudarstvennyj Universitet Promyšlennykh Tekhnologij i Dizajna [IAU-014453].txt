History
Founded 1828 as St. Petersburg Practical Technological Institute, in 1930 became Leningraskij Tekstil'nyj Institut (Leningrad Textile Institute), Leningradskij Institut Tekstil'noj i Legkoj Promyshlennosti im. Kirova (Leningrad Institute of Textile and Light Industry named after Kirov) in 1963, Sankt-Peterburgskij Universitet Tekhnologii i Dizajna (Saint-Petersburg University of Technology and Design) in 1992, and acquired current title and status in 2015 following a merger with Sankt-Peterburgskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekhnologičeskij Universitet Rastitelnykh Polimerov (Saint-Petersburg State Technological University of Plant Polymers), which became the School of Technology and Energy
