History
Founded 1909 as Gansu Law and Politics School. Successively became Lanzhou Sun Yat-sen University 1928, National Lanzhou University 1945. Merged with the former Gansu Grassland Ecological Institute 2002 and with Lanzhou Medical School 2004. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Under the administration of the Ministry of Education
