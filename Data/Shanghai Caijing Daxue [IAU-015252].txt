History
Created 1917 as Nanjing Teacher School's Business Section. Became Shanghai Business University branch of the Southeast State-run University and moved to Shanghai 1921. Changed to Shanghai Business Institute 1932. Acquired present title 1985. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
