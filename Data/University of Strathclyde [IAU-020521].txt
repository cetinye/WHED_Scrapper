Admission Requirements
Scottish Certificate of Education (SCE) with passes in 5 approved subjects, including pass in English, with 3 at the Higher ('H') grade, or General Certificate of Education (GCE) with passes in 3 subjects at Advanced ('A') level. SCE with passes in 4 approved subjects, including pass in English, at 'H' and one at 'A' level, or 2 at 'H' and 2 at 'A' or 3 at 'A'; or recognized foreign equivalent. Proficiency in English, IELTS 6.0 (TOEFL 550-600) is required
