History
Founded 1953 as Semipalatinsk State Medical Institute (Semipalatinskij gosudarstvennyj meditsinskiy institut, became Semipalatinsk State Medical Academy (Semipalatinskaja gosudarstvennaja meditsinskaja akademija) in 1997, then Semey State Medical University (Gosudarstvennyj Meditsinskij Universitet Goroda Semei) in 2009 and acquired current title and status in 2019.
