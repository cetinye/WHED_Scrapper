History
Organized as a clinical paediatric hospital 1905. Became the Research Institute of Maternity and Child Welfare in 1925. Became Sankt-Peterburgskaja Gosudarstvennaja Pediatričeskaja Medicinskaja Akademija (Saint-Petersburg State Medical Academy of Paediatrics) in 1994 and acquired current title and status 2012
