History
Founded 1919. During the Anti-Japanese War (1937-1945), Nankai, Peking (Beijng) and Tsinghua (Qinghua) universities united in Kunming to form the renowned Southwest Associated University.Incorporated the former Tianjin Institute of Foreign Trade 1994. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
