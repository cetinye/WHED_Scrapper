History
Founded 1945 by Indiana University. One of 8 campuses of Indiana University, it has developed as a regional University for commuter students, and serves an 11-county area in North-Central Indiana. Through a co-operative agreement with Purdue University, students can pursue studies and receive Purdue degrees on Campus
