History
Founded 1961 on the basis of the Faculty of Education of Hassanuddin Universitas. Attached as a branch of IKIP Yogyakarta 1964. Became IKIP Makassar 1965. Changed title to IKIP Ujung Pandang 1972. Acquired university status and present title 1999.
