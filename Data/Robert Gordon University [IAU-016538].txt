History
Founded 1729 as Robert Gordon's Hospital, in 1821 it was transformed into Robert Gordon's College. In 1991 became The Robert Gordon Institute of Technology and acquired present status and title in 1992

Admission Requirements
Undergraduate: Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA) Higher Grade passes (minimum of 3) plus Standard Grade passes, or General Certificate of Education (GCE) Advanced Level passes (minimum of 2) plus Ordinary Level passes, or alternative qualifications. Postgraduate: an honours degree or degree equivalent.
