History
Founded in 1996 as Foundation of Technical Education Sulaimani (FTES). Restructured to Sulaimani Polytechnic University in 2012 by a ministry decision from the Council of Ministers,Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. The University is comprised of 13 Institutes and Colleges and offers a variety of major areas of studies including Engineering, Health and Medical Sciences, Agriculture, Computer Science, ICT and Business Administration leading to Technical Diploma, Bachelor and Master degrees.
