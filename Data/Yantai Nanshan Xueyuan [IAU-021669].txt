History
Founded 1991 as Xinhua Vocational High School. Became Nanshan Training Institute 1996. Renamed as Nanshan Vocational College of Technology 2000. Acquired present title 2005. Officially upgraded to a university-level college by the State Council Academic Degrees Committee 2016. Main adminsitrative body: Shandong Provincial Education Department
