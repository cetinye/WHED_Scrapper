History
Founded 1964 as Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA), prviously knoww as Royal Military Forces Training College (RMFTC) and after independence in 1960, became known as the Nigerian Military Training College. Upgraded into a degree-awarding institution 1985. A federal university, open to both military and civilian students
