History
Created 1948 as Almaty Industrial College (Almatinskij Industrialnyj Tekhnikum). Became Almaty Industrial Pedagogical Academy (Almatinskaja Industrialno-pedagogičeskaja Akademija) in 2000, Almaty Humanitarian Technical University (Almatinskij Gumanitarno-Tekhničeskij Universitet) in 2005 and acquired current title and status in 2014.
