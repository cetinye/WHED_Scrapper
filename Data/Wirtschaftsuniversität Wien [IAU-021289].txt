History
Founded 1898 as Imperial Export Academy, soon took on the characteristics of a university. Received the right to confer doctorates in 1930. Became Vienna University of Economics and Business Administration in 1975. Gained full institutional autonomy in 2004 through the University Act of 2002. Earned current title (Vienna University of Economics and Business) in 2009
