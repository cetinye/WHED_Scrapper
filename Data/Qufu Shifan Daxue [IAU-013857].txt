History
Founded 1955 as Shandong Teachers Junior College. Upgraded and became Qufu Teachers College 1956. Became Shandong University 1970-1974. Restored aas Qufu Teachers College 1974. Acquired present status and title 1985. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
