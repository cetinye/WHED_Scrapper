History
Founded 1875 as Branch Normal College, a branch of Arkansas Industrial University, now the University of Arkansas (UA) in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Renamed Arkansas Agricultural, Mechanical and Normal College (AM&N) 1927, acquired current status and title 1972 when merged with the University of Arkansas system
