History
Founded 1722. Used to be member of the former COMUE Université de Bourgogne Franche-Comté (Community of Universities and Establishment) – Décret n° 2015-280 - 11 mars 2015. Known as Université de Bourgogne until 2024. Acquired present title 2025.
