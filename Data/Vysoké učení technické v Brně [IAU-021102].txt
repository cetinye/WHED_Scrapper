History
Founded as Czech Technical University of Franz Joseph in Brno, 1899. Became the Czech Technical University of Brno 1918 and Technical University of Dr. E. Beneš in Brno 1937. Closed during the German occupation, and reopened in 1945. Acquired present title in 1956. An institution financed by the State.
