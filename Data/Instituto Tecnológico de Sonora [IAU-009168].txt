History
Founded 1955 as preparatory school, acquired present status and title 1973. On 1976 the State Congress approved an initiative of the Executive and promulgated the Organic Law that grants university autonomy to ITSON, which defines it as a decentralized public body of a university nature, of legal personality and own assets; autonomous in the exercise of their teaching and research functions
