History
Founded 1939, became Moscow State Technical University 'MAMI' (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Tehničeskij Universitet 'MAMI') in 1997, merged with Moscow State University of Environmental Engineering (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Inženernoj Ekologii) in 2013, and acquired current title 2012. Merged with Moscow State Open University (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Otkrytyj Universitet) and Moscow State Institute of Metallurgy - Evening Studies (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Večernij Metallurgičeskij Institut) at the end of 2012, and was also known as Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Mašinostroitelnyj Universitet 'MAMI' (Moscow State University of Mechanical Engineering 'MAMI'), and merged with Moscow State Industrial University (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Industrialnyj Universitet) in 2015 and Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Pečati (Moscow State University of Printing Arts) in 2016 when acquired current status and title
