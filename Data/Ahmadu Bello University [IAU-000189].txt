History
Founded 1962 as University of Northern Nigeria, later renamed Ahmadu Bello University. Became a federal institution 1975. Has 3 affiliated Institutions: Federal College of Education Zaria; Federal Polytechnich Bauchi; Niger State College of Education, Minna

Admission Requirements
