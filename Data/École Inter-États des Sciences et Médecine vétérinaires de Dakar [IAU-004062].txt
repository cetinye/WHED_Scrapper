History
Founded 1968 as an institute of the Cheikh Anta Diop University of Dakar (UCAD). Adopted current status and title 1971 through the signing of an interstate agreement between 14 African countries (Benin, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Republic of Congo, Gabon, Mauritania, Niger, Central African Republic, Senegal, Chad, Togo and Mali). Acquired administrative and financial autonomy 1976. A panafrican training and research school
