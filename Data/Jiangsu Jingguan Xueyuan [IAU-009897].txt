History
Founded 1949 as Nanjing Public Security Institute. Renamed as Jiangsu School of Public Security 1953. Became Jiangsu Public Security Vocational School 1982. Merged with Jiangsu People's Police School 1998. Merged with Jiangsu Judicial School 2000. Upgraded to College and acquired present title 2002. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
