History
Founded 1968 as Čeljabinskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kul'tury (Chelyabinsk State Institute of Culture). Became Čeljabinskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstva i Kul'tury (Chelyabinsk State Institute of Art and Culture) in 1991, and Čeljabinskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury i Iskusstv (Chelyabinsk State Academy of Culture and Arts) in 1999. Acquired current title and status in 2015
