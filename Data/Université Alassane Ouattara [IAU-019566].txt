History
Founded 1992 as University Centre based in Bouaké. Acquired status of autonomous university and renamed Université de Bouaké 1995. Relocated to Abidjan 2002 following political turmoil. Closed down for renovation from 2010 to 2012 when reopened under current title and relocated in Bouaké
