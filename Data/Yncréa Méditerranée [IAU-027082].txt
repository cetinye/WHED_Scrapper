History
Founded in 1991 under the name of Institut supérieur d'électronique et du numérique (ISEN) of Toulon. ISEN Toulon became a member of the Yncrea group in 2016 and was renamed Yncrea Méditarrannée. Yncrea Mediterrannée also includes campuses in Marseille and Nîmes
