History
Founded 1950, incorporating the Balai Perguruan Tinggi Republik Indonesia (Institution of Higher Learning of the Republic of Indonesia) and Universiteit van Indonesia. Faculties in Bandung, Surabaya and Makassar have since been incorporated in newly-established State universities.
