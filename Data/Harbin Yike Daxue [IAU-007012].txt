History
Founded 1926 by the merger of the Harbin Vocational Medical College (whose founder was the famous Malayan-born Chinese doctor Wu Delian) and the China Medical University in Xingshan (its predecessor being China Red Army Health School founded in Ruijin, Jiangxi, in 1931). Acquired present status and title 1949. Main administrative body: Heilongjiang Province
