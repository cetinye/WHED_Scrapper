History
Founded 1910 as 4th Normal School for Primary Education. Renamed Xingtai Fourth Normal Vocational School 1984. Became Xingtai Normal Vocational College 1996 following the merger of Xingtai Institute of Education and Xingtai Economic Management Cadres School 1996. Acquired university status and present title 2002. Main administrative body: Hebei Province
