History
Founded 1942 as Peili Polytechnic School. Was transformed into Gansu Mechanical Manufacturing Secondary School in 1958. Merged with five other technical secondary schools (the original Coal Technical Secondary School, Power Technical Secondary School, Light Industry Technical Secondary School, Heavy Industry Technical Secondary School, and Telecommunication Technical Secondary School) to form Gansu Technical School 1962. Later renamed Lanzhou Technical School in 1974, then as Lanzhou Polytechnic College 1989. Acquired present status and title 2012. Under the direct jurisdiction of Gansu Province
