History
Founded 1923 as School of Finance and Economics of Jiangxi Province. Became Jiangxi College of Finance and Economics 1958. Closed down during the Cultural Revolution. Reopened 1978. Became Ministry of Finance affiliated College 1980. Acquired present status and title 1996. Main administrative body : Jiangxi Province
