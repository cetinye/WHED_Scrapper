History
Founded 1862 as Missionshaus (Mission House), a combined academy-college-seminary. Became Lakeland College 1956, an independent, private, liberal arts college affiliated with the United Church of Christ. Formed its two-year campus in Tokyo, Japan 1990. Acquired current status and title 2016
