History
Founded 2022 - opened 2024 - through the ratification of an agreement signed 2021 between the Government of the Italian Republic and the Athens-based headquarters of the European Public Law Organization (EPLO) - established 2007 as the continuation of the European Public Law Center (EPLC) created 1995. The European Law and Global Governance School (ELGS) - offering degrees - was founded in 2013 and started its programmes 2016
