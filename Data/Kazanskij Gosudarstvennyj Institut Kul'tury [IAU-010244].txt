History
Founded 1969 as a branch of Leningrad State Institute of Culture (Leningradskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kul'tury), became independent under the name of Kazan State Institute of Culture (Kazanskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Kul'tury) in 1974, then Kazan State Institute of Arts and Culture (Kazanskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Iskusstv i Kul'tury) in 1991, changing to Kazan State Academy of Culture and Arts (Kazanskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury i Iskusstv) in 1995, then to Kazan State University of Cultureand Arts (Kazanskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Kul'tury i Iskusstv) in 2002. Acquired current title 2015
