History
Founded 1949 as the Changsha Martial Law Commmission Trade Department Training Class for Cadres. Became Hunan School of Trade 1951. Successively became Hunan Cadres School of Finance and Trade 1958, Hunan College of Finance and Trade 1960, Hunan Commerce Vocational College 1993. Merged with Hunan Commerce and Administration School for Cadres (founded 1980) to shape the current university 1994. Formerly known as Hunan College of Commerce. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
