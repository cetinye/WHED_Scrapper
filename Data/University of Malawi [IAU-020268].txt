History
Founded 1964, integrating all the country's facilities for further and higher education. Started academic activities1965. Until 2021 composed of four constituent colleges (Chancellor College, College of Medicine, Kamuzu College of Nursing, The Malawi Polytechnic) which were delinked, except the Chancellor College (CHANCO) now known as the University of Malawi (UMA)
