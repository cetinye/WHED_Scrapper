History
Founded 1978 as Changzhou 712 Industrial University. Became Changzhou Vocational University 1980. Became Changzhou Institute of Industry and Technology 1982. Changzhou Institute of Industry and Technology merged with Changzhou Vocational University of Machinery and Metallurgy and gave rise to present Institute, also upgraded to College 2000. Incorporated Changzhou Normal Vocational School 2003. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
