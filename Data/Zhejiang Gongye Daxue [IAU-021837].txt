History
Founded as Hangzhou Chemical School 1953. Changed name to Zhejiang Chemical Vocational College 1958. Renamed Zhejiang College of Chemical Technology following merger with Quzhou Chemical Technology Vocational School 1960. Renamed Zhejiang Institute of Technology 1978. Acquired present title 1993. Merged with Zhejiang Economic and Management Cadres College, Hangzhou Shipbuilding Industry School, Zhejiang Building Material Industry School respectively 1994, 1999 and 2001. Main administrative body: Zhejiang Province
