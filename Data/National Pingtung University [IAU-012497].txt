History
National Pingtung University (NPTU) was founded on August 1, 2014 with the mergier of National Pingtung University of Education and the National Pingtung Institute of Commerce. The Origins of both universities were respectively the Taiwan Governor General Normal School established in April, 1940, and National Pingtung Junior College of Commerce established in July, 1991.
National Pingtung University has 4 campuses, namely the Minsheng campus, the Pingshang campus, the Pingshi campus, and Checheng campus, The administrative center is located in the city and is close to every department where traffic and transportation are convenient and available, so it has the great potential for future development. In addition to the Administrative Affairs Organization, there are 5 colleges of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences, Education, Sciences, Management, and Computer Sciences. Most departments provide both under- and post-graduate programs.
