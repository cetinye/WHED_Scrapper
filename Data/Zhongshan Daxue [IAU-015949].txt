History
Founded 1924 as Guangdong National University. Renamed National Zhongshan (Sun Yat-Sen) University 1926. Acquired present title and status 1952, following merger with Zhongshan University and Zhongshan University of Medical Sciences. Part of "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
