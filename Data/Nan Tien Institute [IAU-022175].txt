History
Created 2009. Graduated first cohort in 2012. A secular tertiary Institute, providing a range of courses designed to educate non-Buddhists, and the world’s growing Buddhist community. It caters for those interested in subjects that are connected to Buddhist values and culture, and whose careers link them with Buddhist philosophy, culture, practices and international relations.
