History
Created 1954 as Chongqing Middle School Teaching After-Hours Training Class. Became Chongqing Normal Training Institute 1978. Incorporated Chongqing First Normal School's Normal Training Class,Chonqing Second Normal School, Chongqing Third Normal School and renamed Chongqing Education College. Acquired present status and title 2012. Main administrative body: Chongqing Municipality
