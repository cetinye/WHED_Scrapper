History
Founded 1904 as Hunan Government Authorized Teachers’ Academy. Successively became Hunan Provincial 3rd Teachers School (1912), Hengyang Teachers College (1953), Hunan 3rd Teachers College (1962). Acquired present status following the merger between Hengyang Normal Junior College (founded 1958) and Hengyang Educational Training College ( founded 1979). Incorporated Hunan 3rd Teachers School 2001. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
