History
Created 1919 as Borys Grinchenko Pedagogical Institute. Became Borys Grinchenko Kyiv City Pedagogical University in 2002. Became Borys Grinchenko Kyiv University in 2007.
Borys Grinchenko Kyiv Metropolitan University was formed through the merger of Borys Grinchenko Kyiv University and Municipal Higher Education Institution 'Kyiv Academy of Arts' in 2023.
