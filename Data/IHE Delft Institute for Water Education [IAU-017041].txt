History
Created in 1957 as the International Course in Hydraulic Engineering, became International Institute for Hydraulic and Environmental Engineering (IHE) in 1976 and in 2003 the UNESCO-IHE Institute for Water Education. Acquired current title and status in 2017. Today, IHE Delft has developed into one of the largest graduate water education facilities in the world
