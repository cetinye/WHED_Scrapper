History
Founded 1471 by Bull of Pope Sixtus IV, degrees recognized by Emperor Maximilian I 1496. Acquired university status and reorganized 1773. Under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Universities and Scientific and Technological Research, but enjoys administrative autonomy. Financially supported by the State
