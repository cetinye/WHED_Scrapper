History
Founded 1958 as Hunan Forestry College. Moved to Guangzhou where it Became Central South Forestry College 1963. Became Guangdong Farm and Forestry College 1970. Moved to Hunan where it was renamed as Hunan Forestry College 1974. Became Central South Forestry College 1978. Acquired present title 1978. Acquired present status and title 2005. Incorporated the Hunan College of Economics and Management for Cadres 2006. Main administrative body: Hubei Province
