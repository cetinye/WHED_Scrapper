History
Founded as Geneva College 1822 (renamed Hobart College 1852) and William Smith School for Women 1906 - where the first class was enrolled 1908, two separate colleges sharing the same faculty, facilities and administration yet taking classes separately. The strict separation eroded gradually as it became increasing impractical to enforce. First joint commencement held 1922, though baccalaureate services remained separate until 1942. William Smith College was elevated from its original status as a department of Hobart College to that of an independent college, on equal footing with Hobart 1943
