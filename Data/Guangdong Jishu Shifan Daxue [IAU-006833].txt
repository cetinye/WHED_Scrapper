History
Founded 1957 as Guangdong College of Ethnic Minorities. Became Guangdong Polytehcnic Normal College 2002. Incorporated Guangdong Machinery School, Guangdong Economics and Administration Cadres College 2002, Guangdong Finance, Trade and Administration College 2005. Acquired present title 2018.
