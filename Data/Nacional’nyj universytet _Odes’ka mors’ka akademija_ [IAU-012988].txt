History
Founded 1944as The Odessa Higher Marine School (OHMS). Renamed the Odessa HIgher Engineering Marine School (OHEMS) 1958, transformed into the Odessa State Maritime Academy (OSMA) 1991, acquired "National Status" and the name of the Odessa National Maritime Academy (ONMA) 2002, acquired current status and title 2016
