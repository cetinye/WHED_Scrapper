History
Following decree n° 2019-209 of March 20, 2019, the University of Paris replaced the universities Paris-V (Paris Descartes) and Paris-VII (Paris Diderot) as of January 1, 2020, and integrated the Institute of Physics of the Globe of Paris (IPGP). It was responsible for all activities previously managed by Paris-V and Paris-VII and shared certain responsibilities with the Institute of Physics of the Globe of Paris, as outlined in its statutes. The Institute remained an institution-component. In March 2022, the University of Paris was officially renamed Université Paris-Cité.
