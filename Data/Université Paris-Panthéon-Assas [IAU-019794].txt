History
Founded in 1957, ISIT Paris is a non-profit higher education institution which grants government-accredited master’s degrees. Graduates pursue a wide range of careers all over the world in the marketing, communications, human resources, sales and purchasing departments of international companies, and as translators, conference interpreters and lawyer-linguists for international organizations including the European Union, NATO, the OECD and the United Nations. ISIT has signed collaborative agreements with high performing schools and universities both in France and abroad.Became in 2015 "Etablissement d'enseignement supérieur prive d'intérêt général (EESPIG)". In 2022, ISIT becomes a component institution of Paris-Panthéon-Assas University

Course/Programme : Conference Interpreting
Fields of study:
Translation and Interpretation
More details:
In the global context of contemporary politics and world economics, there is a need for high-level multilingual communication. ISIT's Master in Conference Interpreting meets international organizations' demands.
Course/Programme : Intercultural Communication and Translation
Fields of study:
Communication Studies, Management, Translation and Interpretation
More details:
At the end of the Master's course, our graduates know how to translate in an intercultural and multidisciplinary environment and have consolidated their expertise in digital multilingual communication.
Course/Programme : Intercultural Digital Strategy
Fields of study:
Communication Studies, Cultural Studies, Graphic Design
More details:
At the end of the program, our students know how to innovate in a multilingual web environment, have reinforced their multimedia creativity as well as their project management skills.
Course/Programme : Intercultural Management
Fields of study:
Human Resources, Leadership, Management, Marketing
More details:
At the end of their Master's course, students will have developed their international agility; know how to manage in an intercultural environment and have become experts in project management.
Course/Programme : International Strategy and Diplomacy
Fields of study:
International Relations and Diplomacy
More details:
At the end of the graduate course, students will be able to interact in a multilingual geopolitical environment and have become experts in project management in a multilateral context.
Course/Programme : Lawyer-Linguist
Fields of study:
Comparative Law, International Law
More details:
Our Lawyer-Linguist program is organized in partnership with leading law schools (double degree). ISIT provides language and intercultural training as students complete a law degree in parallel
