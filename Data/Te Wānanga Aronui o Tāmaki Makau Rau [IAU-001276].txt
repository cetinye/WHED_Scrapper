History
Founded 1895 as Auckland Technical School. Renamed Auckland Technical College 1906 and Seddon Memorial Technical College 1913. Separated into two institutions - a technical high school and a technical institute 1960 which was renamed Auckland Technical Institute (ATI) 1963. Became Auckland Institute of Technology (Te Whare Takiura o Tamaki Makau Rau) 1989. Acquired present status and title 2000
