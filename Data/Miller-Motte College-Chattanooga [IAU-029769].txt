History
Founded 2002 as Miller-Motte Technical College in Chattanooga, as part of the Delta Educational Systems, Inc. system of schools. Miller-Motte Technical College was purchased by STVT-AAI Education Inc., dba Ancora Education 2018 and received accreditation by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). Miller-Motte Technical College changed it's name to Miller-Motte College 2020, Chattanooga campus re-designated as the institution's main campus 2021
