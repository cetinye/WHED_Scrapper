History
Founded 1705 as Qiongtai Academy, reorganized and renamed Qiongzhou Prefecture Middle School 1902 with a normal school attached. Later successively renamed Guangdong Provincial Sixth Normal School, Guangdong Qiongtai Normal School, "Hainan Normal School and Hainan Qiongtai Normal School. Merged with Hainan Lingao Normal School and upgraded into Qiongtai Normal College 2004. Acquired current status and title 2016
