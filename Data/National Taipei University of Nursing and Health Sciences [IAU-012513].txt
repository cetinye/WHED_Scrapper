History
Founded 1954 as as Taiwan Provincial Junior College of Nursing. Merged with Taipei Senior Vocational School of Medicine and renamed Taiwan Provincial Vocational School of Nursing and Midwifery 1963. Renamed National Taipei College of Nursing 1994. Acquired present title 2010.
