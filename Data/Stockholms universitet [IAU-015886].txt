History
Founded 1878 as a private higher education institution, became State University in 1960. Lärarhögskolan i Stockholm (Stockholm Institute of Education) is now part of Stockholm University
Academic Year
August to June (August-January; January-June)
Admission Requirements
Secondary school certificate or foreign equivalent, or aged at least 25 yrs with 4 yrs work experience and knowledge of English. Admission to all programmes and courses is restricted
