History
Created 1956 as the Sichuan Training Class for Middle School Teachers. Successively became Neijiang Normal Vocational College, Neijiang Vocational College. It closed down 1962. Reopened 1978 as Neijiang Normal Vocational College, became Neijiang Normal Junior College 1992. Merged with Neijiang Education College (founded 1905) to shape the present university 2005. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
