History
Founded 1931 as Health School of Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army. Merged with Shenyang Medical School (formerly known as the Manchuria Medical College established by the Japanese South Manchuria Railway in 1911) and the former private Liaoning Medical College (formerly known as Shengjing Medical College, founded by the United Presbyterian Church of Scotland in 1883) 1948. Acquired present title 1940. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
