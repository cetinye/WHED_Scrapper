History
Founded 1938 as the School of Agriculture of National Yunnan University. Separated from Yunnan University and renamed Kunming Agriculture and Forestry College (KAFC) 1958. Incorporated Diannan University, Dianxi University 1962. Incorporated Yunnan Agriculture Labour University, acquired present status and title 1971. Under the administration of Yunnan Province
