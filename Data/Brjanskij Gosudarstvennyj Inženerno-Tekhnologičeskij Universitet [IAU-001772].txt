History
Founded 1930 as Institute of Forestry Engineering, became Brjanskiy Tekhnologičeskiy Institut (Bryansk Technical Institute) in 1960, Brjanskaja Gosudarstvennaja Inženerno-Tekhnologičeskaja Akademija (Bryansk State Engineering Academy) in 1995, and acquired current title and status 2015
