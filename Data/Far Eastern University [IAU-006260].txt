History
Founded 1928 as Institute of Accountancy and Business Administration, consolidated with Far Eastern College and incorporated under present title 1933. East Asia College of Information Technology (EACIT). FEU took over East Asia College of Information Technology (EACIT) and merged it with its own College of Engineering to form FEU - East Asia College (FEU - EAC) 2002.
