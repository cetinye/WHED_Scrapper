History
Founded 2020. Institut Agro is made up of two internal schools : Agrocampus Ouest (Institut national supérieur des sciences agronomiques, agroalimentaires, horticoles et du paysage - 2008), and Montpellier SupAgro (Institut national d'études supérieures agronomiques de Montpellier - 2015). It is a public scientific, cultural and professional establishment (EPSCP)
