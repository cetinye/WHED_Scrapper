History
Created 1950 as Northeastern Bank Heilongjiang Branch Training Class for Cadres. Successively became China People's Bank Qiqihar School for Cadres, Heilongjiang Bank School, Harbin Postsecondary Training School of Finance, Harbin Junior College of Finance. Acquired present status and title 2010. Main administrative body: Heilongjiang Province
