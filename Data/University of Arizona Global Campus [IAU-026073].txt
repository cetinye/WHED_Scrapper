History
Founded as Mt. St. Clare College in 1918. Became the Franciscan University in 2002, then the Franciscan University of the Prairies in 2004, Ashford University in 2005. Acquired current title and status in 2020 when it was acquired by the University of Arizona, to become an independent, accredited, online institution affiliated with the University of Arizona.
