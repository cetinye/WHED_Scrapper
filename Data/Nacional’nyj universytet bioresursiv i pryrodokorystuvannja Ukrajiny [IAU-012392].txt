History
Founded 1898 as Department of Agriculture at Kyiv Polytechnic Institute (KPI). Structure transformed to Kyiv Agricultural Institute 1923. Was formerly known as the Ukrainian Agricultural Academy then became The National Agricultural University of Ukraine in 1992. Acquired current title in 2008
