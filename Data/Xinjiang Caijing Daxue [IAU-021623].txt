History
Founded 1950 as Xinjiang Provincial Government Training School for Cadres. Successively became Xinjiang College of Finance and Economics 1959, Xinjiang School of Finance and Economics 1962, Xinjiang Institute of Finance and Economics 1980. Acquired present status 2000 following merger of Xinjiang School of Finance and Taxation and Xinjiang Economic and Management College. Acquired present title 2007. Under the administration of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region
