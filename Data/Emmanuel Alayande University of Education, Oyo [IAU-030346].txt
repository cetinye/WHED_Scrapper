History
Founded 1896 as St. Andrew’s College, Oyo - a Grade II Teacher’s College - by the Church Missionary Society (C.M.S.). Upgraded into a campus of the then Oyo State College of Education, Ilesa 1980. Supervision transferred to the Oyo State College of Education, Ila Orangun 1983. Gained full autonomy as a full-fledged College of Education 1985. Successively renamed: St. Andrew’s College of Education, Oyo; Oyo State College of Education, Oyo; and Emmanuel Alayande College of Education, Oyo 2008. Relocated to its present site 2003. Acquired current status and title 2022
