History
Founded 1996 as Libero Istituto Universitario "San Pio V". The Istituto has a distinctive network structure comprising the Universities of Bologna, Firenze, Milano-Bicocca, Napoli "Federico II", Napoli "L'Orientale", Napoli "Suor Orsola Benincasa", Roma "La Sapienza" and Siena. In March 2013, the University adopted the name of 'International University of Rome-UNINT"
