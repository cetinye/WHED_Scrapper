History
Founded 1950 as Yunnan Public Security Junior College. Successively became People Liberation Army Martial Law Commission Public Security Department's Public Security School, Yunnan People's Government Public Security Bureau's Public Security School, Yunnan Politics and Law Cadres School, Yunnan Politics College, Yunnan Public Security School, Yunnan People Police School, Yunnan Public Security Junior College. Became vocational secondary school 1978, then college for professional training 1984. Acquired present status and title 2003. Under the administration of Yunnan Province
