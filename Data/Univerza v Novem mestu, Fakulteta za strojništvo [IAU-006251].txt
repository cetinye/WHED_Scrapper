History
Founded 2006 as Visoka šola za tehnologije in sisteme (High School for Technologies and Systems). Renamed Fakulteta za visoke tehnologije in sisteme, (Faculty of Technologies and Systems) 2014. Now Faculty of Mechanical Engineering (FTS). Merged with 3 other faculties: Economics and Informatics (FUPI); Faculty of Health Sciences Novo mesto (FZV); and Business and Administrative Sciences (FPUV), to become University of Novo Mesto, 2017
