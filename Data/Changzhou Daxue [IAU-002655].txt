History
Founded 1978 as the Changzhou and Wuxi Branches of the Nanjing Institute of Chemical Engineering. Became Jiangsu Institute of Chemical Engineering 1981. Became Jiangsu Institute of Petrochemical Engineering 1992. Renamed as Jiangsu Polytechnic University in 2002. Acquired present title 2010. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Department of Education
