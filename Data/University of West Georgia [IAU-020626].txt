Admission Requirements
Graduation from high school or equivalent, and College Board SAT or ACT examination. TOEFL test for foreign students and evidence of financial solvency. The College waives non-resident tuition for a number of international students who meet the following minimum criteria: TOEFL Score of 550, SAT-verbal score of 480, and SAT-math score of 490
