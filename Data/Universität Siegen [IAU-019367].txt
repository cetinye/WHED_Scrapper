History
Founded 1972 as Gesamthochschule, incorporating former Technical College, Siegen-Gummersbach and branch of College of Education, Westfalen-Lippe. Became University 1980. An autonomous institution under the jurisdiction of and financially supported by the State of North Rhine-Westphalia
