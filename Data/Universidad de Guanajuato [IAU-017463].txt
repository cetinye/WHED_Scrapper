History
Created in 1732, Universidad de Guanajuato is a public comprehensive institution based in Guanajuato. The university traces its history back to the Hospice of the Holy Trinity, which was established in 1732. In 1945, the institution became the University of Guanajuato, nowadays It offers programs at the high school, undergraduate, and graduate levels.
