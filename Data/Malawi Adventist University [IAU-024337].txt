History
Founded 2000 by turning the Lakeview Seminary into the Malawi Adventist College (MAC), a Junior College temporarily closed 2002, reopened 2004 and affiliated to the University of Eastern Africa, Baraton (Kenya), started offering its own degrees 2018. Owned and operated by the Malawi Union Conference of Seventh-day Adventists
