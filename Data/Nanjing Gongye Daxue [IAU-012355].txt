History
Founded 1958. Renamed Nanjing University of Chemical Technology 1995. Became Nanjing University of Technology in 2001 following merger with Nanjing Institute of Architectural and Civil Engineering and Nanjing University of Chemical Technology. Acquired present title 2011. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
Admission Requirements
Foreign students:
no criminal record, aged 18 or above, in good health condition and in accordance with the Health Standard for International Students stipulated by the Ministry of Education, China. Good moral character and discipline towards the laws and regulations of the Chinese government and the university, respect Chinese customs and habits.Good academic performance, English or Chinese language proficiency. Certificate from highest education attained.
