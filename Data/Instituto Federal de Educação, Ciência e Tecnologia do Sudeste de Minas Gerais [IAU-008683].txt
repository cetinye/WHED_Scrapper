History
Founded 1964 as Ginásio Agrícola de Rio Pomba. Became Colégio Agrícola de Rio Pomba 1968. Became Escola Agrotécnica Federal de Rio Pomba - MG 1979. Transformed into Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica de Rio Pomba 2002. Acquired present status and title 2008
