History
Founded 1995 following the merger between Foshan University (founded 1958 as Foshan Normal Vocational College) and Foshan Farm and Husbandry Vocational College (founded 1958 as the Foshan branch of the South China Agricultural College). Incorporated Foshan Workers Medical College and Foshan Education College 2005. Under the jurisdiction of Guangdong Province
