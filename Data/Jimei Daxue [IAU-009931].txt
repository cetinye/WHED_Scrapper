History
Founded 1913 by Chen Jiageng (1874-1961) as the Teaching Department of the Jimei School and the Jimei School of Fishery and Commerce sections (1920). Acquired present title 1994 following merger between Jimei Maritime Navigation College, Xiamen Fishery College, Fujian Physical Education College, Jimei Finance and Economics Higher Vocational School of Fujian and Jimei Normal Higher Vocational School. Main administrative body: Fujian Province
