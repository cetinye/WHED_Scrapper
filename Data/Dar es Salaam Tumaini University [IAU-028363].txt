History
Founded 1997 as out of the Waldorf College Tanzania (WCT) (established as a branch and later a mission of Waldorf College, Forest City in Iowa, USA). Became Tumaini University Dar es Salaam College (TUDARCo) 2003, a constituent college of the Tumaini University Makumira (TUMa), owned by Eastern and Coastal Diocese of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in Tanzania (ELCT). Acquired current status and title 2024
