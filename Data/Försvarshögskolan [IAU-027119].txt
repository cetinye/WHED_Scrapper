History
Founded 1997 through merger of Försvarshögskolan (founded 1952) and the Military Academy ("Militärhögskolan (MHS)" as an institution for higher military education within the Swedish Armed Forces. Became an independent higher education institution that offers education at all academic levels for civilian students as well as military officers 2008
