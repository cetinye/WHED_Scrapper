History
Founded 1938 as National Teachers College. Merged into Hunan University 1950. After Hunan University closed down, it became Hunan Normal Vocational College 1953. Acquired present status and title 1984. Incorporated Hunan Education College, Hunan Politics, Law and Administration College for Cadres, Hunan Medical Vocational College 2000. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
