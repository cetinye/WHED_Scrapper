History
Founded 1952 as East China School of Finance. Became Ministry of Finance Shanghai Cadre School of Finance and Administration 1956. Became Shanghai City Cadre School of Finance, Administration and Trade 1958. Incorporated several schools and became Shanghai City Higher Vocational School of Business 1994. Acquired present status and title 2004. Main administrative body: Shanghai Municipality
Admission Requirements
Foreign Students: Non-Chinese citizen in good health; High school graduates younger than 35 years old; 2. Holding HSK 3 certificate or above acquired in the latest two years; 3. Having a sound art training background for art majors such as Artistic Design, Digital Media Art, Animation, and Technology and Art of Exhibition. Main administrative body: Shanghai Municipality
