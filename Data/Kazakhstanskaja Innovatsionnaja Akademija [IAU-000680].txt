History
Created 1992 as Agro-technical Institute. Became Kazakh Finance-Economic Academy (Kazahskaja Finansovo-Ekonomičeskaja Akademija) in 2008, and acquired current title and status in 2013. Was also known as Kazakhstan Innovative University (Kazakhstanskij Innovatsionnyj Universitet).
