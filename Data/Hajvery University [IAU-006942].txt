History
Founded 1992 as Hajvery College. Acquired present status 2002. Over time HU has developed 8 Faculties and world-class infrastructure in the Lahore & Sheikhupura cities of Pakistan. HU's partner universities are based in Europe, the Far East and North America to promote the mobility of students and teachers for research and education.
