History
Founded 1991 as Baškirskaja Kommerčeskaja Akademija (Bashkir Commercial Academy), became Baškirskaja Akademija Upravlenija (Bashkir Academy of Management) in 1993, became Baškirskaja Akademija Gosudarstvennoj Služby i Upravlenija pri Prezidente Respubliki Baškorstortan (Bashkir Academy of Public Administration and Management under the Auspices of the President of Bashkortostan Republic) in 1994, and acquired current title in 2015
