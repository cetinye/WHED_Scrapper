History
Founded 1816 as Tsisar-Royal Real School in Lviv. Successively renamed Tsisar-Royal School of Technical Sciences and Trade in Lviv (1825), Tsisar-Royal Real-Trade Academy in Lviv (1835), Tsisar-Royal Technical Academy (1844), Tsisar-Royal Higher Technical School in Lviv (1877), Tsisar-Royal Polytechnic School in Lviv (1894), Lviv Polytechnic (1921), Lviv Polytechnic Institute (1939), Professional Technical Courses in Lviv (1942), Lviv Polytechnic Institute (1944), Order of Lenin Lviv Polytechnic Institute (1967), Order of Lenin Lviv Polytechnic Institute named after Lenin Komsomol (1978), Lviv Polytechnic State University (1993). Acquired current title 2000
