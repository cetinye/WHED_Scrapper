History
Founded 2001 as Chonqing Business College Rongzhi College by Chongqing Business College. Chongqing Business College merged with Yuzhou University to form Chongqing Technology and Business University 2002. Therefore, Chonqing Business College Rongzhi College became Rongzhi College of Chongqing Technology and Business University. Acquired present status 2003.
