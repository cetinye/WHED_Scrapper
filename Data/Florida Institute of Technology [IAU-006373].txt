History
Founded in 1958 as Brevard Engineering College, Florida Institute of Technology (Florida Tech) acquired its current status and title in 1966. Florida Tech is a private research university located in Melbourne, Florida with four colleges: College of Engineering & Science, College of Psychology & Liberal Arts, College of Business, College of Aeronautics.
