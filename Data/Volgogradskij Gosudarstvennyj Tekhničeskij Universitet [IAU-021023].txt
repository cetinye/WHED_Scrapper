History
Founded 1930, acquired present status and title 1993. Was reorganized in 2015 and integrated Volgograd State University of Architecture and Civil Engineering (Volgogradskij Gosudarstvennyj Arkhitekturno-Stroitelnyj Universitet) whcih became the Institute of Architecture and Construction within the university
