History
Founded 1858 as LaGrange College based in LaGrange, Missouri. Merged with a Baptist college at Hannibal and renamed Hannibal-LaGrange College 1928, consolidated with Missouri Baptist College of St. Louis 1967 and operated as two campuses: Missouri Baptist College-Hannibal-LaGrange Campus and the Missouri Baptist College-St. Louis Campus. Separated and reestablished as Hannibal-LaGrange College 1973, accredited as a four-year institution 1975, acquired current status and title 2010. Affiliated with the Missouri Baptist Convention (Southern Baptist)
