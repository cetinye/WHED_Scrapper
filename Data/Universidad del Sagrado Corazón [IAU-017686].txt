History
Founded 1880 as Colegio del Sagrado Corazón, also known as el Pensionado (the boarding school), an elementary school dedicated to the education of girls. Transformed into Colegio Universitario del Sagrado Corazón (CUSC) 1935, became co-ed 1972, acquired current status and title 1976
