History
Founded 1786 as National School, reorganized as Pedagogical Institute 1948, became Kalužskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet im. K.E. Tsiolkovskogo (Kaluga State Pedagogical University named after K.E. Tsiolkovskij) in 1994 and acquired current title and status in 2010
