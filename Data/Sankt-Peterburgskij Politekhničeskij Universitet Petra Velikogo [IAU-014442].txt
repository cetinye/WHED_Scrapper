History
Founded 1899 as St. Petersburg Politechnic Institute. Was also known as Saint-Petersburg State Polytechnic University (Sankt-Peterburgskij Gosudarstvennyj Politekhničeskij Universitet). Acquired current title 2016 following a reorganisation and merger with Sankt-Peterburgskij Torgovo-Ekonomičeskij Institut (Saint-Petersburg Institute of Trade and Economics), created in 1930 as Leningrad Institute of Soviet Trade (LIST)
