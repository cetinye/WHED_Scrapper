History
Founded 1909 as Jiaozuo Coal Mining School. Successively became Fuzhong Mining Business University, Jiaozuo Private College, National North West College of Technology, JIaozuo Mining Industry College, Jiaozuo College of Technology. Acquired present title 2004. Main administrative body: Henan Province
