History
Founded 1826. Revolutionized university education in 19th century United Kingdom by widening access to social and religious groups previously denied entry. Incorporated in the University of London 1907. A Constituent College of the University from 1977. Integrated the Institute of Education, University of London" as a single-faculty school called the UCL Institute of Education (IOE) 2014. University College London is a Member Institution of the Federal University of London

Admission Requirements
General Certificate of Education (GCE) or recognized equivalent, with good passes in 3 approved subjects at Advanced ('A') level; plus one subject at Advanced Subsidiary ('AS') level. In addition, candidates to show evidence of broad general education, eg, GCSE or equivalent, with passes at not less than Grade C in Mathematics and English Language plus 3 other subjects. Further information may be obtained from UCL College Admissions Office
