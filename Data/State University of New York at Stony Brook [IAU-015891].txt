History
Founded 1957 at Planting Fields, Oyster Bay as a college for the preparation of secondary school teachers of mathematics and science. Moved to Stony Brook 1962. Classified by Carnegie Foundation as one of the nation's 70 leading Research Institutes. Is part of the State University of New York (SUNY) system
