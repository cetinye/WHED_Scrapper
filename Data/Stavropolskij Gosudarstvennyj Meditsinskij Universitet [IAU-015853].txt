History
Founded 1938 as Vorošilovskij Gosudarstvennyj Meditsinskij Institut (Voroshilovsk State Medical Institute), became Stavropolskij Gosudarstvennyj Meditsinskij Institut (Stavropol State Medical Institute) in 1943, Stavropolskaja Gosudarstvennaja Meditsinskaja Akademija (Stavropol State Medical Academy) in 1994, and acquired current title and status 2013
