History
Founded 1883 as a seminary (high school) under the Wesleyan Methodist Connection, first diploma awarded 1901. Renamed Houghton College 1923 as it received its provisional charter from New York State. Awarded its first 19 baccalaureate degrees 1925. A permanent charter granted 1927, and accreditation by the Middle States Association of Schools and Colleges 1935. Acquired current status and title 2022
