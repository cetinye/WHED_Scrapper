History
Founded 1948 as Zhongyuan University. Hubei University 1958. Renamed Hubei Training School of Finance and Economics 1971. Separated into Zhongnan University of Finance and Economics 1985, Zhongnan Institute of Politics and Law 1984. Acquired present status and title 2000 resluting from the merger of the two higher institutions. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
