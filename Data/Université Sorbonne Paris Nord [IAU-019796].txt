History
Founded 1970 as one of the thirteen universities Universities replacing the former Université de Paris (founded in 12th century) under the 1968 law reforming higher education. Successively named "Université Paris XIII", "Université Paris-Nord", "Université Paris 13 Paris Nord", then "Université Paris 13" (name adopted in 2014. Renamed "Université Sorbonne Paris Nord" since January 1, 2020. Member of the COMUE Université Sorbonne Paris Cité (Décree n° 2014-1680 of 29 décembre 2014, modified), and also member of Campus Condorcet
