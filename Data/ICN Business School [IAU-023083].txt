History
Founded in 1905 as 'Institut Commercial de Nancy' by the University of Nancy and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Meurthe-et-Moselle. Became 'ICN Business School' in 2003. The Artem campus is shared with Mines Nancy and the Nancy School of Arts and Design
