History
Founded 1858 as Athens Female College. Became a male college called the East Tennessee Wesleyan College 1967, became coeducational and renamed East Tennessee Wesleyan University 1868, renamed Grant Memorial University 1886, merged with Chattanooga University 1889 to form U.S. Grant Memorial University, split from Chattanooga to become a junior college called Tennessee Wesleyan College 1925, became a liberal arts college 1957, acquired current status and title 2016
