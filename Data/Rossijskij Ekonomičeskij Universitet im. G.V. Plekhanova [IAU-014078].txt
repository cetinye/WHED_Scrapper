History
Founded 1907 as Moscow Commerce Institute, became Rossijskaja Ekonomičeskaja Akademija im. G.V. Plehanova (Russian Academy of Economics named after G.V. Plehanov) in 1991. Acquired current title 2010. In 2015 merged with Russian State University of Trade and Economics (Rossijskij Gosudarstvennyj Torgovo-Economičeskij Universitet), Moscow State University of Economics, Statistics and Informatics (Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Ekonomiki, Statistiki i Informatiki) and Saratov State Socio-Economic University (Saratovskij Gosudarstvennyj Socialno-Ekonomičeskij Universitet)
