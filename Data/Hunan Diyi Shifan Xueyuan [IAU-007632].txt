History
Founded 1903 as Hunan Normal School. Became Public Hunan First Normal School 1912. Merged with Hunan Provincial 4th Normal School, becoming Hunan Provincial First Normal School 1914. Upgraded into a normal vocational college 2000. Acquired present status and title 2008. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
