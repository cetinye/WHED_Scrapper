History
Founded 1897 by the Northern Presbyterian Church of the United States and became 4-year College 1905. Merged with Tae Jon College and became Soong Jun University 1971. Tae Jon College separated from the main campus 1983, and the School restored to its original name of Soong Sil 1987. A private institution.
