History
Founded 1970 as Khabarovskij Institut Narodnogo Khozyjajstva (Khabarovsk Institute of National Economy), became Khabarovskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Ekonomiki i Prava (Khabarovsk State Academy of Economics and Law) in 1994 following merger with the Faculty of Law of Moscow State Law Academy, and Faculty of Commerce of Moscow Commercial University, and acquired current title and status 2015
