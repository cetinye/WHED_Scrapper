History
Founded 1991 officially recognized by Administrative Rule No. 794/91 of August 9. Evaluation and Quality Assurance Office created January 2008, and the International Relations Office and the Professional Insertion Office created 2005 and 2000 respectively. Centre for Studies and Applied Research (CEIA) created February 2010; and International Center for Studies and Research in Graphic Technologies (TGRAF) created July 2016; The Civil Protection and Safety Observatory, created December 2013; The Optometry Clinic created October 2014; and the Center for Engineering Studies, created Dec 2015. Located on the Lumiar Academic Campus
