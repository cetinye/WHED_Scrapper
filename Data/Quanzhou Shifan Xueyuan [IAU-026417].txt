History
Founded 1958 as Quanzhou University Normal College. Restored as Quanzhou Normal Postsecondary Training School 1981. Quanzhou Education College, Quanzhou Normal School merged into it 1998. Acquired present status and title 2000. Incorporated Nan'an Normal School 2001. Main administrative body: Fujian Province
