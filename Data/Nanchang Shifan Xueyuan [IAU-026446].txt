History
Established 1952 as Jiangxi Provincial Middle School Education Normal Training School. Successively became Nanchang Normal Postsecondary School 1956, Jiangxi Education College 1958. Merged with Jiangxi University's Humanities Section and became part of the Jinggangshan University and Jiangxi Normal College 1969. Jiangxi Education College was restored 1979. Acquired present status and title 2013. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Province
