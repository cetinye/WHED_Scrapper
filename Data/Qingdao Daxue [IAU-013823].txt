History
Created as Qingdao Technical Advanced School 1909 and Qingdao Private University 1924. The latter successively became National Qingdao University, National Shandong University. Through the merger with Qingdao Medical Technical School became Qingdao Medical College 1956. Acquired present title 1985. Acquired present status 1993 through the merger of Qingdao University and Shandong Textile Engineering College (1950), Qingdao Medical College and Qingdao Teachers College (1951). Main administrative body: Shandong Province
