History
Founded 1949 as Shanghai Russian College (PRC first foreign language school). Renamed Shanghai Foreign Language College, affiliated to East China People’s Revolution University 1950. Renamed Shanghai Foreign Language Institute 1956. Acquired present status and title 1994. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
