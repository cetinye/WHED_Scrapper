History
Founded 1921 as Faculty of Dentistry wihin Kharkiv Medical Academy, became Kharkiv Dental Institute (Harkivs'kij stomatolohičnij instytut) in 1931, Poltavs’kij Medičnij stomatolohičnij instytut (Poltava Medical Dental Institute) in 1967, Ukrajins’ka medyčna stomatolohična akademija (Ukrainian Medical Stomatological Academy) in 1994 and acquired current title and status in 2021
