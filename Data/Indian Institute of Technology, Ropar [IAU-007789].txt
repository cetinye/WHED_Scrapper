History
The Indian Institute of Technology Ropar is one of the eight IITs set up by the Government of India, Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) (now Ministry of Education) in 2008. The institute is committed to provide state-of-the-art technical education in a variety of fields, and also to facilitate transmission of knowledge using the latest developments in pedagogy. The Indian Institute of Technology Ropar notified under the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961 vide gazette notification no. 59 of 1961. The Institute has been declared as an Institute of national importance.
