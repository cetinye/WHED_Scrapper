History
Founded in 1924 as the Department of Education of Yangon University, became a Teacher's Training College in 1931, a constituent college of Yangon University, then became the Faculty of Educatin of Yangon University in 1946. It acquired independent status as Yangon Institute of Education in 1964 and finally acquired its current title and university status in 2014.
