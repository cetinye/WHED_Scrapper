History
Founded 1920. Received fourth level of accreditation (the highest one) 1996, acquired National status 2000, received the name of Volodymyr Dahlin 2001, started research activity 2010.
One of the first universities in Ukraine to join the Magna Charta of the European Universities in Bologna (2004) before Ukraine joined the Bologna process (2005). Was awarded a Silver Stella in the nomination “Quality of the Third Millennium” 2001. The Association of Business Executives of Oxford proved that the University was working according to European standards 2002. Received the title “The Leader of Modern Education” 2005-2009. Received the award for the first place in the National Inventors Competition “Invention-2014” and “Invention-2015” in the category “New Substances and Materials”. Awards received include: a gold medal in the nomination “Modern Programs, Innovative Approaches and Solutions to Improve the Quality of Education” 2015, as well as the certificate “Quality of Publications”; the Grand Prix “Leader of Research and Development Activity” At VII International Exhibition “Modern Education Institutions” 2016, received the certificate “Quality of Publications”, and a gold medal in the nomination “Psychological Support for Children and Youth in Difficult Situations” (that also won “Invention of the Year” competition"); a diploma and a gold medal in the nomination “Modern Socio-Psychological and Pedagogical Technologies of Support of Children and Adults under Conditions of Crisis Situation” 2017; Also received the certificate “Quality of Publications”; the Grand Prix “Leader of Research and Development Activity” at the International Exhibition “Modern Education Institutions” 2018, and a gold medal in the nomination “Implementing Innovations into the Educational Process to improve the Quality of Graduates’ Knowledge”. An active member of many international associations, including Eurasian Universities Association, European Universities Association (EUA), International Association of Universities (IAU), The Association of the Carpathian Region Universities (ACRU)
