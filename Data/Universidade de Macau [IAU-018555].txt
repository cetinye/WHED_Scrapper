History
Founded in 1981, as University of East Asia, a private institution. The University of Macau (UM) is since 1988 a public international university located in Macau. Acquired present status and title 1991. It has a multicultural campus and a global education system supported by residential faculties and colleges within an international education structure. Eighty percent of its faculty members are from outside Macau. With English as the main medium of instruction, the University is committed to producing creative and socially responsible graduates with a global mindset and international competitiveness
