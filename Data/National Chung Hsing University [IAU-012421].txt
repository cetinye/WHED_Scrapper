History
Founded 1919 as Academy of Agriculture and Forestry, became part of Japanese Taihoku Imperial University 1928, transferred to Taichung as independent institution 1942, re-established as State institution 1945 and became Provincial College of Agriculture 1946 and university 1961. Acquired present title 1971.
