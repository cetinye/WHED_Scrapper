History
Founded 1921. Incorporated as a Central University 1951. A Centre of Culture where research into and study of Religious Literature, History, Science and Art of Hindu, Buddhist, Jain, Islamic, Sikh, Christian and other civilizations may be pursued with the culture of the West.
