History
First created in 1963 as Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College at Belgaum, and became KLE Academy of Higher Education and Research in 2006 as per the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India (u/s 3 of the UGC ACT 1956). The university offers programs - Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Medicine, Dentistry, Ayurveda, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Nursing and Homoeopathy.
