History
Founded 2014 at the initiative of Drs. Walid Slaybi and Ogarit Younan, AUNOHR is founded on the pillars of non-violent pedagogy, both in form and content. Received ministerial license 2015. AUNOHR has signed a protocol with the Lebanese University (Al Jamiat Al-Lubnaniya) allowing it to use certain parts of its buildings. AUNOHR is also building a major university campus worthy of the concept of non-violence and will be the first university campus to provide non-violent majors in Lebanon and the Arab world
