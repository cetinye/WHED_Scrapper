History
Created in 1980 as Brjanskij Sel'skohozjajstvennyj Institut (Bryansk Agricultural Institute). In 1995 became Brjanskaja Gosudarstvennaja Sel'skohozjajstvennaja Akademija (Bryansk State Academy of Agriculture). Acquired current title in 2014
