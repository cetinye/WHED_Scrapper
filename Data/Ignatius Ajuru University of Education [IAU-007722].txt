History
Founded 1971 as Advanced Teachers’ Training College, upgraded into Rivers State College of Education 1975 offering Nigeria Certificate in Education and as of 1982 a Bachelor of Education degree programme in affiliation with the University of Ibadan. Acquired autonomy and university status 2009, acquired current title 2010
