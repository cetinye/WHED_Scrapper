History
Founded 1896 as the Sichuan Sino-Western School under the rule of Qing Dynasty Emperor Guangxu. Following several mergers successively became National Sichuan University 1931, Sichuan Union University 1994 and Sichuan University 1998. Acquired present status through the merger with West China University of Medical Sciences 2000 (founded 1910 as West China Union University). Part of "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
