History
Founded 1965 as an Agricultural College. Transformed into a Faculty of Agriculture of University of Dar Es Salaam (UDSM) 1970. Renamed Faculty of Agriculture and Forestry 1974 and Faculty of Agriculture, Forestry and Veterinary Sciences 1976. Acquired current status and title 1984
Academic Year
August to July
Admission Requirements
Certificate of Secondary Education Examination, or East African Certificate of Education Ordinary ('O') level, or equivalent, with passes in 5 approved subjects obtained prior to sitting Advanced ('A') level with a total of not less than 4 points. Entrance examination, or East African Certificate of Education Advanced ('A') level, or equivalent. Two principal passes at 'A' level with a total of not less than 4 points. Entrance examination
