History
Founded 1921 as Moscow Institute of Civil Engineering named after V.V. Kuibyshev, acquired current title in 1993 and acquired status as a National Reasearch University in 2010 and was merged with Moscow State Academy of Municipal Services and Building (Moskovskaya Gosudarstvennaya Akademiya Kommunalnogo Khozyajstva i Stroitel'stva) in 2013
