History
Founded 1994 as Arrupe College. Was granted a provisional charter as a university by ZIMCHE 2017. Acquired current status and title 2018. An autonomous international academic institution, owned by the Southern Africa Province of the Society of Jesus on the behalf of the Jesuit Conference of Africa and Madagascar (JCAM)
