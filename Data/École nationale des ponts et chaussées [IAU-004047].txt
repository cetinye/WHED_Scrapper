History
Founded 1747 as Ecole Royale des Ponts et Chaussées. Acquired present status 1994. A public 'grande école'. Member of the COMUE Université Paris-Est (Decree n° 2015-156 of February 11, 2015). A Public Scientific, Cultural and Professional Establishment (EPSCP). Formerly known as Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (2008-2024)
