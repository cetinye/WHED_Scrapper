History
The INP (Institut national du patrimoine) is a higher education institution of the Ministry of Culture, created by decree n°90-406 of 16 May 1990. Its mission is to recruit, by competitive examination, and provide initial training for heritage curators from the State, the local civil service and the City of Paris, as well as to select, also by competitive examination, and train heritage restorers qualified to work on public collections. The training of these two closely complementary professions in the same institution is unique in Europe
