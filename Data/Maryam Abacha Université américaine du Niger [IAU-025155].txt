History
Founded 2013 and named after the former first lady of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, Hajiya Maryam Sani Abacha for her philanthropic gesture and contribution to the development of the African continent. It is the first English-language medium university in Niger Republic.
Academic Year
September to June (Sep-Dec; Jan-Mar; May-July)
Admission Requirements
Under Graduate: Five 'O’Level Credits in English Language, Mathematics and three relevant subjects: Science, Medical and Health Science Programmes (English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry and Physics), Social and Management Science Programmes (English Language, Mathematics, and three relevant subjects), Law (English Language, Mathematics, and three relevant Subjects).
Direct Entry (200 level): National Diploma (Upper/Lower Credit with five 'O’ Level credits in English Language, Mathematics and three relevant Subjects. 'A’Level Transcript in cases of transfer students.
Postgraduate:
Master’s Programme (MSc.): Bachelor of Science degree (Second Class Upper Level) in related fields from a recognized University.
Doctorate Degree (PhD): Master’s Degree (MSc.) or M. (Phil.) with research thesis component (not project). Candidates with a CGPA score less than 3.5 are not eligible.
