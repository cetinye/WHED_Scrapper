History
Founded 1902 as Sanjiang Normal School 1902. Successively became Liangjiang Normal School, Nanjing Higher Normal School, National Southeast University, Fourth Zhongshan University, National Central China University, National Nanjing University. Acquired present title 1950. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
