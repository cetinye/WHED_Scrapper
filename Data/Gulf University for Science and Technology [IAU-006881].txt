History
Created in 2000 by the Private Universities Decree, No. 34, issued by the State of Kuwait, resulting in the establishment of a temporary campus in Hawally. The Emiri Decree No. 156 in 2002 completed the legal establishment of GUST as the first private university in the State of Kuwait and permitted the start of the first academic year in 2002–2003.
