History
Founded 1971. Formerly known as Stavropolskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekhničeskij Universitet (Stavropol State Technical University). Became Severo-Kavkazskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekhničeskij Universitet (North Caucasus State Technical University) 1994. Acquired current title and status 2012 after merger between Stavropolskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet (Stavropol State University) and Pjatigorskij Gosudarstvennyj Gumanitarno-Tekhnologicheskij Universitet (Pyatigorsk State University for the Humanities and Technology)
