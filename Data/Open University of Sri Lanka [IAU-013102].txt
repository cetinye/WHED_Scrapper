History
Founded 1980 to enable those of 18 years of age and over to pursue courses mainly in their own time and in their own homes. The multimedia learning system developed by the British Open University has served as a model.
Admission Requirements
Three GCE 'A' level passes in relevant subjects may be required for undergraduate entry to Bachelor programmes.
