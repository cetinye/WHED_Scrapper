History
Founded 1833 as NewArk College, renamed Delaware College 1843. A women's college opened 1914 and in 1921, the two colleges joined to become the University of Delaware
Academic Year
September to May (September-December; February-May)
Admission Requirements
Graduation from high school or equivalent, and College Board SAT or ACT examination. TOEFL or ELPT test for foreign students. Minimum TOEFL score is 550 on the paper-based test and 213 on the computer-based test. Confidential Financial Form to verify available financial resources to cover cost of one year of education. Summary of Educational Experiences to describe all formal educational experiences of the applicant
