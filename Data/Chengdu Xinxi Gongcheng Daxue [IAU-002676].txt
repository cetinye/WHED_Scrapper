History
Created 1951 as the People Liberation Army Southwest Air Force Meteorology Cadres Training Unit. Successively became Central Bureau of Meteorology Chengdu Meteorology School for Cadres 1954, Central Bureau of Meteorology's Chengdu Meteorology School 1956, Chengdu Institute of Meteorology 1978, Chengdu College of Information Technology 2000. Incorporated National Bureau of Statistics' Sichuan Statistics School 2001. Acquired present title 2015. Main administrative body: Sichuan Province
