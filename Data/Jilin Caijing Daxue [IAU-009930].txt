History
Founded 1946 as the Northeast Bank Headquarters training class. Merger of Northeast School of Planning and Statistics, Northeast Technical School of Economics and Finance, Northeast Technical School of Banking and setting-up of the Northeast School of Economics and Finance 1952. Following several mergers, it became Changchun Taxation College 1992. Acquired present title 2010. Main administrative body: Jilin Province
