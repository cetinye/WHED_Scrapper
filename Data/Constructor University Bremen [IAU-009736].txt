History
Founded 1999 as International University Bremen with preliminary state recognition by the Free Hanseatic City of Bremen. Received institutional accreditation by the German Science and Humanities Council (“Wissenschaftsrat”, WR) and opened 2001. Received full state recognition and programmes accreditation by ACQUIN 2004. Renamed Jacobs University Bremen 2007, acquired current title 2022
