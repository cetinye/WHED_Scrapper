History
Created 1773 as Lviv Medical Collegium, became Imperial-Royal School of Midwives in Lviv in 1784, State Obstetrics School in Lviv in 1919, Lviv Medical and Obstetrics School in 1939, Lviv Medical School in 1967, Andrey Krupinsky Lviv Institute of Nursing and Laboratory Medicine in 2015 and acquired current academy status and title 2019.
