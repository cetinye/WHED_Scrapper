History
Founded 1952 as Shanghai Shipping Industry School. After several incorporations was relocated to Zhenjiang City and renamed Zhenjiang Shipping Industry School. Upgraded to College, renamed twice, respectively Zhenjiang Shipbuilding Institute and East China Shipbuilding Institute 1978, 1993. Incorporation of Jianghai Vocational Business School, the Sericulture Research Institute of Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences and acquired present title 2004. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
