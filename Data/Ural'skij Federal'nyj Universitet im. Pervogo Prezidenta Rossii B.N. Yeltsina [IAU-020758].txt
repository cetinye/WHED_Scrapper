History
Founded 1920 as Ural State Technical University (Uralskij Gosudarstvennyj Tehničeskij Universitet). Acquired current title and status 2011 after merger with Uralskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet im. A.M. Gorkogo (Ural State University named after A.M. Gorky)
