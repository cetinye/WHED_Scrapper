History
Founded as a school of singing in 1823. Became a conservatorium of music in 1932. Converted to private university and granted accreditation by the Austrian Accreditation Council (OAR) in 2004. Accreditation was renewed in 2014 by the Austrian Agency for Quality Assurance and Accreditation (Agentur für Qualitätssicherung und Akkreditierung Austria (AQ Austria). Today, the Anton Bruckner Private University is one of seven Austrian Universities for Music, Drama and Dance
