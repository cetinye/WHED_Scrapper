History
Founded 1952 as Nanjing Aeronautical Industry Vocational College. Became Nanjing Aeronautical College acquired present status 1956 (upgraded to a university-level institution). Acquired present title 1993. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Industry and Information Technology
