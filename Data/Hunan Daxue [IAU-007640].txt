History
Originating from Yuelu Academy founded in 976 A.D. It is the oldest institution in chinese history. Transformed into "Hunan Institute of Higher Education" in 1903. "Hunan Institute of Higher Education" was closed down, became "Hunan Senior Teachers School" in 1912. Replaced by "Hunan Public Poly-Technical College" in 1917. "Public Poly-Technical College", "Hunan Public College of Law and Politics" and "Hunan Public Business College" merged to shape the modern "Hunan University" in 1926. Upgraded 1937. Acquired present status 1959 (officially restored). Incorporated "Hunan University" and "Hunan College of Economics and Finance" in 2000, "Hunan Computer College" in 2002. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
