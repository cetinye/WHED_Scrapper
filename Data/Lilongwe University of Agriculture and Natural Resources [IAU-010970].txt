History
Founded 1964 as Bunda College of Agriculture (a former constituent college of the University of Malawi). Acquired present status and title 2011 from the merging of Bunda College of Agriculture (Bunda), Natural Resources College (NRC) and Agricultural Research and Extension Trust (ARET). Started operations 2012, NRC was fully integrated 2014
