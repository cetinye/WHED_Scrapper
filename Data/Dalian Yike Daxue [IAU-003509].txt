History
Founded 1947 as Guandong Medical College. Incorporated Dalian College of Engineering and Became Dalian University Medical College 1949. Dalian Medical College became indipendent 1950 and was relocated to Guizhou 1969. The College went back to its former place 1978. Acquired present status title 1994. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
