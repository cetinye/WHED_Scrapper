History
Founded 1847 as the Free Academy of the City of New York, a men's institution, renamed the College of the City of New York 1866, admitted women for the first time to graduate programmes only 1930, became entirely coeducational 1951, acquired present title 1961. Is part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system and its founding member
