History

October - March; April - September
Admission Requirements
Undergraduate programmes: 12th Grade Certificate or equivalent with a background in natural sciences.
MS programmes: Bachelor's degree in related health fields.
Master of Public Health in General Public Health: an MD in medicine or at least a Bachelor degree in Public Health/Public Health Officer and related fields (Nursing, Veterinary Medicine, Environmental Health, Medical Laboratory Technology, Biology or other health related fields).
Master of Public Health in Public Health Nutrition: Bachelor's degree or its equivalent in Food Science, Nutrition, Chemistry, Biology, Health Sciences or related agricultural fields from an institution of acceptable standing.
Master of Public Health in Reproductive Health: an MD in Medicine or at least a Bachelor degree in Public Health/Public Health Officer and related fields (Nursing, Veterinary medicine, Environmental health, Medical Laboratory Technology, Biology and other health related fields).
