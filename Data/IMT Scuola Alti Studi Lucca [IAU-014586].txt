History
IMT was founded as independent institution in November 2005 by way of a partnership between four leading Italian universities and the Fondazione Lucchese per l’Alta Formazione e la Ricerca (FLAFR), a private foundation in Lucca which aims to promote social and economic growth in Italy via higher education
