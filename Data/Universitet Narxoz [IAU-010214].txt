History
Created 1963 as T. Ryskulov Kazakh Economics University (Kazakhskij Ekonomičeskij Universitet imeni T. Ryskulova). Underwent various brand-name changes (Almaty Institute of People Economy, Kazakh State Academy of Management, New Economic University named after Turar Ryskulov, Narxoz University). Was officially renamed Narxoz University in 2016.
