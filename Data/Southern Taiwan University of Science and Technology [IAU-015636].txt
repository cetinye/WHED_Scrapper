History
Founded 1969 as Nan-Tai Junior College of Engineering. Successively renamed Nan-Tai Junior College of Engineering and Business (1990), Nan-Tai Institute of Technology (NTIT) (1996), Southern Taiwan University of Technology (STUT) 1999, Southern Taiwan University (STUT) (2007). Acquired present title 2012.
