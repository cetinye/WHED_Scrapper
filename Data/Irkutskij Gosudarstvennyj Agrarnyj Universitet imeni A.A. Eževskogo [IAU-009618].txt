History
Founded 1934 as Irkutsk Agricultural Institute (Irkutskij Sel'skokhozjajstvennyj Institut), was awarded Academy status in 1996 and in 2002 was renamed Itkutsk State Agricultutal Academy (Irkutskaja Gosudarstvennaja Sel'skokhozjajstvennaja Akademija). Acquired current title 2014
