History
Founded 1987 by Act of the Queensland Parliament. The first non-profit, privately funded and independent university to be established in Australia. Classes started May 1989.

Admission Requirements
Selection on the basis of a combination of academic merit, work experience and references, as well as other factors considered important indicators for success at Bond University. In some cases an interview may be required.
