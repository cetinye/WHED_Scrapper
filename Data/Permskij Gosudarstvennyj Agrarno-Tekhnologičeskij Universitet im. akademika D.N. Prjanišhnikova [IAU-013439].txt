History
Created 1930 as Ural'skij Sel'skokhozjajstvennyj Institut (Ural Agricultural Institute), became Permskij Sel'skokhozjajstvennyj Institut (Perm Agricultural Institute) in 1933, became Permskaja Gosudarstvennaja Sel'skokhozjajstvennaja Akademija im. Akademika D.N. Prjanišhnikova (Perm State Agricultural Academy named after D.N. Pryanishnikov) in 1995, and acquired current title and status 2017
