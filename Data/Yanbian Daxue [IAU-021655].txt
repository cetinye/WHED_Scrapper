History
Founded 1949. Separated into Yanbian University, Yanbian University, Yanbian Medical College, Yanbian Agricultural College, Yanbian Institute of Technology 1958. Merged with Yanbian Medical College, Yanbian Agricultural College, Yanbian Teachers College, Jilin College of Arts, the Yanbian Branch of and Sino-foreign cooperative education institution - the Yanbian University of Science and Technology 1996. Main administrative body: Jilin Province
