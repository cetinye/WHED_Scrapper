History
Founded 1953 as a Branch of the People Liberation Army Military Institute of Engineering. Named Artillery Engineering Institute, eventually renamed as East China Institute of Engineering. Acquired present title 1993. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Industry and Information Technology.
