History
Founded 1958 as Liaoning College of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Merged with Liaoning Research Institute of TCM, Liaoning Staff Medical School 1997. Merged with Liaoning Research School of Basic Medicine 2000. Acquired present title and status 2006. Since 2013 also known as Liaoning College of Medical Sciences. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
