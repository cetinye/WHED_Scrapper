History
Founded 1949 as National University, incorporating Okayama Medical School, affiliated Medical College, Sixth National Higher School, Okayama Normal School, Okayama Youth Normal School, and Okayama Prefectural College of Agriculture. Agricultural Biological Research Institute added in 1951, and Faculty of Engineering established 1960
