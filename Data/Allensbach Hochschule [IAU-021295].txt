History
Founded in 1996 under the name of AKAD University of Applied Sciences Lahr (WHL), this university, located in Lahr, was originally a subsidiary of the AKAD and group. In 2011, the university was transferred to Stuttgart. In 2015, the WHL University of Science Lahr was taken over by the European Education Group Deutschland GmbH and renamed Allensbach University (Allensbach University)
