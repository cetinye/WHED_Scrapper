History
Founded 1890 as the Biological Laboratory by the Brooklyn Institute of Arts and Sciences, acquired current title 1962 following merger the Carnegie Institute of Washington (established 1904 and later renamed the Department of Genetics). Gained accreditation from the Board of Regents of the University of the State of New York 1998, on behalf of the State Education Department as a Ph.D. degree-granting institution, which led to the
establishment of the School of Biological Sciences 1999. Its first doctorate was awarded in 2003
