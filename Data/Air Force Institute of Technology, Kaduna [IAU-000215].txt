History
Founded 1977 as NAF Technical and Supply School (TSS) in Kaduna. Renamed Technical Training Wing in 1979 under the Ground Training Group (GTG). Transformed into the 320 Technical Training Group (320 TTG) 2000. Upgraded to a National Diplomas awarding institution 2004. Acquired institute of Technology status and current title 2008. A federal university
