History
Founded 1915 as Hohai Civil Engineering Technical School and known by the famous educator and industrialist Zhang Jian. Became Hohai University of Engineering 1924. Became the East China Technical University of Water Resources 1952. Renamed Hohai University by Deng Xiaoping 1985. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
