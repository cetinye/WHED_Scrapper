History
Founded 1999, became operational 2001. Merged with Zanzibar Institute of Financial Administration (ZIFA), College of Health Sciences (CHS) and the Zanzibar Institute of Tourism Development (ZIToD) 2016. The only public University in Zanzibar. The total university annual budget is about $US 6,000. The Government of Zanzibar contributes a total of 40% of the budget, 45% comes from internal source of revenue such as fees, consultancy, while the remaining percentage is contributed by development partners in funding specific projects or programmes
