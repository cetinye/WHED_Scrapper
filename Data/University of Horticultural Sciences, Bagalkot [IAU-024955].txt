History
Created 2008 after the Governor of Karnataka promulgated an ordinance to establish the University of Horticultural Sciences (UHS) at Bagalkot through a special Ordinance No 2 of 2008 of 22/11/2008. The University is modelled on the pattern of the Land Grant Institutions of the USA abd aims to integrate education, research and extension in horticultural sciences
