History
Created in 2022 when it replaced the National School of Administration (ENA), created in 1945 and abolished by decree at the end of 2021. INSP is the French public operator of reference for recruitment, initial training and continuing education of senior and executive managers of the state and is a public administrative institution under the supervision of the Prime Minister.
