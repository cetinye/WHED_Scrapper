History
Founded 1922 as Electromechanical Institute. Became Mechanical Institute 1948 and Polytechnical Institute 1953. Acquired present status and title 1992. Merged with North University of Baia-Mare (Universitatea de Nord Baia-Mare) in 2012. A state institution
