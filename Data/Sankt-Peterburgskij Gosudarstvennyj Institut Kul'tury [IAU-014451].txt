History
Founded 1918 as an institute of out-of-school education and after several changes of name, became a university in 1999 under the name Sankt-Peterburgskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Kul'tury i Iskusstv (Saint-Petersburg State University of Culture and Arts), acquiring its current title and status in 2014
