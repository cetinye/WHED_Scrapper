History
Founded in 1968 as University of Sulaymaniyah, in 1981 moved to Erbil and the name is changed to Salahaddin University-Erbil. It is the oldest and biggest public comprehensive university in Kurdistan (Iraq).
Academic Year
Academic year consists of two semesters : emester one from September to February; semester two: from February to June.
Admission Requirements
Kurdistan National Exam (Year 12 Secondary school general exam) or equivalent.
