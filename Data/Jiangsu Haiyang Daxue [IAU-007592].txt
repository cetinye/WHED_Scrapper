History
Founded 1985 as part of Huaihai University. Merger with Jiangsu Salt Industry School 1959, became ormerly Huaihai Institute of Technology 1998. Incorporated Lianyungang Ocean School 2000 and Lianyugang Higher Vocational School of Chemical Engineering 2002. Acquired present title 2019. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
