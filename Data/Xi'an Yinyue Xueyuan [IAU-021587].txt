History
Created 1949 as Northwest Military and Politics University Music Department. Successively became Northwest Arts Institute 1950, Northwest Arts Vocational College 1953, Xi'an Music Vocational College 1956,Xi'an Conservatory of Music 1960, Shaanxi Arts College before the Cultural Revolution. Acquired present status and title 1980. Under the administration of Shaanxi Province
