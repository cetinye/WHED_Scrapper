History
Founded 1960 as Ecole nationale d’Administration du Sénégal (ENAS). Transformed into Ecole nationale d’Administration et de Magistrature (ENAM) 1975. Acquired current title 1995 and the status of a public administrative institution 2011. Directly under the authority of the Office of the Prime Minister ("Primature")
