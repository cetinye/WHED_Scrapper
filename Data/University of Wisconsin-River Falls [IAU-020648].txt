History
Founded 1874 as the fourth State Normal School in Wisconsin. Destroyed by fire 1897 and rebuilt 1898. Renamed Wisconsin State College at River Falls 1951 and Wisconsin State University-River Falls 1964. Acquired current title 1971 following the merger of the state universities and the University of Wisconsin. Is part of the University of Wisconsin System
