History
Created in 1996 as Caledonian College of Engineering, acquired current status and title in 2018 following merger with Oman Medical College (created 2001) and the Department of Pharmacy within Oman Medical College.
Academic Year
Sept - Jan; Feb - June
Admission Requirements
Bachelor in Engineering: General Education Diploma or equivalent with grade not less than C or equivalent in the Pure/Applied Mathematics, Physics/Chemistry or Industrial/Technical subjects; depending on the area of engineering chosen (Prior study of Chemistry is mandatory for those joining Chemical Engineering);
Doctor of Medicine: General Education Diploma or equivalent with not less than Grade B or equivalent in Chemistry and Biology individually;
Bachelor Program in Pharmacy: General Education Diploma or equivalent with not less than Grade C or equivalent in Chemistry and Biology individually
