History
Founded 1958 as Jinzhong Normal Training School. Closed down between 1962 and 1978. Merged with Jinzhong College of Education to form regional Jinzhong Teachers College 1998. Acquired present statusand title 2004, following the merger with Jinzhong Normal Junior College, Jinzhong Education College, Jinzhong Staff University. Main administrative body
