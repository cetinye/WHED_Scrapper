History
Founded 1970 under the 1968 law reforming higher education as one of three Universities replacing the former Université de Toulouse, founded in 1229. Known as University of Toulouse - Le Mirail and acquired current title 2014. Member of the COMUE Université fédérale de Toulouse Midi-Pyrénées – Décree n° 2015- 663 of 10th June 2015
