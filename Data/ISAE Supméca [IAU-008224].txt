History
Founded 1956. The ISMCM (Institut Supérieur des Matériaux et de la Construction Mécanique) was created 1948 jointly by the Federation of Mechanical Industries and by the Ministry of Education : it is an autonomous State Institution of Training and Research in Mechanical Engineering, placed under the Ministry of Education. Since 1956 in Paris and 1994 in Toulon, the Institute has launched the CESTI (Centre d'Etudes supérieures des Techniques Industrielles) Engineering curriculum. In 2004, ISMCM-CESTI changed it's name to Supméca
