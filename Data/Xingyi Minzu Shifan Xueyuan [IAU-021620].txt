History
Founded 1813 as Bishan Academy. Became Advanced Primary School, Xingyi County Middle School, Guizhou Xingyi Middle School, Guizhou Xingyi Normal School 1952, Xingyi Normal Vcational College 1978, Qianxinan Teachers Training for Nationalities 1982, Qianxinan Normal Junior College for Nationalities 1993. Merged with Xingyi Normal School, Anlong Normal School for Nationalities 2005. Acquired present status and title 2009. Under the administration of Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture
