History
Founded 1984. Inception of Master’s Degree courses, Opening of Center for Educational Research, In-service Center 1986. Inception of Doctoral courses, Opening of Affiliated elementary and middle school 1988. Opening of Affiliated High School 1994. Opening of Educational Graduate School 1997. Opening of Graduate School of Educational Policy and Administration 2001. International Symposium to found the first Asian Teachers Association 2006.
