History
Founded 1969, started operating 1972 as Lien Ho Industrial and Technological Junior College. Successively renamed Lien Ho Junior College of Technology (1973), Lien Ho College of Technology and Commerce (1992) National Lien Ho College of Technology and Commerce (1995), National Lien Ho Institute of Technology (1999). Acquired present status and title 2003.
