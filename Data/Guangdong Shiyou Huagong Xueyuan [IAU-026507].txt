History
Founded 1954 as Huanan Institute of Technology. Became Guangdong Petroleum School 1965. Renamed Guangdong Junior College of Petrochemical Technology 1992. Merged with Guangdong Maoming Education College (founded 1970) to form Maoming College, acquired university-level status 2000. Acquired present title 2010. Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
