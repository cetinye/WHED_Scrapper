History
Founded 1928 in the former provincial capital Anqing. After the Sino-Japanese war, the school was restored and renamed as the National Anhui Unversity. Moved to Hefei 1956. Acquired present title 1958. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Anhui Province
