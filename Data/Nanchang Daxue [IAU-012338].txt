History
Established 1940 as National Zhongzheng University. Jiangxi University and Jiangxi Industrial University merged, giving rise to Nanchang University 1993. NCU entered a new era of development when it merged with Jiangxi Medical College in August 2005. Part of "211 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Jiangxi Province
