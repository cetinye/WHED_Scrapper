History
Founded 1946 as The New York State Institute of Applied Arts and Sciences, renamed New York City Community College, becoming the city’s first community college 1951, became part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system 1964, designated “a technical institute within the CUNY system" and renamed New York City Technical College (City Tech) 1980, acquired current title 2002
