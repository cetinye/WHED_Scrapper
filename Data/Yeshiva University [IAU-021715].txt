History
Founded 1886. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine, was a division/the medical school of the Yeshiva University until 2019, when it became an autonomous educational institution. Three Schools are affiliated: Samuel H. Wang Yeshiva University High School for Girls, The Marsha Stern Talmudical Academy – Yeshiva University High School for Boys, Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary (RIETS)
