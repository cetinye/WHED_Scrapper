History
Founded 1998 as Ikram College, became Ikram College of Technology (iCT) in 2001 then changed name to Kuala Lumpur Infrastructure University College (KLIUC), in 2003. Became Infrastructure University Kuala Lumpur (IUKL) in 2012 and acquired present status and name (Kuala Lumpur University of Science and Technology) June 2025.
