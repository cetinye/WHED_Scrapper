History
Founded in 2003 as part of the National University of Singapore (NUS). Became the education and training arm (and subsidiary) of the Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS) - a statutory board of the Government of Singapore - in 2012. Was previously known as the IP Academy. A dedicated centre for IP education and training.
