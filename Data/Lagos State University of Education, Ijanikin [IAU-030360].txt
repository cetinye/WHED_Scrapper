History
Founded 1958 as Lagos Teacher Training College, Ebute-Elefun - a Grade II Teacher Training Institution. Allowed to award N.C.E. (Primary) certificates as of 1973. Affiliated to the then College of Education of the University of Lagos and then granted autonomy and renamed from Advanced Teachers’ College to Lagos State College of Education (LACOED) 1980. Renamed Adeniran Ogunsanya College of Education (AOCOED), Oto/Ijanikin, Lagos State 1996 when started serving as an affiliate College of the Ekiti State University, Ado Ekiti, by awarding first degrees in programmes in Education. Acquired current status and title 2021
