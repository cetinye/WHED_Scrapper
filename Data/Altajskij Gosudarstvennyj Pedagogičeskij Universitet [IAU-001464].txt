History
Created 1933 as Barnaul Teachers Institute (Barnaul'skij uchitel'skiy institut), became Barnaul State Pedagogical Institute (Barnaulskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Institut) in 1941, becamed Barnaul State Pedagogical University (Barnaulskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogičeskij Universitet) and acquired current title 2014
