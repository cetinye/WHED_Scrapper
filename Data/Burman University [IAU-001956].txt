History
Founded 1907 as Alberta Seventh-day Adventist secondary school. The Board of Trustees authorized the establishment of a junior college 1919. Became known as Canadian Union College and statred offering its first four-year programme in Theology 1947. Acquired present title 1997. Formerly Canadian University College (until 11 December 2015)
