History
Founded 1918 as Catarman Farm School supported by municipal and provincial funds of the Province of Samar. Became Catarman Agricultural School 1951, supported by national and provincial funds and then Catarman National Agricultural High School supported entirely by the national Government. Became Samar Institute of Technology 1957 and University 1964.
