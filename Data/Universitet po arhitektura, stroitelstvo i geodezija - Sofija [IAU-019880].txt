History
Founded 1942 as Higher Technical School, reorganized as State Polytechnic 1945 and as Higher Institute of Civil Engineering 1953. Became Higher Institute of Architecture and Civil Engineering (VIAS) 1977 and University of Architecture, Civil Engineering and Geodesy 1990. Acquired present status 1995
