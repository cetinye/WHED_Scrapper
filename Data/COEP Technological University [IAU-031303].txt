History
Founded 1854 as Poona Engineering Class and Mechanical School, renamed College of Engineering, Poona (COEP) 1911. Affiliated to the University of Bombay and then to the University of Pune as the College of Engineering, Pune (COEP). Acquired autonomy, university status and current title 2022
