History
Founded 1939 as Bijskij Uchitel'skij Institut (Bijsk Teachers Institute), in 1952 became Bijskij Gosudarstvennyj Pedagogicheskij Institut (Bijsk State Pedagogical Institute), in 2001 became Bijskij Pedagogicheskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet imeni V.M. Shukshina (Bijsk State Pedagogical University named after V.M. Shukshin), in 2010 became Altajskij Gosudarstvennaja Akademija imeni V.M. Shukshina (Altai State Academy of Education named after V.M. Shukshin) and acquired current title 2015
