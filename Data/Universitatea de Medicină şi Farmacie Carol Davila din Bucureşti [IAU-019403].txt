History
Founded 1857 as National School of Medicine and Surgery by Carol Davila, became School of Medicine and Pharmacy of the University of Bucharest in 1869. Separated to become Institute of Medicine and Pharmacy in 1948. Acquired present status and title in 1989. A State Institution
