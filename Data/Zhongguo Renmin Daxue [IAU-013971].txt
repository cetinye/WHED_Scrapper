History
Founded as Shan Bei Public School 1937. Shanbei Public School, Luxun Academy of Arts, Yan’an School of Workers, and Anwu Youth Training Centre were merged into one institution named North China United University. Merger of North China United University and Northern University into North China University 1948. North China University and the China University of Political Science and Law were merged into Renmin University 1949. Part of "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
