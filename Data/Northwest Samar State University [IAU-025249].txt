History
Created 1959 as Tiburcio Tancingo Memorial Vocational School, became Tiburcio Tancinco Memorial Institute of Science and Technology in 1982, and acquired current title and status in 2009 following merger with Samar State College of Agriculture and Forestry, located in San Jorge, Samar, founded in 1954 (IAU-014349).
