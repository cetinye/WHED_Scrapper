History
Founded as Faculty of the Technical University of Budapest in 1949. Became Veszprém University of Chemical Engineering in 1951. The Georgikon Faculty of Agriculture of the Pannon University of Agricultural Sciences attached to the University in 1989. Faculty of Teacher Training started in 1990. Obtained current title and status in 2006
