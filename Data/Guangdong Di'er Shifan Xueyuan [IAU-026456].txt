History
Established 1955 as Guangdong Education Aministration Institute. Renamed Guangdong Education Institute 1960. The same year incorporated Guangdong Technology and Agriculture School. Closed down 1970. Restored as Adults Normal School 1978-1985. Upgraded to Higher Education Institution and acquired present title 2010. Acquired present status 2014. Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
