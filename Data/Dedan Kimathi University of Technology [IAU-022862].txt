History
Created 1972 as Kimathi Institute of Technology (KIT), with first students admitted in 1978. Upgraded to University status in 2007, and renamed Kimathi University College of Technology (KUCT), a constituent college of Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. Acquired full university status and current name after being granted its charter in 2012.
