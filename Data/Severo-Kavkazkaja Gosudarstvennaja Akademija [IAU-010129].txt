History
Created 1991 as Karačaevo-Čerkesskij Tekhnologičeskij Institut (Karachaevo-Cherkessk Institute of Technology), became Karačaevo-Čerkesskij Gosudarstvennyj Tekhnologičeskij Institut (Karachaevo-Cherkessk State Institute of Technology) in 1998, Karačaevo-Čerkesskaja Gosudarstvennaja Tekhnologičeskaja Akademija (Karachaevo-Cherkessk State Academy of Technology) in 2003 and Severo-Kavkazkaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija (North Caucuses State Academy) in 2018
