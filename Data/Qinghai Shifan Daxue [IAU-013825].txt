History
Founded 1956 as Qinghai Normal Vocational College. Upgraded to university-level institution and renamed as Qinghai Normal College 1958. Acquired present title 1984. Merged with Qinghai Education College, acquired present status 1997. Incorporated Qinghai Normal Junior College for Nationalities, Qinghai Bank School 2000-2004. Under the administration of Qinghai Province
