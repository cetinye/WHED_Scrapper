History
Founded 1902 as Sanjiang Normal College, before evolving into Nanjing Higher Normal School (1915) National Southeast University (1921) and National Central University (1928). Transformed into Nanjing Institute of Technology 1952. Acquired present title 1988. Incorporated Nanjing Railway Medical College, Nanjing College of Communications and Nanjing Geological School 2000. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
