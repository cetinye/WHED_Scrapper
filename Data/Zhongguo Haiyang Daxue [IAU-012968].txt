History
Founded 1924 as Private Qingdao University. Successively became National Qingdao University, Shandong University. Renamed as Shandong College of Oceanology 1959. Renamed as Qingdao Ocean University 1988. Acquired present title 2002. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
