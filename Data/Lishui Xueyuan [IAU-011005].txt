History
The origins of the University trace back to the creation of the Chuzhou Primary-Level Normal School 1902. Foundation of the Lishui Junior Level Normal School 1978. Lishui Junior Level Normal School merged with Lishui Vocational Institute of Technology, was upgraded and became Lishui University 2004. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province
