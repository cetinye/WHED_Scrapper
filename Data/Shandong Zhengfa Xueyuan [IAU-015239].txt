History
Created 1955 as Shandong Political Science and Law Cadre School. Closed from 1961 to 1979, reopened by the Shandong Provincial Committee and Government, 1983 became Shandong Justice Administration Cadre College, 1987 changed name to Shandong Administratives Cadres Institute of Political Science and Law. Acquired present title and status 2007. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
