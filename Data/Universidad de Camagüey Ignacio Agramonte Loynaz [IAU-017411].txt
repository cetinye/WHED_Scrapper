History
Founded 1967 as Centro Universitario de Camagüey, incorporating institutions previously forming part of the Universidad de Las Villas. Acquired present title 1974. Merged with the University of Pedagogical Sciences "José Martí" and the University of Physical Culture and Sports Sciences "Comandante Manuel Fajardo", from the province of Camagüey, approved by the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba in June 2014
