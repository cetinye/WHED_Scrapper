History
Founded 1960 as a Librarian Training Institute. Reorganized into Institute of Culture 1964, acquired present title 1995. Was also known as East Siberian State Academy of Culture and Arts (Vostočno-Sibirskaja Gosudarstvennaja Akademija Kul'tury i Iskusstv)
