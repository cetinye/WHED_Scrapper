History
Founded in 1432 by the King of England Henry VI, the University of Caen was destroyed in 1944 during the Battle of Normandy. The reconstruction of the University started in 1948 under the authority of Rector Pierre Daure and the new campus was inaugurated in 1957.
Became member of the COMUE Normandie Université (Communities of universities and higher education institutions) - Décret n° 2014-1673 - 29 décembre 2014
