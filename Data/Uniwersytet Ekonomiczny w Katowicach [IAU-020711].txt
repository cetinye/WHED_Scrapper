History
Founded 1936 as a private college (College of Social and Economic Sciences). Reorganized 1950 as Higher School of Economics, named after Karol Adamiecki 1972, and acquired present status and named Akademia Ekonomiczna im. Karola Adamieckiego w Katowicach (Karol Adamiecki University of Economics in Katowice) 1974. Acquired present title 2010.
