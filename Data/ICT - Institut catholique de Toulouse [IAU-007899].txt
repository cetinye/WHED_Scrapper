Note
Under French law Catholic institutions are not entitled to award official degrees and diplomas. Students prepare for State degrees and diplomas at the same time. However, the Faculties of Theology, Canon Law, and Philosophy award a Baccalauréat canonique, Licence canonique and Doctorat canonique and the various schools award their own diplomas
