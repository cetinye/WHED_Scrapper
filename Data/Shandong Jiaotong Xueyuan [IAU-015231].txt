History
Founded 1958 as Ministry of Communications Jinan Automotive Machinery School. Became successively Shandong Communications Junior College 1958, Jinan Communications College 1961, Jinan Weidong Machinery Factory 1970, Shandong Communication School 1973, Ministry of Communications Jinan Communication School 1980, Jinan Communication Vocational College 1988. Acquired present title and status 2002. Main administrative body: Shandong Province
