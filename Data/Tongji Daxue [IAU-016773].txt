History
Founded 1907 as Tongji German Medical School. Renamed Tongji Medical and Engineering College 1912. Successively became Tongji Medical and Engineering School and Private Tongji Medical and Engineering Technical School 1917. Acquired present name 1923. Incorporated Shanghai Urban Construction College, Shanghai Building Materials Industry College 1996, Shanghai Railway University 2000 and acquired status 2000. Also incorporated Shanghai Aviation Industry School 2003. Part of "211 Project" and "985 Project" universities. Main administrative body: Ministry of Education
