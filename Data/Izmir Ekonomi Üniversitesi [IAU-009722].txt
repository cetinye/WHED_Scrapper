History
Izmir University of Economics, which is the first foundation university in Izmir and in Aegean region, was established in 2001 by “Izmir Chamber of Commerce Education and Health Foundation. IUE, which is accredited by Higher Education council of Turkey (YÖK) offers degree programs in associate, undergraduate, master’s , and doctorate levels
Academic Year
One academic year consists of two semesters( Fall semester: September to January, Spring semester: February to June) each of which has 16 weeks of education including the examination period.
