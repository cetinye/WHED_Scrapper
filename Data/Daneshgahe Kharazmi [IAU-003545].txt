History
Founded 1919 as the Central Teachers’ Institute, university acquired 1974 and renamed Tarbiat Moallem University of Tehran. Acquired current title 2012. Absorbed University of Economic Science 2015, which became its faculty of management, faculty of financial sciences and faculty of economics. Regarded as the oldest higher education institution in Iran
