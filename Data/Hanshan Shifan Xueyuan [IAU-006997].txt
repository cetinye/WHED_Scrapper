History
Founded 1903 as Huichaojia Teachers School. Successively became Guangdong Provincial 2nd Normal School 1921, Guangdong provincial Hanshan Normal School 1935, Hanshan Normal School 1949. Upgraded to Junior College 1958. Acquired present status and name 1993. Main administrative body: Guangdong Province
