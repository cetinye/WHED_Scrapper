History
Founded 1912 as Shanghai Drawing and Painting Fine Arts Institute by Liu Haisu. Renamed as Shanghai Fine Arts Vocational School 1930. Merged with Suzhou Fine Arts Vocational School, Shandong University's Departements of Fine Arts and Music into East China Vocational School of Arts 1952. Moved to Nanjin gand renamed as Nanjing Vocational School of Arts 1958. Acquired present title 1959. Main administrative body: Jiangsu Province.
