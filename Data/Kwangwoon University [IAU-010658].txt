History
Founded 1934 as Institute of Technology, acquired present status and title 1988. A private Institution operated by the Kwangwoon Foundation but supervised by the Ministry of Education.

Admission Requirements
Graduation from high school (vocational or training schools are not acceptable), language proficiency over TOPIK level 4 or equivalent and entrance examination (both written and spoken)
