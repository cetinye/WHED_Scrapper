History
Founded 1962 as Université fédérale du Cameroun, replacing the Institut national d'Etudes supérieures, founded 1961. Renamed Université de Yaoundé 1973. Acquired present status and title 1993 following university reform leading to the splitting into two distinct institutions: Université de Yaoundé I and Université de Yaoundé II
