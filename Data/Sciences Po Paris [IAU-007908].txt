History
Founded 1871 as Ecole libre des Sciences politiques; became institute attached to the Université de Paris 1945, independent autonomous institution 1969 and 'Grand Etablissement' 1984. Under the supervision of and financially supported by the Fondation nationale des Sciences politiques. 7 University College campuses in Le Havre, Paris, Reims, Dijon, Nancy, Poitiers Menton
Admission Requirements
Entrance by competition following 1 undergraduate programmes study after secondary school certificate (baccalauréat) or two university years (foreigners). Direct entrance to graduate programme (master degree) by competition following appropriate university degree
