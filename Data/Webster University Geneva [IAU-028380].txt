History
Created in Switzerland in 1978 as an accredited American university campus that offers high-quality programs in English at the undergraduate (bachelor level) and graduate (masters) level. The university provides a broad liberal arts approach to teaching in the culturally-rich context of Switzerland,
Admission Requirements
For undergraduate degrees, a recognized secondary school certificate; minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4 point scale, or its equivalent in other grading systems.
For postgraduate programmes, an undergraduate degree from a recognized higher education institution; minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4 point scale, or its equivalent in other grading systems; proficiency in English language.
Admission to the MA in Counseling requires a recommendation for admission by the program director, following an admissions interview, and enrollment may be contingent upon meeting/completing specific prerequisite courses.
Admission to the MBA may include prerequisite course(s), dependent on prior degree studies and/or the intended start date.
