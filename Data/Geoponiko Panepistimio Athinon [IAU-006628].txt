History
Founded 1920 as an Independent Higher Education Institute with university status under the name of the Highest Agricultural School of Athens (H.A.S.A.). Acquired present status and english title 1989. Acquired present greek title 1995 as it was known as Georgikon Panepistimion Athinon. Under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Education.
