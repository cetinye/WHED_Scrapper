History
Founded as Waterford Regional Technical College in 1970. Became Institiúid Teicneolaíochta Phort Láirge (Waterford Institute of Technology) in 1998 and acquired current title and status in May 2022 following a merger with Institiúid Teicneolaíochta Ceatharlach (Institute of Technology Carlow).
