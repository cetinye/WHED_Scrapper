History
Founded 1978 as Xianyang Training Section of Shaanxi Normal University. Renamed Xianyang Teachers Postsecondary Training School 1978. Merged with Shaanxi Xianyang Education College, acquired present status and title 2001. Incorporated Shaanxi Radio and Television University Xianyang branch 2004 and Shaanxi Electrical and Mechanical Engineering School 2016. Main administrative body: Shaanxi Province
