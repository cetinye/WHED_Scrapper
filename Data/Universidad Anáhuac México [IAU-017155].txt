History
Founded 1964 as Anáhuac University of North Mexico (Universidad Anáhuac México Norte). Merged with Anáhuac University South Mexico (Universidad Anáhuac México Sur (IAU-017156)) in 2016 when it acquired current title. Part of the Anáhuac University Network which is a private universities system grouped and administered by the religious congregation of the Legionaries of Christ. This network is also affiliated with the Education Consortium Anáhuac (CEA)
