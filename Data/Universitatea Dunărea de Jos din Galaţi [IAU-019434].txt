History
Founded as Land Improvement Institute in 1948. Reorganized as the Institute of Fish Breeding and Fishing in 1953. Became Polytechnical Institute of Galaţi 1957. Acquired present status in 1974 with merger of Polytechnical Institute and the College of Education. Earned present title in 1989. A state institution
