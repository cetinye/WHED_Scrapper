History
Founded 1834 as St. Volodymyr Imperial University. Renamed after Taras Shevchenko in 1939. On 21 April 1994, Taras Shevchenko University of Kyiv was granted the status "National" by Leonid Kravchuk, the President of Ukraine by Decree № 176/94
