History
Founded 1951 incorporating Faculty of Economics, established 1942, Faculty of Philosophy, 1943, and School of Engineering, 1912. Reorganized 1973 with a structure comprising centres for professional education. A private institution under the supervision of the Society of Jesus and recognized by the Federal Government
