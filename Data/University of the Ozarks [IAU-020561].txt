History
Founded 1834 as Cane Hill School, the all-male student institution closed 1861 due to civil war conscription, became co-ed 1875, closed 1891 and replaced by the Arkansas Cumberland College, The College of the Ozarks 1921, the first traditionally white college in Arkansas to admit African American students 1957, acquired current status and title 1987
