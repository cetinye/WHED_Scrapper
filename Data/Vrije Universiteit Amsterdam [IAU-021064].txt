History

Admission Requirements
For Bachelor’s: Secondary school certificate in subjects appropriate to faculty requirements (diploma eindexamen Gymnasium A or B, Atheneum A or B) or equivalent. For Master’s programmes: Academic Bachelor’s diploma closely related tot the Master’s programme. Additional language requirements.
