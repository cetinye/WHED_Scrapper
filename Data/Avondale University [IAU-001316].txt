History
Founded 1897 by the Seventh-Day Adventist Church as Avondale School for Christian Workers, became Australasian Missionary College in 1911, Avondale College in 1964, Avondale College of Higher Education in 2011 and Avondale University College in 2019. Awarded self-accrediting status in 2014, and acquired current title and status in 2021.
