History
The Academy of Economic Studies of Moldova (ASEM) was founded on September 25, 1991, based on Decision no. 537 of the Government of the Republic of Moldova. Currently, it is the main public higher educational institution with economic profile in Moldova comprising a university complex, within which 6 faculties with 26 departments, 2 departments, 7 centers, 13 services, the Master's School of Excellence in Economics and Business, the Doctoral School, National College of Commerce and National Institute of Economic Research.
