History
Founded 1986 as Ukrainian branch of Moscow Interdisciplinary Institute of Advanced Training of Professional Accounting and Statistics of the State Statistics Committee of USSR. Became an independent higher educational called institution of Ukraine Institute of Training Specialists in Statistics and Market 1992. Renamed Institute of Statistics, Accounting and Audit of the State Statistics Committee of Ukraine and then State Academy of Statistics, Accounting and Audit of the State Statistics Committee of Ukraine 2003. Acquired current status and title 2010
