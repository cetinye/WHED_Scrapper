History
Founded 1934 as Hunan Chinese Medicine Vocational College. The Hunan Chinese Medicine Training School was founded 1953. Upgraded to a college, became Hunan College of Chinese Medicine and acquired present status 1960. Incorporated Hunan University of Science and Technology 1990. Merged with Hunan Academy of Chinese Medicine 2002. Acquired present title 2006. Main administrative body: Hunan Province
