History
Createdin 2012 by Decree of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan and started to function in 2014, with the first students graduating in 2017/2018. ASCCA (or Azerbaijan Customs Academy, ACA) is an education, training and research institution under the State Customs Committee (SCC) of the Republic of Azerbaijan. As a special purpose, public higher education institution accredited in the country, the Academy carries out higher education programs (BA and MA levels in economics, customs studies, legal studies and international law and information security), as well as the customs officers advanced training and continuous/lifelong learning programs, and aims to conduct scientific, fundamental and applied research primarily related to the Academy's activity areas as well as on the national, regional and international customs affairs.
In line with its Statute and the indicated Decree, the Academy is authorized to conduct its international cooperation independently. International cooperation is one of the most important priorities for the ASCCA, which establishes mutually beneficial relations with the most relevant higher education institutions and particularly, with the (inter-) national customs (and customs-related) training institutes, across the continents
