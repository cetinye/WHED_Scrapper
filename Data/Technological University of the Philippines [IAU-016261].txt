History
Founded 1901 as School, became College of Arts and Trades 1959. Reorganized as University 1978 including the Manila Technician Institute, Taguig, Bocolod Technician Institute, Talisay, and Iligan Institute of Technology, and 10 Regional Manpower Training Centres. Financed partly by the State and partly from tuition fees.
