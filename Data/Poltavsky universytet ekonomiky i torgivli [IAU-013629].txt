History
Founded 1961 as a teaching and consultation centre. Became a department 1968 and then a branch of Lviv Trade and Economics Institute 1970. The Poltava Cooperative Institute was established in 1974 and its Belgorod (Russia) branch started its activties 1978 and was transformed into Belgorod Cooperative Institute of Central Committee of Consumer Cooperatives of the USSR 1981. The Board of the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives in Ukraine adopted the Resolution "On creation of Poltava University of Consumer Cooperatives in Ukraine" May 21, 2001. Acquired current title 2010
