History
Established in cooperation with the State University of New York, New Paltz, and the State University of New York Empire State College in 1998. Acquired national accreditation status in 2001. Established partnership with La Salle University Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 2002. Earned membership with the international Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) International in 2005.
