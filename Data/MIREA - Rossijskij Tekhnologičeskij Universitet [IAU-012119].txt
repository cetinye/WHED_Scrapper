History
Founded 1947. Previously known as Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Institut Radiotekhniki Elektroniki i Avtomatiki (Tekhničeskij Universitet) (Moscow State Institute of Radio Engineering, Electronics and Automation (Technical University). Became Moskovskij Tekhnologičeskij Universitet (Moscow Technological University) in 2015 when merged with Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Priborostroenija i Informatiki (Moscow State University of Instrument Engineering and Computer Science) and then merged with Moskovskij Gosudarstvennyj Universitet Tonkikh Khimičeskikh Tekhnologi im. M.V. Lomonosova (Moscow State Academy of Fine Chemical Technology named after M.V. Lomonosov) in 2016 and acquired current title 2018
