History
Founded 1949 as Fuxin Coalmine Institute. Incorporated Fushun Mining Institute, Liaoning Coalmine Teachers Institute and Jixi Mining Institute 1958. Became Fuxin Mining Institute 1978. Acquired present title 1996. Incorporated Fuxin Technical School, Liaoning Computing School and Liaoning Finance School 1999-2000. Main administrative body: Liaoning Province
