History
Founded 1947 as School of Law. Faculty of Arts and Science opened 1948, Faculty of Education, Elementary School, and High School added 1949. Graduate School and Faculty of Commerce established 1952, Faculty of Engineering and Architecture 1954. Granted University status 1958. Operating under the supervision of the Ministry of Education, Culture and Sports.
