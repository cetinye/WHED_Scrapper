History
Founded 1996 as Namangan Industrial Technological Institute, acquired present status and title 1998. In accordance with the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan №PP-2909 from 20.04.2017, Namangan Engineering-Pedagogical Institute was transformed into Namangan Engineering-Construction Institute.
